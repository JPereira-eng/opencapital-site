=== CONTEXTO ===
O topico HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-11 visa reforcar o acesso autonomo da UE ao espaco atraves de cosmodromo europeus (spaceports). As propostas devem explorar sinergias com acoes anteriores (HORIZON-CL4-2023-SPACE-01-23 e HORIZON-CL4-2025-02-SPACE-11).

A call faz parte da estrategia europeia de soberania espacial, no quadro do Horizonte Europa Cluster 4 — Destination 5: Unleashing the full potential of new components and systems for digital and industrial leadership.
