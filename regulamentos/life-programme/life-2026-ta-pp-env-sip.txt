Programme: Programme for the Environment and Climate Action (LIFE) (2021/2027)
Sub-programme: Circular Economy and Quality of Life
Status: Open

Budget:
- ENV SIP track: EUR 300,000
- Total SIP/SNAP 2026 envelope: EUR 1,000,000 distributed as:
  - NAT SNAP: EUR 500,000
  - ENV SIP: EUR 300,000
  - CLIMA SIP: EUR 200,000

Description:
This technical assistance call supports preparation of Strategic Integrated Project (SIP) proposals under the Circular Economy and Quality of Life sub-programme. Funding targets projects addressing Circular Economy, Waste, Water, and Air quality issues.

Specifically, it finances development of proposals implementing:
- National or regional Circular Economy Action Plans
- National and regional Waste Management Plans
- River Basin Management Plans
- Air Quality Plans
- Noise Action Plans

Projects must operate at large territorial scales with multi-stakeholder engagement and coordination mechanisms for complementary funding from other EU, national, and regional sources.
