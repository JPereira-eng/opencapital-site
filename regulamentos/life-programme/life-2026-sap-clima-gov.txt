Status: Open

Key Dates:
- Publication/Opening Date: April 21, 2026
- Submission Deadline: May 19, 2026 (from F&T Portal)
- CINEA page reference deadline: September 22, 2026 (broader LIFE 2026 window)
- Submission Model: Single-stage procedure

Budget:
- Climate Governance and Information (GOV): EUR 4,000,000
- Total LIFE 2026 SAP CLIMA envelope: EUR 60 million across three related topics:
  - Climate Governance and Information (GOV): EUR 4,000,000
  - Climate Change Mitigation (CCM): EUR 28,000,000
  - Climate Change Adaptation (CCA): EUR 28,000,000
- Grant Type: LIFE Action Grant Budget-Based

Objective:
The initiative aims at supporting the development, implementation, monitoring and enforcement of the Union legislation and policy on climate change, while enhancing governance capacity among public and private actors and civil society.

Scope - Areas of Intervention:
- Awareness-raising and behavioural change linked to the European Climate Pact
- Green skills and capacity building for climate policies
- Fluorinated greenhouse gas monitoring
- Development of climate and energy strategies
- Sustainable finance implementation support
- Greenhouse gas monitoring and reporting
- LULUCF sector inventories and carbon registries
- EU Emissions Trading System training
- Climate policy assessment and evaluation
