LIFE 2026 Technical Assistance - Preparation of SNAPs

Call Name: Technical Assistance for the preparation of SNAPs (Strategic Nature Projects)
Call Reference: LIFE-2026-TA-PP-NAT-SNAP
Programme: Programme for the Environment and Climate Action (LIFE) (2021/2027)
Status: Open
Funding Body: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)

Key Dates:
- Opening Date: April 8, 2026
- Submission Deadline: May 13, 2026
- Submission Model: Single-stage

Budget:
- Total for SNaP track: EUR 500,000 (2026)
- Single allocation for this topic
- Budget-based LIFE Action Grant model applies

Description:
This Technical Assistance call supports preparation of Strategic Nature Project (SNaP) proposals for implementing Priority Action Frameworks, National Restoration Plans, or equivalent strategies at territorial scale with stakeholder involvement and coordination mechanisms for complementary funding.

SNaPs (Strategic Nature Projects) are large-scale, multi-stakeholder nature projects aimed at implementing national and regional biodiversity strategies, habitat and species conservation plans, and ecosystem restoration at a broad territorial level.

Detailed requirements specified in the call document sections 6-7, covering:

- Legal entity validation requirements
- Exclusion criteria compliance

Selection Criteria:
Award criteria and scoring methodology are outlined in call document section 9. Evaluation follows procedures detailed in section 8 and the Online Manual, including assessment of proposal quality and strategic alignment.
