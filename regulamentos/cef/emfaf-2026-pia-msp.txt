Call for Proposals: Maritime Spatial Planning in Atlantic and Black Sea

Name: Call for proposals to support Maritime Spatial Planning in the Atlantic and Black Sea
Code: EMFAF-2026-PIA-MSP
Programme: European Maritime, Fisheries and Aquaculture Fund (EMFAF) (2021/2027)
Managing Agency: European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency (CINEA)

Funding Amount: Up to 1.7 million EUR in co-funding
Expected Awards: One or two projects are expected to be funded

Publication Date: 15 January 2026
Application Deadline: 15 April 2026, 17:00 (CEST)
Submission Model: Single-stage
Status: Open

Eligibility:
The call targets participants from different Member States located in the same sea basin, or from different sea basins. Projects should address specific challenges that EU Member States in the Atlantic and Black Sea might encounter when putting into effect, monitoring and revising their Maritime Spatial Plans.

Objectives:
Strategic coordination for marine protection and human activities through cross-border cooperation and data-sharing initiatives. Projects must address sustainable blue economy, marine protected areas, and maritime planning coordination across EU Member States.

Timeline:
- Application Deadline: 15 April 2026, 17:00 CEST
- Evaluation: April-May 2026
- Results notification: June 2026
- Grant agreement signature: September-December 2026

Submission Method: Electronic submission via the Funding and Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System
