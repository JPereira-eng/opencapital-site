Innovation Fund 2025 Net-Zero Technologies Call

Name: Innovation Fund 2025 Net-Zero Technologies Call
Code: INNOVFUND-2025-NZT
Programme: Innovation Fund (2021-2027 framework period)
Managing Agency: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)

Total Allocation: 2.9 billion EUR
Status: Open

Publication Date: 4 December 2025
Opening Date: 4 December 2025
Deadline: 23 April 2026, 17:00 CEST
Deadline Model: Single-stage

Eligible Topics / Sectors:
- Energy storage (ES)
- Energy-intensive industry (EII)
- Carbon capture, utilisation and storage (CCUS)
- Mobility (maritime, road transport, aviation)
- Renewable energy sources (RES)

Key Resources and Support:
- Online info day: 16 December 2025 with recordings and presentations
- Multiple tutorial videos covering application procedures, financial requirements, and greenhouse gas (GHG) methodology
- Self-check questionnaire to assess project fit
- Q&A documentation
- Innovation Fund Helpdesk support

Application Portal: All documentation and submission available through the Funding and Tenders Portal under call identifier INNOVFUND-2025-NZT.

Selection Criteria: Projects must demonstrate significant greenhouse gas emissions avoidance and commercial viability of innovative low-carbon technologies. Proposals evaluated on innovation degree, scalability, maturity, and project team competence.

Source: https://cinea.ec.europa.eu/funding-opportunities/calls-proposals/innovation-fund-2025-net-zero-technologies-call_en
