CALL IDENTIFIER: ERASMUS-EDU-2025-PCOOP-ENGO
TITLE: Partnerships for Cooperation - Cooperation Partnerships in the field of education and training submitted by European NGOs (2025)
FRAMEWORK: Erasmus+ (ERASMUS2027)
SOURCE: erasmus-ka2-cooperation

DESCRIPTION

The call supports organisations in enhancing activity quality and developing transnational partner networks. It aims to support the development, transfer and/or implementation of innovative practices and facilitate peer learning exchanges across Europe. The applicant coordinator must be a European NGO active in education and training. Results should demonstrate reusability, transferability, and scalability with transdisciplinary dimensions where possible.

BUDGET

5,000,000 EUR available for 2025 under ERASMUS Lump Sum grants

DEADLINES

- Publication: 28 November 2024
- Application Deadline: 5 March 2025, 17:00 (Brussels time)
- Evaluation Period: March - July 2025
- Applicant Notification: September 2025
- Grant Agreement Signing: September - December 2025

ELIGIBILITY

Applicant coordinator must be a European NGO active in education/training. Eligible countries and other conditions are detailed in the ERASMUS+ Programme Guide 2025.

CONDITIONS

- Part B proposal limit: 70 pages
- Single-stage submission process
- Evaluation based on scoring criteria outlined in Programme Guide
