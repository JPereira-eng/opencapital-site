PROGRAMME DIVISION: Partnership for Cooperation in Education and Training - European NGOs
SOURCE: erasmus-ka2-cooperation

DESCRIPTION

The call supports organisations in enhancing activity quality and relevance while developing transnational partner networks. It promotes development, transfer and/or implementation of innovative practices and encourages peer learning and experience exchange at European level. Projects must produce reusable, transferable, and scalable results with cross-sectoral potential. Results should be shared locally, regionally, nationally, and transnationally.

The European Education and Culture Executive Agency (EACEA) manages these partnerships. The applicant coordinator must be a European NGO active in education and training, applying through their European secretariat.
