CALL IDENTIFIER: ERASMUS-YOUTH-2025-PCOOP-ENGO
TITLE: Cooperation Partnerships in the field of Youth (2025)
FRAMEWORK: Erasmus+ (ERASMUS2027)
SOURCE: erasmus-ka2-cooperation

DESCRIPTION

The scope encompasses increasing quality in the work, activities and practices of participating organisations while building capacity of organisations to work transnationally and across sectors. Initiatives must address common priorities in education, training, youth and sport, and enable transformation and change (at individual, organisational or sectoral level) proportional to each organisation's context.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Only European NGOs meeting ENGO definitions per the Erasmus+ Programme Guide 2025 may apply. Eligible countries are specified in the Programme Guide. Proposals must not exceed 70 pages (Part B of Application Form).

KEY KEYWORDS

Civil society, democratic engagement, digital competences, youth empowerment, youth participation, inclusion, social inclusion of refugees, grassroots NGOs, green deal, environmental initiatives, and European values promotion.
