HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-41
Enhancing industry-academia knowledge exchange in Social Sciences and Humanities (SSH) (CSA)

Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2026-04-21

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 2.000.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 2
- Contribution per project: 1.000.000 EUR

Description:
Esta acao visa aproveitar os pontos fortes das ciencias sociais, humanidades e artes para atender necessidades especificas de empresas, fomentando co-criacao dinamica entre industria e academia. Implementara metodologias de troca de conhecimento SSH-industria, como hackathons e programas de intercambio, focando desafios especificos da industria.

Key Activities:
- Development of methodology to understand how industry needs (PMEs and large companies) can be addressed by SSH researchers
- International team-based services to businesses with SSH expertise
- Feasibility study on SSH knowledge exchange for innovation management and organizational development
- Hackathons and knowledge exchange programs connecting SSH academia with industry

Expected Outcomes:
- Greater industrial innovation capacity leveraging Social Sciences and Humanities
- Improved strategies for bringing products to market using SSH insights
- Better understanding of industrial needs by SSH researchers
- New methodologies for SSH-industry co-creation

Conditions:
- Admissibility per Annexes A-G of Work Programme General Annexes
- Lump sum grant model per Decision of July 7, 2021
- Standard CSA evaluation forms
