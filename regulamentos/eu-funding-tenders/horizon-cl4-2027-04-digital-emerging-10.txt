Horizon Europe - Horizon scanning and foresight in future enabling digital technologies (CSA)

Title: Horizon scanning and foresight in future enabling digital technologies (CSA)
Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2027-04-DIGITAL-EMERGING-10
Parent Call: HORIZON-CL4-2027-04 (DIGITAL cluster)

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON) | Digital, Industry and Space division

Budget and Funding:
- Total Budget: EUR 4,000,000 (2027 budget year)
- Expected Grants: 1
- Contribution Range: EUR 4,000,000 (minimum and maximum - lump sum)
- Funding Type: Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
- Action Type: HORIZON Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)

Deadline:
- Planned Opening: April 18, 2027
- Submission Deadline: October 10, 2027
- Procedure: Single-stage

Objective and Description:
The initiative seeks to establish European leadership in foresight on emerging technologies. It aims to foster collaboration between academia, industry, and stakeholders through interdisciplinary approaches.

Proposals should:
- Establish forums for emerging areas in digital enabling technologies
- Support diverse participants in developing innovative concepts for future enabling digital technologies
- Cover the range from fundamental research to proof of concept
- Encourage civil society engagement
- Establish connections with European Commission initiatives

Eligibility:
- Eligible countries: described in Work Programme General Annexes, Annex B
- Subject to European communication network protections
- Financial and operational capacity requirements apply (Annex C)
- Not specifically SME-focused; large-consortium CSA format expected
