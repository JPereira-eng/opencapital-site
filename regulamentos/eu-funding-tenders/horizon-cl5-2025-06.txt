HORIZON-CL5-2025-06 - Cluster 5 Climate Energy Mobility Call 6 (2025)
======================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL5-2025-06
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe
CLUSTER: Cluster 5 - Climate, Energy and Mobility
MANAGING AGENCY: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)
STATUS: Closed (Deadline: 23 September 2025)

FINANCIAL DETAILS
-----------------
Total budget: EUR 139.5 million
Number of topics: 7
Total portfolio Cluster 5 (2025): EUR 636.6 million across all calls

ELIGIBLE PROJECT TYPES
-----------------------
- Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
- Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)
- Programme Co-funded Actions

THEMATIC FOCUS
--------------
This call focuses on cross-sectoral solutions for the climate transition and climate sciences for transformation towards climate neutrality, under the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2025 Cluster 5.

KNOWN TOPICS
------------
HORIZON-CL5-2025-06-D1-07: Implementing the climate action pillar of the EU-African Union Partnership on Climate Change and Sustainable Energy (Co-funded Partnership).

HORIZON-CL5-2025-06-D2-07: Driving Urban Transitions to a sustainable future (Co-funded Partnership) - addressing urban sustainability transitions across sectors including energy, mobility, buildings, and land use.

RESEARCH AREAS COVERED
-----------------------
- Cross-sectoral climate transition solutions
- Climate sciences and responses for transformation towards climate neutrality
- EU-AU Partnership on Climate Change and Sustainable Energy
- Urban transitions to climate neutrality
- Systems approaches for decarbonisation
- Behavioural and social dimensions of climate action
- Low-carbon innovation in energy-intensive sectors
- Clean transport solutions across all modes (aviation, maritime, road, rail)
