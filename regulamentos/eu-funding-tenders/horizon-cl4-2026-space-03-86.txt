HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-86
Space critical Equipment for EU non-dependence – Space Refuelling Interface

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 2,940,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 1
- Min Contribution: 2,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 3,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: July 6, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Strengthen EU strategic autonomy by reducing critical space equipment dependencies. Expected outcomes include: unrestricted access to critical space technologies; development of independent operational capability in space; competitiveness through products of equivalent or superior performance compared to global competitors.

SCOPE:
Addresses the lack of high-performance solutions in the EU. Funds development of critical equipment to reduce extra-EU dependency, establishing sustainable and reliable supply chains. The chain must preferably be EU-based; where partial, external suppliers must guarantee they are not subject to export control restrictions.

Critical requirements include: detailed supply chain analysis; description of technological development processes avoiding export restrictions; vulnerability identification; applicable standards (EN, ECSS, ESCC, MIL, JEDEC); space qualification data as deliverables; specific tasks with confidential reports within six months.

TRL TARGET: TRL 5 at project end (ISO 16290:2013).

Beneficiaries must guarantee availability of developed hardware, manufacturing and assembly processes for up to 4 years post-project at fair and reasonable prices without legal restrictions. Ownership transfers or exclusive licenses require prior notification to the granting authority.

Gender dimension integration is not relevant to this topic.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Entities established in EU Member States, Norway and Iceland
- Entities controlled by non-eligible countries need positive guarantees
- Mandatory use of Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS data for satellite-based Earth observation, positioning or navigation
- Mandatory ownership and control declaration forms

CONDITIONS:
- Page limit: 80 pages
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Partial evaluation by representatives of EU institutions
