Horizon Europe - Innovation for responsible EU sourcing of primary raw materials (RIA)

Title: Innovation for responsible EU sourcing of primary raw materials, the foundation of the Green Deal (RIA)
Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01-06
Parent Call: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Action Type: RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)

Budget:
- Total Budget: EUR 30,000,000
- Expected Grants: 4 projects

Deadline:
- Submission Deadline: September 23, 2021
- Status: CLOSED

Description:
Projects should develop knowledge and conceptual models using innovative technologies to strengthen EU supply of primary raw materials. Activities include:
- Generating better geological understanding of mineral deposits
- Developing new genetic models for ore deposit types hosting critical minerals
- Deploying innovative geological and data analysis approaches
- Mapping EU and third-country raw materials potential using UNFC and UNRMS standards
- Improving public awareness about raw materials importance for climate-neutral and digitized economies

TRL Target: TRL 3-5 by project end

Keywords: Geology, Mechanical engineering, Mining and mineral processing

Eligibility:
- Legal entities established in EU Member States
- Associated countries
- OECD countries
- African Union countries
- MERCOSUR, CARIFORUM, and Andean Community member states
- Proposals including entities from other countries are ineligible

Programme Context:
Part of Cluster 4 Resilience call focused on securing EU access to critical raw materials essential for the Green Deal and digital transition.

How to Apply:
Submission via Funding and Tenders Portal (F&T Portal).
