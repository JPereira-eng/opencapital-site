Mapping and service finder for Technology Infrastructures (CSA)

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2026-04-21

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 2.000.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 1
- Min/Max contribution: 2.000.000 EUR (fixed)

Description:
The initiative seeks to develop an interactive, open-source portal/inventory mapping European technology infrastructures and their services. The platform should facilitate SMEs, startups, and scaleups in discovering relevant research infrastructure services across Europe. Proposals must establish mechanisms for self-sustainability, stakeholder networking, and promote transnational collaboration among technology infrastructure operators.

Objectives:
- Create an open-access, interactive mapping portal of Technology Infrastructures across Europe
- Develop a service finder tool accessible to SMEs, startups and scaleups
- Support transnational use of technology infrastructure services
- Establish long-term sustainability mechanisms for the platform
- Foster networking among technology infrastructure operators
