CALL IDENTIFIER: ERASMUS-YOUTH-2025-CSC-OG-FPA
TITLE: Civil Society Cooperation in the field of Youth - FPA (2026-2027)
FRAMEWORK: Erasmus+ (ERASMUS2027)
PROGRAMME DIVISION: Civil Society Cooperation - Youth (FPA)
SOURCE: eu-funding-tenders

DESCRIPTION

The call provides structural support through operating grants to European non-governmental organisations and EU-wide youth networks. Key objectives include:

- Raising awareness of the EU Youth Strategy and European Youth Goals
- Promoting the EU Youth Dialogue to strengthen youth confidence in European institutions
- Improving access to reliable information and critical evaluation skills
- Strengthening democratic participation and youth autonomy
- Enhancing cooperation between youth civil society and public authorities
- Boosting stakeholder participation through digital and traditional channels
- Disseminating policy results and best practices

The call prioritizes inclusion and diversity, digital transformation, environmental sustainability, and participatory democracy.
