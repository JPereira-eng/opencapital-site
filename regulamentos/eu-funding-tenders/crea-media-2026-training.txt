OBJECTIVES:
The initiative aims to enhance the capacity of audiovisual professionals to adapt to new creative processes, market developments and digital technologies. Expected outcomes include promoting sustainable industry practices, supporting digital transition, fostering IP exploitation, and equipping professionals with combined creative and digital skills.

FOCUS AREAS:
- New creative processes and cross-disciplinary collaboration
- Digital transformation (virtual production, post-production, marketing)
- IP rights exploitation and new market trends
- Videogames training with digital tools
- Green transition and sustainability practices
