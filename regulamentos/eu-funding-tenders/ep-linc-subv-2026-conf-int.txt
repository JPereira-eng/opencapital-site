EP-LINC-SUBV-2026-CONF-INT - Supporting excellence, cooperation or research in conference interpreting training 2026-2027

Call Identifier: EP-LINC-SUBV-2026-CONF-INT
Topic: EP-LINC-SUBV-2026-CONF-INT-02
Programme: European Parliament (EP), Grants in the Area of Interpretation (EP-1-2)
Model Grant Agreement: EP Action Grant Budget-Based (EP-AG)
Status: Open

BUDGET
- Topic 02 (este topico): 150.000 EUR (2026)
- Topic 01: 450.000 EUR
- Topic 03: 150.000 EUR
- Total call: 750.000 EUR

PRAZOS
Opening date: 19 de marco de 2026
Submission deadline: 7 de maio de 2026 (single-stage)

DESCRICAO
The initiative aims to ensure the availability of a sufficient pool of highly qualified conference interpreters by strengthening training programs. Priority actions include integrating modern communication technologies, fostering international university cooperation, establishing regional collaboration networks, and creating centers of excellence in interpreter training.

ELEGIBILIDADE
- Higher education institutions and universities offering postgraduate conference interpreting courses
- University consortia and associations
- Professional bodies supporting worldwide conference interpreter training cooperation

CONDICOES PRINCIPAIS
- Admissibility and proposal formatting (Sections 4-5)
- Country eligibility (Section 5)
- Financial and operational capacity assessments (Sections 6-7)
- Evaluation and award criteria (Sections 8-9)

AREAS DE FOCO
- Conference interpreter training
- Postgraduate education
- International cooperation
- Language combination development
- Technology integration in training
