CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-INNOVBUSMOD
TITLE: Innovative tools and business models
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Project Grants (CREA-PJG) - CREA Action Grant Budget-Based
BUDGET: 10,500,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 15 January 2026, 17:00 Brussels time (closed - 172 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Evaluation results expected July 2026; grant agreements October 2026.

DESCRIPTION:
The action targets development and distribution of innovative tools and business models to enhance visibility, availability, and diversity of European works digitally while strengthening audiovisual industry competitiveness and sustainability.

KEY EXPECTED RESULTS:
- Enhanced competitiveness through technological innovation adoption
- Improved adaptation to AI and virtual world opportunities
- Greening of the European audiovisual industry
- Better production and circulation of European audiovisual works
- Increased online availability and visibility of diverse European content

FUNDED ACTIVITIES:
- Cross-border accessibility and discoverability tools
- Automated rights management systems and data analytics
- Distribution platform synergy optimization (festivals, cinemas, VOD)
- Innovation in production, financing, and promotion via AI, blockchain, virtual worlds
- Sustainability-focused industry tools

EXCLUSIONS: Hardware development (cameras, headsets) is not eligible.

ELIGIBILITY AND CONDITIONS:
Details regarding admissibility, eligible countries, financial/operational capacity, and exclusion criteria are specified in the official call document. Applications require strategies promoting sustainability, gender balance, inclusion, and diversity, plus disclosure plans for results transparency.

TIMELINE:
- Publication: 30 September 2025
- Evaluation: January-May 2026
- Results notification: July 2026
- Grant agreements: October 2026
