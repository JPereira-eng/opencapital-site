CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-DEVVGIM
TITLE: Video games and immersive content development
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Lump Sum Grants (CREA-AG-LS)
BUDGET: 10,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 11 February 2026, 17:00 Brussels time (closed - 336 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Evaluation results August 2026; grant agreement signing November 2026.

DESCRIPTION:
The initiative supports European video game producers, XR studios, and audiovisual companies developing interactive experiences for global audiences. It aims to increase the capacity of European video game producers and strengthen competitiveness while preserving intellectual property ownership.

OBJECTIVES:
- Enhance quality, appeal, feasibility, and cross-border potential
- Strengthen European market positioning for developers and immersive content producers
- Fund narrative-driven works with original content and quality gameplay across PC, console, mobile, and emerging platforms

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
Support targets development of interactive storytelling prototypes with original content and/or quality gameplay intended for production and global commercial exploitation. Applications must address sustainability, environmental responsibility, gender balance, and diversity.

KEY DATES:
- Publication: 30 September 2025
- Application deadline: 11 February 2026
- Evaluation: February-June 2026
- Results notification: August 2026
- Grant agreement signing: November 2026
