HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-14
Improving availability of secondary raw materials through recycling (IA)

Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Innovation Actions (HORIZON-IA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2027-01-29

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 28.000.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 4 projects
- Per project: 7.500.000 EUR (fixed)

Description:
This initiative develops EU-based recycling systems for metals and critical raw materials. Projects must demonstrate new or improved recycling systems located in the EU developing material efficient high-quality recycling, addressing the entire value chain from collection through refining.

Key Objectives:
- Develop and demonstrate new or improved high-quality recycling systems for metals and critical raw materials
- Address the full value chain from waste collection through material refining
- Analyze biodiversity impacts on urban mining sites and propose mitigation solutions
- Support implementation of the EU Critical Raw Materials Act (CRMA) objectives
- Promote international cooperation with Strategic Partnership countries, particularly Ukraine

Scope:
- Focus on metals and critical raw materials recycling (explicitly excluding downcycling/lower-quality outputs)
- The recycling industry and secondary material users must play prominent roles with adequate resource allocation
- Activities start at TRL 5, reaching TRL 7 by project completion
- Dedicated work package for clustering and cross-project cooperation required

Eligibility:
- Legal entities in Member States, associated countries, OECD nations, African Union members, MERCOSUR, CARIFORUM, Andean Community, or nations with EU strategic raw materials partnerships
- Entities controlled by non-eligible countries excluded
- Financial and operational capacity per Annex C
- Business case and credible exploitation strategy required

Special Conditions:
- Page limit extended by 3 pages for business case documentation
- Granting authority retains objection rights to ownership transfers up to 4 years post-project
- Evaluation per Annex D; award procedures per Annex F; grant agreement per Annex G
