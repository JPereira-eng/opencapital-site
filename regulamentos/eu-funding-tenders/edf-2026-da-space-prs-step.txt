## Description

The general objective supports development necessary for integrating and qualifying Public Regulated Service (PRS) receivers into existing military operational platforms and enhancing navigation warfare (NAVWAR) capabilities. Focus areas include Galileo PRS receiver technology development and NAVWAR information management systems, addressing guided munitions and missiles in operational environments.

Beyond defence technologies, this topic contributes to STEP objectives as defined in the STEP Regulation targeting deep and digital technologies.

Targeted activities encompass design, system prototyping, testing and qualification, including eligible upstream and downstream development activities.

Indicative budget: EUR 50,000,000 for this topic under EDF-2026-DA. One proposal anticipated for funding, though multiple proposals may be funded depending on submission quality and available resources.
