HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-31
Efficient capture / purification / utilisation of CO2 for the production of competitive products (RIA)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
STATUS: Open
FUNDING SCHEME: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
SUPPORTED ACTIONS: Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
PROGRAMME PERIOD: Horizon Europe 2026-2027
PUBLICATION DATE: June 2, 2025

BUDGET OVERVIEW (2026):
Total Budget: EUR 43,800,000
Expected Grants: 7
Min Contribution per Grant: EUR 5,000,000
Max Contribution per Grant: EUR 7,000,000

DEADLINE: January 28, 2027

DESCRIPTION:
Projects must focus on reducing production costs of CO2-based products to achieve market competitiveness. The scope emphasizes smart integration of CO2 capture, purification, and conversion, with a focus on reducing energy demand throughout the value chain.

Key requirements:
- Incorporate renewable energy sources in the process
- Leverage available heat sources to improve efficiency
- Overcome conversion process constraints
- Develop competitive products from captured CO2
- Demonstrate significant cost reduction potential vs. fossil-based alternatives

Exclusions: CO2 conversion to methanol and fuels is explicitly excluded from scope.

Activities must start at TRL 4-5 and reach TRL 6 by project completion.

ELIGIBILITY:
Eligibility details in Annex B of the Work Programme General Annexes. Support resources include the Online Manual, National Contact Points, and the Funding and Tenders Portal. Partner search functionality available.

CONDITIONS:
- Extended 3-page limit in Part B for business case and exploitation strategy
- Lump sum budget items in categories C/D require justification in Excel budget table
- GHG avoidance methodology recommended
- Comprehensive lifecycle and economic assessments mandatory
- Single-stage submission procedure

KEYWORDS: CO2 capture, CO2 purification, CO2 utilisation, carbon utilisation, circular carbon economy, renewable energy integration, industrial decarbonisation, competitive CO2-based products, TRL 4-6
