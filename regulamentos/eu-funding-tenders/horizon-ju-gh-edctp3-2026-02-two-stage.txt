STATUS: Two-stage call (2026 Work Programme)

ABOUT THE GLOBAL HEALTH EDCTP3 JU
----------------------------------
The Global Health EDCTP3 Joint Undertaking is a partnership between:
- The European Union
- The EDCTP Association (European and Developing Countries Clinical Trials Partnership)
- Members: Several European countries + African partner countries

The partnership aims to:
1. Deliver new solutions (diagnostics, drugs, vaccines) for reducing the burden of infectious diseases in sub-Saharan Africa
2. Strengthen research capacities for epidemic preparedness and response to re-emerging infectious diseases

RESEARCH PRIORITIES
-------------------
- HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, and malaria (endemic diseases in sub-Saharan Africa)
- Neglected tropical diseases
- Emerging and re-emerging infectious diseases
- Research capacity strengthening in African institutions
- Clinical trial methodology and regulatory capacity
- Vaccine development and evaluation
- Point-of-care diagnostics
- Antimicrobial resistance

PARTNERSHIP GOVERNANCE
-----------------------
Co-funded by the EU under Horizon Europe and by the EDCTP Association member states.
Joint programme managed by the EDCTP Association Secretariat.
Projects involve partnerships between European and African research institutions.

TWO-STAGE CALL PROCESS
-----------------------
Stage 1: Outline application evaluated on Excellence and Impact.
Stage 2: Full proposal evaluated on Excellence, Impact, and Implementation.
Full proposals submitted by successful Stage 1 applicants.

ELIGIBLE APPLICANTS
-------------------
- Research institutions and universities in EU Member States and associated countries
- African research institutions in eligible countries
- International organisations
- Consortia must include partners from both Europe and sub-Saharan Africa
