TIPO DE ACAO: Lump Sum Grants (CERV-LS)

DESCRICAO:
The initiative promotes exchanges between citizens of different countries, in particular through networks of towns, to foster awareness of European heritage and diversity. It aims to develop sustainable town networks that deepen cooperation while promoting Union values and fundamental rights.
