INSTRUMENTO: DIGITAL-ECCC-2026-DEPLOY-CYBER-10-NCH
NOME: National Cyber Hubs - CyberHUBs Capacity Building
PROGRAMA: Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)
CALL IDENTIFIER: DIGITAL-ECCC-2026-DEPLOY-CYBER-10
FONTE: EU Funding & Tenders Portal

DOTACAO: EUR 2,000,000 (National Cyber Hub track); EUR 2,000,000 (Cross-Border track)

PRAZO: January 29, 2025
DATA DE ABERTURA: April 9, 2024

TIPO DE ACAO: DIGITAL JU Simple Grants (DIGITAL Action Grant Budget-Based)

DESCRICAO:
The initiative aims to establish world-class National Cyber Hubs functioning as clearing houses for detecting, gathering and storing data on cybersecurity threats. These entities will analyze threat intelligence and enable information sharing among public and private cybersecurity actors.

OBJETIVOS PRINCIPAIS:
- Create or strengthen National Cyber Hubs with advanced monitoring capabilities
- Build threat intelligence and situational awareness infrastructure
- Enable collaboration between cybersecurity stakeholders
- Deliver early warnings to critical infrastructure operators
- Monitor undersea infrastructure, including submarine cables

ATIVIDADES ELEGIVEIS:
- Equipment and tools procurement
- Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) data feeds
- Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) systems
- Artificial intelligence and automation tools
- Training initiatives aligned with the European Cybersecurity Skills Framework
- Submarine cable surveillance solutions

ABORDAGEM - DOIS WORKSTREAMS:
1. Joint Procurement Action coordinated with Member States
2. Grant Support covering preparatory activities, stakeholder cooperation, and operational costs

ELEGIBILIDADE:
- EU Member States and associated countries
- Appointment decision from Member State designating the National Cyber Hub entity required
- Applicants must meet financial and operational capacity standards

CONTACTO: applicants@eccc.europa.eu

PALAVRAS-CHAVE: Artificial intelligence, Capacity building, Cyber-physical systems, Cybersecurity, Defence, Real-time data analytics, Technical Specifications for Interoperability
