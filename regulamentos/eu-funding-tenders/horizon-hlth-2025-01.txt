PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe Cluster 1 - Health (Single Stage 2025)
CODIGO: HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01
ESTADO: Closed (prazo encerrado em setembro 2025)
FONTE: HaDEA (European Health and Digital Executive Agency) / Horizon Europe

DEADLINE
- Prazo de submissao: 16 de setembro de 2025, 17h00 (CEST)

TOPICOS IDENTIFICADOS (call single-stage 2025)
1. HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01-DISEASE-01: Testing safety and efficacy of phage therapy for antibiotic-resistant bacterial infections
2. HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01-DISEASE-03: Development of antibodies and antibody-derived proteins for prevention and treatment of infectious diseases with epidemic potential
3. HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01-DISEASE-04: Leveraging artificial intelligence for pandemic preparedness and response
4. Topicos em doencas nao transmissiveis
5. Topicos em terapias celulares
6. Topicos em inteligencia artificial na saude
7. Total de 13 topicos identificados na call

AREAS TEMATICAS
- Doencas infeciosas (incluindo tuberculose, epidemias, resistencia antimicrobiana)
- Doencas nao transmissiveis (cancer, doencas cardiovasculares)
- Terapias celulares e genicas
- Inteligencia artificial em saude
- Saude mental

OBJETIVOS
A Cluster 1 - Health do Horizon Europe financia investigacao e inovacao no dominio da saude com foco em: melhorar a saude e o bem-estar dos cidadaos europeus e globais; desenvolver abordagens inovadoras para prevencao, diagnostico e tratamento de doencas; fortalecer os sistemas de saude.

TIPO DE ACAO
- Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
- Innovation Actions (IA)
- Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)

ELEGIBILIDADE
- Consorcio multinacional (minimo 3 entidades independentes de 3 paises diferentes)
- Universidades, centros de investigacao, empresas, hospitais, PMEs
- Paises da UE e paises associados ao Horizon Europe

KEYWORDS
Horizon Europe, Cluster 1, Health, Infectious Diseases, Phage Therapy, Artificial Intelligence, Pandemic Preparedness, Clinical Research, HaDEA, Medical Research

CALL ANTERIOR / CONTEXTO
A call HORIZON-HLTH-2025-01 e a call single-stage para 2025. Existe tambem a HORIZON-HLTH-2025-03-two-stage para modalidade em duas fases.
