INSTRUMENTO: Supporting the implementation of the Adaptation to Climate Change Mission
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-MISS-2025-01
CALL REFERENCE: HORIZON-MISS-2025-01-CLIMA (6 topics)
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - EU Missions
AGENCIA: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)
STATUS: Closed

BUDGET: EUR 113,600,000 (indicativo)
DEADLINE: 29 October 2025, 17:00 CET
PUBLICACAO: 6 May 2025
MODELO: Single-stage submission

MISSAO:
Supporting EU regions, cities and local authorities in their efforts to build resilience against the impacts of climate change. Part of the EU Mission for Adaptation to Climate Change, which aims to make at least 150 European regions and communities climate resilient by 2030.

TOPICOS COBERTOS (HORIZON-MISS-2025-01-CLIMA, 6 topics):
- Supporting regions and local authorities in assessing climate risks and developing Action Plans towards climate resilience
- Testing and demonstrating innovative solutions to improve resilience to extreme heat
- Understanding incentives for private sector engagement in climate adaptation
- Additional topics focused on regional and local climate adaptation implementation

CONTEXTO:
EUR 233.65 million in EU funding available across the Adaptation to Climate Change and Restore our Ocean and Waters missions (deadlines September-October 2025). A recorded info day presentation is available from 23 May 2025.

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Eligible entities must be established in EU Member States or Horizon Europe Associated Countries. Full eligibility and funding rate details available on the Funding and Tenders Portal under call reference HORIZON-MISS-2025-01-CLIMA.
