HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-48
'Proof of market' to improve valorisation and commercialisation of Horizon generated R&I results (IA)

Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Innovation Actions (IA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2026-04-21

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 5.000.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 25 projects
- Per-grant amount: 200.000 EUR (fixed)
- Min/Max contribution: 200.000 EUR

Description:
This initiative funds small consortia (spin-offs, startups, SMEs) to explore the commercial potential of results from Cluster 4 'Digital, Industry and Space' or LEIT-funded Horizon 2020 projects. The goal is to accelerate the path from research outputs to market-ready innovations.

Eligible Valorisation Activities:
- Technology demonstration
- Market analysis and business case development
- Certification and regulatory compliance
- User testing and validation
- IP consolidation and protection strategy

Eligibility Requirements:
- Applicants must own or have documented access rights to relevant knowledge assets from prior Horizon projects
- Primarily targets spin-offs, startups, and SMEs
- Small consortia structure preferred
- Entities controlled by non-eligible countries excluded (notably entities controlled by China in critical technology areas)

Conditions:
- Admissibility per Annexes A and E
- Eligible countries per Annex B
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Award criteria per Annex D
- Restrictions on Chinese-controlled entities in critical technology areas
