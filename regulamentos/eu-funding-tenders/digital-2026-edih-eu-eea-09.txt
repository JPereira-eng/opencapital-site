DESCRICAO:
The initiative aims to strengthen European Digital Innovation Hubs across EU and Associated Countries. Each EDIH delivers four core service categories: testing facilities, workforce development, investment support, and ecosystem networking. The program emphasizes AI adoption and green digital technologies while maintaining human-centric values.

CATEGORIAS DE SERVICO DOS EDIHs:
1. Testing facilities
2. Workforce development
3. Investment support
4. Ecosystem networking
