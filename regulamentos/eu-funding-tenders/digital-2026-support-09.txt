DESCRICAO:
The action aims to enhance coordination and capacity-building for National Contact Points across the Digital Europe Programme. It seeks to maximize awareness of funding opportunities, boost programme visibility, and ensure long-term dissemination of results. The initiative strengthens NCP capabilities, promotes peer learning, and fosters an interactive community of practice with emphasis on inclusiveness and geographical coverage.

Proposals should facilitate transnational cooperation, enable cross-border activities, support knowledge exchange, and raise overall support standards for applicants. Activities encompass programme communication, information sessions for stakeholders, dissemination efforts, and material development. Special focus lies on enhancing NCP competence and supporting less experienced contact points.
