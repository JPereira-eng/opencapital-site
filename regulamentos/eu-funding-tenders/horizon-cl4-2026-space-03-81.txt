HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-81
Space critical EEE components for EU non-dependence – Radiation Hard FPGA on 7nm

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 12,740,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 1
- Min Contribution: 12,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 13,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: May 5, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Projects must contribute to: (1) strengthening EU strategic autonomy by reducing critical EEE space component dependencies; (2) providing unrestricted access to technologies for European space missions; (3) developing independent operational capacity in space; (4) improving competitiveness with products of equivalent or superior performance compared to global competitors.

SCOPE:
Fund development of critical EEE space components to reduce extra-EU dependency. The supply chain shall preferably be built fully based in EU, with restrictions on advanced semiconductor processors. Specific focus: radiation hard FPGA on 7nm technology.

Proposals must include: detailed supply chain analysis, description of technological processes, compliance with export regulations (ITAR, EAR), industrial technical roadmap and commercial plan, state-of-the-art literature review.

Activities must achieve TRL 6 by project end. Beneficiaries must guarantee availability of developed hardware/processes for up to 4 years after completion for entities in EU, Norway and Iceland at fair prices without legal restrictions.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Entities established in EU Member States, Norway and Iceland
- Entities established in an eligible country that are directly or indirectly controlled by a non-eligible country may not participate without approved guarantees
- No subcontractors in non-eligible countries
- Protection of strategic/sensitive information (EUCI and SEN)

- Page limit: 80 pages
