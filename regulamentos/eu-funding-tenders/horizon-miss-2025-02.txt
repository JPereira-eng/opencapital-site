CALL: HORIZON-MISS-2025-02
NAME: Supporting the implementation of the Cancer Mission (2025)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - EU Missions - Mission Cancer
MANAGED BY: HaDEA (European Health and Digital Executive Agency)
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
The EU Mission on Cancer aims to save 3 million more lives by 2030 and to improve the lives of people affected by cancer. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02 (the 2025 edition call under this mission) covers six specific topics from topic -02-01 through -02-06, each targeting different dimensions of cancer research and care.

SIX TOPICS UNDER THIS CALL:

1. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-01: Sustained collaboration of national and regional cancer funders to support the Cancer Mission through translational research
2. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-02: Understanding the effects of environmental exposure on the risk of paediatric, adolescent and young adult cancers
3. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-03: Innovative surgery as the cornerstone of affordable multi-modal therapeutic interventions benefitting cancer patients with locally advanced or metastatic disease
4. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-04: Investigator-initiated multinational early-stage innovative clinical trials for paediatric cancer
5. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-05: Pragmatic clinical trials to enhance the quality of life of older cancer patients (65 years and older) through nutrition
6. HORIZON-MISS-2025-CANCER-02-06: Support to the network of National Cancer Mission Hubs (NCMHs)

DEADLINE:
Unified deadline for all six topics: 16 September 2025, 17:00 CEST

BUDGET:
Budget contributions from both the Health cluster budget and the Culture, Creativity and Inclusive Society budget. Specific allocations per topic are detailed in the official work programme. Indicative per-topic budgets in the range of EUR 10-40 million.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Universities, research hospitals, clinical centres, cancer institutes
- National cancer alliances and mission hubs
- Patient organisations and advocacy groups
- SMEs and industry (where relevant to the topic)
- Established in EU Member States or Horizon Europe Associated Countries
- Consortium requirement: minimum 3 independent legal entities from 3 different EU/associated countries

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Collaborative translational research between funders and research teams
- Environmental epidemiology studies in paediatric/adolescent cancers
- Clinical trials for innovative surgical and multi-modal approaches
- Early-stage investigator-initiated trials for paediatric cancer
- Nutrition-focused clinical trials in elderly cancer patients
- Networking and capacity building for National Cancer Mission Hubs

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Scientific and technological excellence
- Coherence with Cancer Mission implementation plan
- Patient involvement and impact on healthcare outcomes
- Cross-border collaboration and data sharing capacity
- Quality of consortium and resource allocation

CANCER MISSION CONTEXT:
The Cancer Mission targets better understanding of cancer causes, better prevention, more innovative treatments, better quality of life for patients and survivors. National Cancer Mission Hubs coordinate national participation across all EU Member States.
