CALL: HORIZON-CL5-2025-01-Two-Stage
NAME: Cluster 5 Call 01-2025 (2-stage) (WP 2025)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - Cluster 5: Climate, Energy and Mobility
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
HORIZON-CL5-2025-01-Two-Stage is part of the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2025 for Cluster 5: Climate, Energy and Mobility. This is a two-stage call covering topics related to cross-sectoral solutions for the climate transition, sustainable and secure energy supply, and competitive mobility solutions.

CLUSTER 5 OVERVIEW:
Cluster 5 covers the full energy value chain, cross-cutting social sciences, and innovative low-carbon solutions for industry and mobility. It aims to deliver the European Green Deal objectives, supporting the clean energy transition and climate neutrality by 2050.

TOPICS AND DESTINATIONS (selected):
Under HORIZON-CL5-2025-01, topics include:
- Cross-sectoral solutions for the climate transition (CCUS, industrial decarbonisation)
- Sustainable, secure and competitive energy supply (hydrogen, renewables integration)
- Efficient and clean energy use in buildings and industry
- Smart and sustainable mobility (clean vehicles, transport systems)

TWO-STAGE PROCESS:
Stage 1: Outline proposals evaluated on Excellence and Impact (abbreviated)
Stage 2: Full proposals for selected outlines, evaluated on all three criteria

BUDGET:
Total EU funding available across Cluster 5 calls in 2025: EUR 636.6 million.
Individual topic budgets vary from EUR 500,000 to EUR 15 million per project, depending on the topic and action type.

DEADLINES:
Stage 1 (outline proposals): typically September-October 2025
Stage 2 (full proposals): typically March-April 2026

ELIGIBILITY:
- Businesses, universities, research institutes, charities, not-for-profit organisations
- Established in EU Member States or countries associated to Horizon Europe
- Consortium-based: minimum 3 independent legal entities from 3 different EU/Associated Countries
- SME participation is encouraged and some topics have SME-specific requirements

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Research and Innovation Actions (RIA): primarily research activities with limited demonstration
- Innovation Actions (IA): activities closer to market, including demonstration and testing
- Coordination and Support Actions (CSA): networking, coordination, studies
- Specific to energy: testing facilities, pilots, demonstration projects for clean energy technologies

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Excellence: quality and soundness of the scientific/technical approach
- Impact: expected outcome and impact, measures to maximise impact
- Quality and efficiency of implementation
