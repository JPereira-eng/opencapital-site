CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-MSCA-2026-DN-01 / HORIZON-MSCA-2026-DN-01-01
TITLE: MSCA Doctoral Networks 2026
FRAMEWORK: Horizon Europe - Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA)
PROGRAMME PILLAR: HORIZON.1.2.1 - Excellent Science
SOURCE: eu-funding-tenders

BUDGET

593,033,846 EUR allocated for 2026, distributed across three action types with equal allocations.

DEADLINES

- Planned Opening Date: January 27, 2026
- Submission Deadline: April 21, 2026
- Deadline Model: Single-stage submission process
- Call Information Event: June 3, 2026

DESCRIPTION

The call supports doctoral programmes through partnerships involving universities, research institutions, businesses (including SMEs), and socio-economic actors across Europe and third countries.

KEY OBJECTIVES

- Equip doctoral candidates with research and transferable skills for improved employability
- Develop knowledge applicable to product/service innovation
- Enhance networking and communication with scientific peers and the public
- Strengthen quality and sustainability of doctoral training programmes
- Foster knowledge transfer between sectors and disciplines
- Boost R&I capacity and internationalisation

CORE COMPONENTS

- Open, transparent, merit-based selection procedures for candidates
- Mandatory supervisory boards coordinating network-wide activities
- Career Development Plans established jointly by supervisors and candidates, reviewed within 18 months
- Substantial training modules addressing transferable skills, digital competencies, and open science principles
- Inter-sectoral secondments to non-academic organisations
- Joint supervision required for Industrial and Joint Doctorates

ACTIONS SUPPORTED

Three action types under HORIZON-AG-UN (Unit Grant):

1. Joint Doctorates - Highly integrated international collaboration delivering joint, double, or multiple degrees recognised in EU Member States or Associated Countries
2. Industrial Doctorates - Joint academic-industry supervision with skills development in business settings
3. Standard Doctoral Networks - General doctoral programmes addressing identified R&I needs

ELIGIBILITY

Eligible Countries: EU Member States and Horizon Europe Associated Countries; third country participation permitted for strategic partnerships. Details in Annex B of Work Programme General Annexes.

Participant Requirements: Open to universities, research institutions, research infrastructures, businesses, SMEs, and socio-economic actors.

KEY CONDITIONS

- Proposals subject to admissibility requirements per Annex A and E of General Annexes
- Financial and operational capacity assessment required (Annex C)
- Evaluation criteria and scoring detailed in MSCA Work Programme section
- Legal/financial setup governed by Annex G of General Annexes
- MSCA-specific conditions and exceptions apply
- Particular attention is paid to the quality of supervision and mentoring
- Networks should foster culture of Open Science, knowledge valorisation

KEYWORDS

Research mobility, doctoral training, inter-sectoral collaboration, industrial partnerships, joint degrees, transferable skills, open science, research integrity, career development
