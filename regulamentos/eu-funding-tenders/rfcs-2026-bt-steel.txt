RFCS-2026-BT-Steel - RFCS-2026-Steel-Big Tickets under the Clean Industrial Deal

Call Identifier: RFCS-2026-BT-Steel
Topic: RFCS-2026-BT-Steel-CID
Programme: Research Fund for Coal and Steel (RFCS 2027), Division RFCS-1-1
Status: Open

BUDGET
- CID action: 50.000.000 EUR (2026)
- SMAP action: 25.000.000 EUR (2026)
- Total: 75.000.000 EUR

PRAZOS
Opening date: 17 de abril de 2025
Single-stage submission deadline: 2 de junho de 2026

DESCRICAO
This call targets recycling technologies in steel production, covering metal and mineral recovery, residue valorization, scrap management with AI, low-carbon production routes, and circular economy approaches. Proposals must achieve Technology Readiness Level 7-8 and demonstrate CO2 reduction aligned with the European Green Deal and Clean Industrial Deal objectives.

ELEGIBILIDADE
- Pilot or demonstration projects only
- Innovative technologies meeting specified recycling objectives
- Clear quantified improvements versus existing installations
- Viable business cases and scalable deployment plans
- Consistency with European Partnership on Clean Steel

CONDICOES
Detailed requirements available in the call document (sections 5-10), covering admissibility, eligible countries, financial capacity, evaluation criteria, and legal frameworks.
