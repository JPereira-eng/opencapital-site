Description:
The objective is to establish an EDIC (European Digital Infrastructure Consortium) Support Hub that ensures EDICs have access to comprehensive legal, operational, and coordination support. The Hub contributes to the consolidation and long-term sustainability of the EDIC ecosystem.

Key functions of the EDIC Support Hub:
1. Comprehensive legal, operational, and coordination support to EDICs and strategic support to the European Commission
2. Strengthening EDIC governance and compliance with the Digital Decade Policy Programme Decision and EU/national legislation
3. Improving accessibility of consolidated information on EDICs
4. Working with the Commission and Member States on identification and mobilisation of new EDIC initiatives
5. Processing gathered data to generate evidence for future strategic development of the EDIC ecosystem

Expected outcomes:
- Comprehensive support to EDICs, the Commission, and Member States through legal and operational advice
- Provision of a central repository for EDIC information
- Support for EDICs in preparation and early stages
- Maintaining a consolidated data and information repository on all EDICs
- Coordination and liaison between EDICs and other digital infrastructure initiatives
- Proposed milestones for future EDIC ecosystem development
- Establishment of an EDIC community of practice
- Organisation of an Annual EDIC Gathering event
- Annual impact report covering EDIC projects and their outcomes

Context:
EDICs are a legal instrument under the Digital Decade Policy Programme that allows multiple countries to jointly implement digital infrastructure projects at EU scale. The EDIC Support Hub fills a gap in the ecosystem by providing centralised expertise, governance support, and coordination across all active and planned EDICs.
