HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-05
Improving Climate and Weather Models for Africa

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07
TOPIC: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-05
STATUS: Open
PROGRAM: Horizon Europe - Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness - Climate, Energy and Mobility - Climate Science and Solutions
ACTION TYPE: RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
FUNDING MODEL: Lump sum grants

DEADLINE: 21 April 2026, 17:00 CET (postponed due to technical issues)

BUDGET:
- This topic (D1-05): EUR 21,000,000
- Expected grants: 3
- Grant per project: EUR 7,000,000
- Total call (all D1 topics): EUR 82,000,000

OBJECTIVES:
Enhanced weather prediction and climate modeling capabilities for Africa. Projects should address knowledge gaps in understanding climate dynamics at regional and continental scales, focusing on rainfall prediction and extreme weather events. Research emphasizes close cooperation and co-creation between African and European teams to ensure solution relevance and local ownership.

SCOPE:
- Capacity-building and data quality assurance in Africa
- Leverage AI/ML and high-performance computing technologies
- Open access required for new models, modules, and tools developed
- Use Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS for satellite-based data

DESPESAS ELEGIVEIS / ELEGIBILIDADE:
- Minimum 30% of beneficiaries must be from African Union member states
- Legal entities in all African Union member states are exceptionally eligible for funding
- Lump sum funding model applies
- Open access required for new models, modules, and tools developed
- Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS must be used for satellite-based data
- Financial and operational capacity per standard Horizon Europe requirements
