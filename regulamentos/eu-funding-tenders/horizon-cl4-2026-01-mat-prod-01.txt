Advanced manufacturing for key products (IA) (Made in Europe partnership)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
STATUS: Open
FUNDING SCHEME: HORIZON Innovation Actions (IA) - HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based
SUPPORTED ACTIONS: Innovation Actions (IA)
PROGRAMME PERIOD: Horizon Europe 2026-2027
PUBLICATION DATE: April 2, 2025

BUDGET OVERVIEW (2026):
Total Budget: EUR 38,000,000
Expected Grants: 6
Min Contribution per Grant: EUR 6,000,000
Max Contribution per Grant: EUR 8,000,000

OPENING DATE: April 5, 2026
DEADLINE: January 29, 2027

DESCRIPTION:
The initiative targets development of manufacturing technologies and machinery to enhance European competitiveness in producing key components. Focus areas include:
- Circularity through secondary raw materials integration
- Innovative advanced materials for manufacturing
- Resource efficiency in materials and energy consumption

Activities should progress from TRL 5 to TRL 7 by project completion. The call explicitly excludes battery production from the scope.

International cooperation is strongly encouraged, particularly with Japan or Taiwan, to strengthen global competitiveness in advanced manufacturing.

ELIGIBILITY:
Eligibility criteria and financial capacity requirements are detailed in the Work Programme General Annexes (Annexes B-C). Entities from EU Member States, associated countries, and relevant international partners may participate.

CONDITIONS:
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Business case and exploitation strategy required
- Technology readiness: TRL 5 at start, TRL 7 at completion
- International cooperation with Japan/Taiwan encouraged

KEYWORDS: Advanced manufacturing, circularity, secondary raw materials, innovative materials, European manufacturing competitiveness, Made in Europe partnership, key products
