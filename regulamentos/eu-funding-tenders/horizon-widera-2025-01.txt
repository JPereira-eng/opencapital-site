OBJETIVO:
Raise excellence in science and in value creation through deeper and geographically inclusive cooperation in alliances of higher education institutions. Universities are positioned as agents of change within research and innovation.

DESCRICAO:
The European Excellence Initiative revitalises academic and research environments by mobilising investments from local and regional governments, resulting in comprehensive enhancements. The action engages with universities to empower them further as actors of change in research and innovation.

TOPICOS: 1 topic under this call identifier (HORIZON-WIDERA-2025-01-ACCESS-01)

CONTEXTO:
Part of the 'Widening participation and spreading excellence' sub-programme, which contributes to building research and innovation capacity for countries lagging behind, in line with the policy objectives of the European Research Area (ERA).
