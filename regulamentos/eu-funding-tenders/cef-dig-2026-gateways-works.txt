CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS-WORKS
Backbone connectivity for Digital Global Gateways - Works

Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS-WORKS
Call Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS
Status: Open
Framework Programme: Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) - CEF2027
Programme Division: CEF-2.3.1 (Digital)
Budget Overview (EUR): 180,000,000 for 2026
Deadline Date: September 5, 2026
Action Type: CEF-INFRA (CEF Infrastructure Projects), CEF Action Grant Budget-Based, single-stage submission

Description:
The call supports deployment or significant upgrade of backbone networks addressing vulnerabilities in EU infrastructure. Proposals must connect at least two Member States, a Member State with its islands/regions, or Member States with third countries. Projects must address market failures including lack of redundancy, inability to satisfy demand, or regulatory non-competitiveness. Expected impacts include significantly improved security and resilience and strengthened connectivity quality. Performance indicators measure backbone length and transmission capacity created.

The Digital Global Gateways line within CEF Digital is the European Union's instrument to deploy strategic backbone connectivity, including submarine cables, terrestrial fibre routes, ground stations and landing infrastructure that strengthen the EU's digital sovereignty. The Works strand finances physical deployment after the studies and permits are in place.

Eligibility:
Eligible applicants include public bodies, telecommunications operators, infrastructure consortia, and research institutions established in EU Member States, EEA countries and CEF-associated third countries. Co-financing rates depend on the type of action and the strategic relevance of the route. Proposals must demonstrate market failure justification and additionality versus commercial financing.

Despesas elegiveis: investimento em infraestrutura passiva (cable, conduits, landing stations), equipamento ativo (DWDM, routers, monitoring), engenharia, marine surveys, permitting, instalacao, testes de aceitacao, gestao de projeto. Componentes de resiliencia (redundancia, deteccao de intrusoes, smart cable systems) sao especialmente valorizados.

Criterios de selecao: relevancia estrategica para a soberania digital UE, contribuicao para resiliencia e seguranca, qualidade tecnica e maturidade, impacto socioeconomico, sustentabilidade financeira post-co-financiamento, alinhamento com prioridades CPEI (Common Programmable European Infrastructure) e cabos submarinos prioritarios.

Conditions: Detailed in sections 5-11 of the call document, covering admissibility, eligibility, financial capacity, submission procedures, evaluation criteria, and grant legal frameworks.

Keywords: Backbone connectivity, submarine cables, terrestrial fibre, digital sovereignty, redundancy, resilience, CPEI, Digital Global Gateways, CEF2027.
