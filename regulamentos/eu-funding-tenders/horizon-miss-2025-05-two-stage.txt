HORIZON-MISS-2025-05-two-stage - Soil Deal for Europe Mission Call 2025
========================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-MISS-2025-05-two-stage
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - EU Mission "A Soil Deal for Europe"
CLUSTER: Cluster 6 - Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment
STATUS: Two-stage call. Stage 1 deadline: 12 March 2026. Stage 2 deadline: 15 September 2026.

MISSION CONTEXT
---------------
The EU Mission "A Soil Deal for Europe" aims to establish 100 Living Labs and Lighthouses to lead the transition towards healthy soils by 2030. The mission mobilises research, citizens, regional and local authorities, and all relevant stakeholders to restore and protect soils across Europe.

CALL TOPICS
-----------
HORIZON-MISS-2025-05-SOIL-01-two-stage: Living labs to enhance soil health in Continental, Boreal and Alpine biogeographical regions

HORIZON-MISS-2025-05-SOIL-02-two-stage: Broadening the living labs approach for soil health in Africa and Latin America and the Caribbean (LAC)

OBJECTIVE
---------
This two-stage call supports activities that contribute to building and scaling soil health Living Labs and Lighthouses across different European biogeographical regions and globally. Projects should:
- Demonstrate multi-actor approaches integrating science, practice and policy
- Develop and test innovative solutions for improving soil health indicators
- Facilitate knowledge exchange and capacity building among farmers, researchers, land managers and policymakers
- Contribute to the 100 Living Labs and Lighthouses target of the EU Soil Mission

ELIGIBLE ORGANISATIONS
-----------------------
Research organisations, universities, SMEs, large companies, NGOs, public authorities, farmer organisations, and other entities established in Horizon Europe eligible countries (EU Member States + associated countries).

TWO-STAGE SUBMISSION PROCESS
-----------------------------
Stage 1: Outline application (maximum 10 pages)
- Evaluated against: Excellence and Impact
- Deadline: 12 March 2026
- Successful applicants invited to submit full proposals

Stage 2: Full proposal
- Evaluated against: Excellence, Impact, and Implementation
- Deadline: 15 September 2026

CONTEXT: EU SOIL MISSION 2030 TARGETS
---------------------------------------
- Restore degraded soils to good ecosystem condition
- Ensure 75% of EU soils are in healthy condition by 2030
- Achieve carbon neutrality in EU managed soils by 2035
- 100 Living Labs and Lighthouses across Europe by 2030
- Reduce soil pollution from pesticides, nutrients, and plastics
