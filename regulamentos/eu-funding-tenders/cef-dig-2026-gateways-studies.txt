CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS-STUDIES
Backbone connectivity for Digital Global Gateways - Studies

Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS-STUDIES
Call Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS
Status: Open
Framework Programme: Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) / CEF2027
Programme Division: CEF-2.3.1 (Digital)
Budget Overview (2026): 180,000,000
Deadline Date: September 27, 2026
Action Type: CEF Project Grants (CEF-PJG), single-stage
Planned Opening: April 6, 2026

Description:
This call supports studies for backbone network deployment addressing risks and vulnerabilities in EU infrastructure. Studies related to the deployment or significant upgrade of backbone networks that address risks, vulnerabilities and dependencies in the EU backbone infrastructure. Eligible projects must connect at least two Member States, a Member State with its islands/outermost regions, or Member States with third countries. Priority given to submarine cable resilience in CPEI Areas 1-7 and 1-13 (excluding Areas 8-10). Fundable activities include preparatory work, marine surveys, permit applications, and feasibility studies for future deployments.

The Studies strand of the Digital Global Gateways line is the upstream complement to Works: it pays for the engineering, marine, environmental, regulatory and economic studies that need to be completed before infrastructure can be built. It is the right entry point for consortia that have a strategic route in mind but still need to mature it to the level of a bankable project.

Eligibility:
Public bodies, telecommunications operators, infrastructure consortia, research institutions, and engineering firms established in EU Member States, EEA countries and CEF-associated third countries. Studies should be timed so the resulting project is ready to be submitted to a future CEF-DIG Works call.

Despesas elegiveis: estudos de viabilidade tecnica e economica, marine surveys, environmental impact assessments, regulatory and permitting studies, route mapping, traffic and demand analysis, business cases, custos de pessoal e consultoria especializada, despesas de viagem, gestao do estudo.

Criterios de selecao: relevancia estrategica para a soberania digital UE, qualidade metodologica do estudo, prioridade para CPEI Areas 1-7 e 1-13, maturidade do consortium, probabilidade de o estudo desembocar num projeto Works candidato em ciclos futuros do CEF Digital.

Conditions: Admissibility, page limits, eligible countries, financial capacity, exclusion criteria, submission/evaluation processes, award criteria, and scoring thresholds are detailed in the call document sections 5-11 and the Online Manual.

Keywords: Studies, feasibility, marine survey, submarine cables, terrestrial fibre, digital sovereignty, CPEI, Digital Global Gateways, CEF2027.
