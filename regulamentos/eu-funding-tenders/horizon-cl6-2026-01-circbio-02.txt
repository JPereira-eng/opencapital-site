HORIZON-CL6-2026-01-CIRCBIO-02
Advancing recycling technologies for mixed post-consumer textiles waste from blended products

Programme: Horizon Europe
Type of Action: HORIZON-IA (Innovation Actions) - Lump Sum Grants
Deadline: 2026-06-17

Budget Overview:
- 2026: 12M EUR (RIA), 3M EUR (CSA)
- Expected Grants: 3

Keywords: Circular economy, Climate change mitigation, Ecodesign, Life Cycle Analysis, Environment, Pollution and Climate, Lightweight construction, textile technology, New business opportunities, New industrial value chains, Sustainable design, Sustainable innovation, Textiles including synthetic dyes, colours and fibres, Waste management, Waste recycling

Description:
This topic addresses the advancement of recycling technologies for mixed post-consumer textiles waste from blended products. Textile waste from blended materials (e.g., cotton-polyester blends) presents significant recycling challenges due to the difficulty of separating fibre types.

The topic seeks innovation actions that develop:
- Advanced sorting technologies for mixed post-consumer textile waste streams
- Novel recycling processes capable of handling blended textile materials
- Chemical and mechanical recycling technologies adapted to fibre blends
- New industrial value chains for recycled textile fibres
- Business models supporting circular textiles economy
- Life Cycle Analysis demonstrating environmental impact improvements

Expected outcomes include:
- Demonstrated recycling technologies at pilot or industrial scale
- Improved material recovery rates from mixed textile waste
- Reduced textile waste going to landfill or incineration
- New products incorporating recycled blended textile fibres
- Contribution to EU Textiles Strategy and circular economy objectives
