Advanced Digital Skills for AI Uptake in Health

Programme: Digital Europe Programme (2021-2027)
Type of Action: DIGITAL-LS (Lump Sum Grants)
Deadline: 2026-10-02

- Available budget: 7,800,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 2

Keywords: Artificial intelligence, Computer sciences, Continuing professional training, Curriculum design, Design of innovative master programmes, Digital Services and Platforms, Education, Education and Training, Health and Ecosystem Services, Health data, Health sciences, IT skills, Learning outcomes, Master's programmes, Personal development, Teaching materials, Technological innovation, Training, Advanced Digital Skills, Cross-Sectoral Collaboration, Digital Health Education, European Health Data Space, Health Workforce Development, Healthcare professionals, STEP-Digital/deep tech

Description:
This topic aims to expand the offer of education and training in Artificial Intelligence in health, jointly designed between higher education/training institutions, research organisations, and industry. The actions tackle the lack of academic training in advanced digital skills in key digital areas while triggering new ways of delivering education programmes and building partnerships across the EU.

Content requirements:
- Reflect latest policy developments including the Apply AI Strategy and European Health Data Space
- Cover developments in and application of AI and related digital health technologies
- Target audience: healthcare professionals, computer/data scientists, programmers, software developers in the healthcare sector
- Training designs should reflect varying depth and technical complexity for different expertise levels and learning preferences

Key requirements:
- Design and deliver trainings in cooperation with the "European network of AI-powered advanced screening centres"
- Training courses available quarterly to network members
- Sessions and materials available in English and other EU languages
- Catalogue adapted regularly based on audience feedback
- Foster use of HealthData@EU infrastructure and other European health data infrastructures
- Explore synergies with EU4Health and Erasmus+ funded digital skills training for health workforce
- Build on relevant education and training activities from existing EU programmes

Expected outcomes:
- Initiatives to collect knowledge on learning needs in AI for health
- Training programmes designed jointly by higher education/VET/research organisations and businesses
- Training catalogue with detailed planning and timetable
- Final analysis of completed training and achievement levels
- Landing page integrated into the Digital Skills and Jobs Platform
- Strengthened European capacity in AI for health across multiple member states

Regulatory and strategic context:
- Apply AI Strategy (European Commission)
- European Health Data Space (EHDS) - Regulation (EU) 2025/327
- HealthData@EU infrastructure
- Digital Skills and Jobs Platform
