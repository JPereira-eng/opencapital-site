HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-61
Scientific analysis and exploitation of space data

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 3,920,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 2
- Min Contribution: 1,500,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 2,500,000 EUR

DEADLINE: June 3, 2026
OPENING DATE: April 10, 2026

DESCRIPTION:
Projects must analyze and exploit data from scientific/exploration instruments in pre-operative, operative, post-operative or data exploitation phases, focusing on astronomy, astrophysics, cosmology, heliophysics, deep space and solar system exploration. Data sources include ESA missions, national space agencies, research organizations and universities - excluding Earth observation missions.

Advanced data processing techniques, artificial intelligence, statistical approaches, multidimensional data fusion and novel visualization methods are encouraged. Data combination with international missions and ground-based infrastructure is supported to maximize scientific return and enable new research using existing datasets.

Projects should generate scientific publications and higher-level data products suitable for ESA Space Science archives. International cooperation is promoted, particularly with space exploration-active nations. Gender dimension integration applies when relevant to research objectives.

TRL TARGET: Activities expected to achieve TRL 4 by project end (ISO 16290:2013 standards).
