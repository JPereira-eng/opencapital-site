PROGRAMME DIVISION
Horizon Europe (HORIZON) > Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness (HORIZON.2) > Climate, Energy and Mobility (HORIZON.2.5)

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Eligible Countries:
All EU Member States and Associated Countries; however, the Hydrogen Valley must be geographically located in "EU-13" countries (Cyprus, Czechia, Estonia, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Poland, Slovakia, Slovenia, Bulgaria, Romania, and Croatia).

Other Eligibility Requirements:
- At least one partner in the consortium must be a member of either Hydrogen Europe or Hydrogen Europe Research
- Maximum funding request: 8,000,000 EUR
- Subcontracted work must be performed in target countries (EU Member States and Associated Countries)

EXPECTED OUTCOMES
Projects should deliver:
- Anchored demand for renewable hydrogen in new markets
- Interaction and synergies among initial testbeds
- Full integration into the broader cross-sectoral energy ecosystem
- Enhanced public perception through high visibility of the project and associated technologies to the local public and EU citizens
- Expanded Hydrogen Valley deployment across EU-13 countries
- Knowledge dissemination enabling replication of hydrogen valley models

SCOPE
Proposals must demonstrate a small-scale Hydrogen Valley combining hydrogen production, distribution, and end-use technologies. Key requirements include:

- Production of minimum 400 tonnes clean hydrogen annually from new capacity
- At least two years of operational monitoring and assessment
- Professional and ambitious communication plan to ensure high visibility to the public
- Sector coupling demonstration (energy, industry, transport applications)
- Carbon emissions reduction compared to incumbent technologies
- Financial viability pathway beyond two-year operation period
- Preliminary hydrogen safety planning and management plan at the project level

Technology Requirements:
- Technology Readiness Level (TRL): minimum 6 at project start; target TRL 8 at completion
- State-of-the-art technologies following Clean Hydrogen Partnership-funded developments

CONDITIONS AND ELIGIBILITY

Legal and Financial Setup:
- Equipment, infrastructure, and assets purchased specifically for the action may be declared as full capitalised costs (exceptional provision for demonstration projects)
- Renewable energy plant costs and operational expenses (e.g., electricity) are not eligible
- Guarantees of Origin (GOs) or equivalent certificates (e.g., CertifHy) required to prove renewable hydrogen character

Additional Obligations:
- Evidence of commitment from public authorities and stakeholders through Letters of Intent
- Scalability analysis demonstrating how the valley may expand
- Collaboration with projects from HORIZON-JU-CLEANH2-2025-05-03 on safety and permitting training
- Demonstration of EU competitiveness and industrial leadership
- Replicability and cross-border cooperation framework
- Synergies with existing hydrogen demonstrations

Submission Requirements:
- Application form page limit: 70 pages (Innovation Actions)
- Detailed budget justification in lump-sum Excel table for costs in categories C and/or D
- Implementation timeline with defined phases (preparation, manufacturing, permitting, deployment, operation)
- Comprehensive funding plan identifying all co-funding sources
