Timeline

Description and Objectives
Expected Impact: The initiative aims to boost public awareness of the potential, importance and contribution of women to the EU innovation ecosystem and cultivate role models encouraging women to pursue innovation careers.

Scope: This recognition prize celebrates female entrepreneurs who have commercialised transformative innovations across the EU and associated nations. It promotes organisational cultures enabling women to become tomorrow's innovators and entrepreneurs.

The prize specifically recognises women who have:
- Founded or co-founded a company based on a scientific or technological innovation
- Successfully brought innovative products or services to market
- Demonstrated significant social or economic impact through their innovation
- Contributed to the development of the European innovation ecosystem

Prize Categories and Criteria
Applicants must have transformed ideas into disruptive innovations benefiting society and the environment. The prize honours women who demonstrate exceptional entrepreneurial achievement and serve as role models for the next generation.

Evaluation focuses on scientific/technological innovation quality, commercial success and market impact, contribution to European innovation ecosystem, and potential for inspiring future women innovators.

Eligibility
Geographic Scope: Women innovators from EU member states and countries associated with Horizon Europe
Key Requirements: Applicants must have transformed ideas into disruptive innovations benefiting society and environment
Eligibility conditions detailed in Annex B of the Work Programme General Annexes
