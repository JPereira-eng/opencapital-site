DESCRICAO
The initiative aims to increase awareness of Union sustainable agriculture practices among consumers, emphasizing benefits for climate, environment, and animal welfare. It seeks to enhance the competitiveness and consumption of sustainably produced agri-food products in the Union, raise their profile and increase their market share. Promoted products must cover at least two action areas per EU Regulation 2021/2115 Article 31(4).
