THEMATIC FOCUS
--------------
This focused two-stage call addresses climate sciences and responses for the transformation towards climate neutrality, specifically targeting evidence-based understanding of climate change impacts, adaptation effectiveness, and policy-relevant scientific outputs.

KNOWN TOPIC
-----------
HORIZON-CL5-2025-05-Two-Stage-D1-05: Adaptation to Climate Change - Effectiveness and Limits

This topic investigates:
- The effectiveness of climate adaptation measures across different sectors and geographies
- Physical, biological and social limits to adaptation
- Maladaptation risks and how to avoid them
- Evidence base for transformation pathways and adaptive governance
- Integration of climate risk assessment into policy and planning frameworks
- Climate services and decision-support tools for adaptation

RESEARCH AREAS
--------------
- Climate change impacts quantification across systems (water, food, ecosystems, cities, human health)
- Adaptation effectiveness assessment methodologies
- Hard and soft limits to climate adaptation
- Compound climate risks and cascading effects
- Nature-based solutions effectiveness for adaptation
- Socio-economic cost-benefit analysis of adaptation investments
- Climate projections and uncertainty communication
