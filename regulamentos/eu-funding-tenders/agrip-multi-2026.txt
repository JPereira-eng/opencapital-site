AGRIP-MULTI-2026 - Call for proposals for Multi programmes 2026 - Promotion of agricultural products

Call Identifier: AGRIP-MULTI-2026
Topic: AGRIP-MULTI-2026-IM
Programme: Promotion of Agricultural Products (AGRIP2027)
Status: Open

BUDGET
Total available funding for 2026:
- AGRIP-MULTI-2026-IM: 7.000.000 EUR
- AGRIP-MULTI-2026-IM-ORGANIC: 10.600.000 EUR
- AGRIP-MULTI-2026-IM-SUST: 13.700.000 EUR

DEADLINE
Single-stage submission deadline: 15 de maio de 2026

DESCRICAO
Proposals target awareness of EU quality schemes (PDO, PGI, TSG) and specific features of agricultural methods, particularly in terms of food safety, traceability, animal welfare, respect for the environment and sustainability.

ELEGIBILIDADE E CONDICOES
Detailed eligibility criteria and conditions are described in the call fiche. Requirements address proposal formatting, eligible countries, evaluation criteria, and award processes.

Model Grant Agreement: AGRIP Action Grant Budget-Based

PALAVRAS-CHAVE
Quality schemes, agricultural exports, marketing, promotion, organic products, sustainability, food safety, EU funding
