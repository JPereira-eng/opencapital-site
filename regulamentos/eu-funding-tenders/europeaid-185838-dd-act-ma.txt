EuropeAid/185838/DD/ACT/MA - Call for Proposals (NDICI Global Europe - Morocco)

Title: Promotion des droits de l'Homme et Renforcement des Organisations de la societe civile au Maroc 2026
Reference: EuropeAid/185838/DD/ACT/MA
Programme: Neighbourhood, Development and International Cooperation Instrument (NDICI) - Global Europe
Source: EU Funding & Tenders Portal + EU Delegation Morocco announcement
Original call URL: https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/prospect/external/publishedcalls.htm?callId=185838

OVERVIEW
The EU Delegation to Morocco has launched a call for proposals to strengthen Moroccan civil society organisations (CSOs) and to promote and protect human rights and democracy in Morocco. The call follows the structure of past Morocco NDICI calls (predecessor reference EuropeAid/173863) and is funded with an indicative envelope of EUR 5.8 million.

KEY DEADLINES AND CALENDAR
- Concept note submission deadline: 2 June 2026.
- Information session: to be announced on the EU Delegation Morocco website and EEAS pages.
- Full application deadline: only invited concept-note shortlistees, after evaluation.

INDICATIVE BUDGET
Total envelope: approximately EUR 5,800,000 across multiple lots, split between Human Rights / Democracy and CSO strengthening priorities.

LOTS / FUNDING STREAMS (TEMPLATE FROM PRIOR EUROPEAID MOROCCO CALLS)
- Lot 1 (EIDHR / Human Rights line): grant range typically EUR 100,000 - EUR 300,000. Promotion and defense of human rights and good governance. Eligible organisations: NGOs, foundations, think tanks, national human rights institutions.
- Lot 2 (CSO / Civil Society line): grant range typically EUR 300,000 - EUR 500,000. Equality, combating violence against women, youth participation, civic engagement.
- Lot 3 (ENI / Bilateral line): grant range typically EUR 250,000 - EUR 300,000. Tripartite dialogue on human rights between government, civil society and EU.

ELIGIBILITY (LEAD APPLICANTS)
- Non-profit legal entities: NGOs, civil society organisations, foundations, networks/platforms, independent research centers, think tanks, national human rights institutions.
- Established in: Morocco, EU 27 Member States, EEA countries, beneficiaries listed in Regulation (EU) 236/2014, OECD/DAC list of developing countries.
- Cannot apply as a natural person.
- Co-applicants: at least one usually required, partnerships with Moroccan local actors strongly encouraged.

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES
- Strengthening CSOs as independent governance actors and watchdogs.
- Defending human rights defenders and at-risk groups (women, minorities, migrants).
- Supporting freedom of expression, association and peaceful assembly.
- Promoting equality and combating discrimination (gender, minorities, persons with disabilities).
- Supporting transitional justice, accountability and the rule of law.
- Civic education, monitoring, advocacy and policy engagement.
- Combating violence against women and supporting youth participation in democratic processes.

ELIGIBLE COSTS
Standard NDICI Global Europe rules apply: staff, travel, equipment and supplies, local office, costs of services, sub-granting where allowed, audit, visibility/communication, indirect costs (flat rate up to 7%).

CO-FINANCING
EU contribution typically up to 95% of eligible costs, applicants must contribute at least 5% from own or other non-EU sources.

PROJECT DURATION
Typically 24-48 months under NDICI Morocco template.

APPLICATION PROCESS
- Online submission via the PROSPECT platform of the EU Funding & Tenders Portal is mandatory.
- Two-stage process: concept note first, then full application for those invited to second stage.
- Languages accepted: French, English, Arabic for Morocco-context calls.

EVALUATION CRITERIA (TEMPLATE)
Concept notes: relevance (40 points), design (20), effectiveness/feasibility (20), sustainability (10), cost-effectiveness (10).
Full applications: methodology, partnership, capacity, budget realism.

CONTACTS
EU Delegation to Morocco, Rabat. Specific call mailbox to be confirmed on EEAS Morocco website. Reference EuropeAid/185838/DD/ACT/MA in all correspondence.

DOCUMENTS
Full Guidelines for Applicants, annexes (concept note template, full application template, budget, logical framework) available via EU Funding & Tenders Portal under reference 185838.

NOTES
This is a recurrent call line for Morocco under the NDICI Global Europe Country Allocation. The 2026 cycle follows the pattern set by EuropeAid/173863/DD/ACT/MA (2022, EUR 5.63 million, 3 lots) with refreshed lot envelopes and updated thematic priorities aligned with the EU Action Plan on Human Rights and Democracy 2025-2027.
