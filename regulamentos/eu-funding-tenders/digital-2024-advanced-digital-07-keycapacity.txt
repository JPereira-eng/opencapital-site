DIGITAL-2024-ADVANCED-DIGITAL-07-KEYCAPACITY
Specialised Education Programmes in Key Capacity Areas

Identifier: DIGITAL-2024-ADVANCED-DIGITAL-07-KEYCAPACITY
Call Identifier: DIGITAL-2024-ADVANCED-DIGITAL-07
Framework Programme: Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)
Status: Closed
Deadline Date: November 21, 2024
Budget Overview in EUR: 55,000,000 (2024)
Programme Period: 2021-2027

Description:
This initiative supports consortia in designing and delivering advanced digital skills education programmes and modules across key technological areas including virtual worlds, edge computing, quantum computing, photonics, robotics, and automatization. Additionally, it targets interdisciplinary green digital skills in strategic sectors (agriculture, transport, renewable energy, waste management) and government/public sector digital training. The programs aim to address persistent ICT specialist shortages across the EU, with minimum enrollment requirements of 150 students across participating institutions over the 48-month project duration.

Scope: Two complementary actions cover (a) cutting-edge advanced digital skills programmes in key capacity areas (virtual worlds, edge computing, quantum, photonics, robotics, automatization, plus interdisciplinary fields like green digital, digital agriculture, transport, energy, waste); and (b) targeted upskilling and reskilling programmes for the public sector, including modular certificates and micro-credentials.

Eligibility:
Consortia must include higher education institutions, vocational education and training providers, research organisations, businesses, and public sector entities. Cross-border partnerships strongly encouraged. Minimum 150 students enrolled across the 48-month duration.

Keywords: Advanced computing, artificial intelligence, big data, cloud services, data analytics, machine learning, Internet of Things, quantum technologies, robotics, virtual reality, higher education, vocational training, semiconductors, edge computing, microelectronics.

Conditions: Eligibility criteria, admissibility requirements, and operational conditions are detailed in the call document sections 5-10, covering country eligibility, financial capacity, evaluation procedures, award criteria, and legal/financial arrangements.

Despesas elegiveis: pessoal docente e tecnico, desenvolvimento curricular, infraestrutura digital de ensino, bolsas de estudantes, mobilidade, dissemination, gestao de projeto. Modelo de financiamento: lump sum (Digital Simple Grants).

Criterios de selecao: relevancia para prioridades do Digital Europe Programme, qualidade pedagogica, impacto esperado em escala UE, capacidade do consortium, sustentabilidade post-projeto, integracao com industria.

Actions: Single-stage submission procedure with lump sum grant mechanism (DIGITAL-SIMPLE Digital Simple Grants Budget-Based). Status: Closed.
