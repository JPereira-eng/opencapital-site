## Description Summary
This Innovation Action targets industrial-scale production and commercialization of nutritional food ingredients from alternative sources. Projects must demonstrate Technology Readiness Level 8 for efficient ingredient production (proteins, lipids, specialty carbohydrates, fibres) and validate their application in food products at TRL 6+.

## Key Expected Outcomes
- Full industrial-scale biorefineries for nutritional ingredient production.
- Enhanced EU resilience through diversified ingredient sourcing.
- Improved environmental sustainability in food sectors.
- Strengthened consumer acceptance of alternative-source ingredients.

## Scope Requirements

In Scope:
- Production of proteins, lipids, specialty carbohydrates, and fibres from bio-based feedstock.
- Co-production of multiple food/feed ingredients following cascading approaches.
- Industrial-grade feedstock conversion from agricultural crops.
- Circular economy and resource efficiency strategies.

Out of Scope:
- Direct food production from crops, livestock, fisheries, aquaculture.
- Structural/functional ingredients as primary products (colourants, preservatives, stabilizers).

## Expected Activities
- Demonstrate efficient production processes at commercial scale.
- Validate ingredients in formulated food products with quality/sensorial verification.
- Assess safety per EU regulatory requirements and EFSA guidance.
- Identify regulatory gaps affecting food companies.
- Engage multi-actor approach involving consumers and end-users.
- Ensure complementarity with existing funded projects.
