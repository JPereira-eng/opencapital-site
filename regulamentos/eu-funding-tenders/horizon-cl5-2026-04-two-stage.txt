HORIZON-CL5-2026-04-Two-Stage - Cluster 5 Climate Energy Mobility Call 2026
===========================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL5-2026-04-Two-Stage
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe
CLUSTER: Cluster 5 - Climate, Energy and Mobility
MANAGING AGENCY: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)
STATUS: Open (Two-stage call)

DEADLINES
---------
Stage 1 (Outline Application): 31 March 2026
Stage 2 (Full Proposal): 20 October 2026

FINANCIAL DETAILS
-----------------
Total call budget: Part of over EUR 466 million available for Cluster 5 in 2026 (WP 2026-2027)

THEMATIC FOCUS
--------------
This two-stage call focuses on Cluster 5 priorities under the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2026-2027, covering climate, energy and mobility research and innovation, in particular sustainable, secure and competitive energy supply solutions.

KNOWN TOPIC EXAMPLES
---------------------
HORIZON-CL5-2026-04-Two-Stage-D3-02: Industrial and research partners sought for plasma-enabled, electricity-driven production of ammonia and urea from air and captured CO2. Focuses on modular, fully electric routes for sustainable ammonia and urea production, combining non-thermal plasma (air-to-NOx) with electrochemical reduction (NOx-to-NH3; NOx/CO2-to-urea).

ELIGIBLE PROJECT TYPES
-----------------------
Research and Innovation Actions (RIA), Innovation Actions (IA), and Coordination and Support Actions (CSA) as specified per topic.

RESEARCH AREAS COVERED (Cluster 5 scope)
-----------------------------------------
- Sustainable, secure and competitive energy supply
- Renewable energy technologies (solar, wind, geothermal, ocean)
- Next generation energy storage systems
- Smart energy systems and grids
- Hydrogen and power-to-X technologies
- Decarbonisation of energy-intensive industries
- Cross-sectoral solutions for climate transition
- Clean transport across all modes

TWO-STAGE PROCESS
-----------------
Stage 1: Outline application (max 10 pages), evaluated on Excellence and Impact only.
Successful applicants invited to Stage 2 full proposal.
Stage 2: Full proposal evaluated on Excellence, Impact, and Implementation.

ELIGIBLE COUNTRIES
------------------
All EU Member States plus Horizon Europe associated countries (including Norway, Iceland, Israel, and others). Third country participation possible under specific conditions.
