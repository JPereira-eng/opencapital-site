CALL: HORIZON-WIDERA-2025-GENDER-Prize
NAME: EU Award for Gender Equality Champions (2025 edition)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - Widening Participation and Strengthening ERA
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
Since 2022, the EU Gender Equality Champions in Research and Innovation are celebrated through this Horizon Europe prize. The EU Award for Gender Equality Champions awards outstanding achievements reached through the implementation of Gender Equality Plans (GEP) by universities, higher education institutions, and other research performing organisations (public or private), established in an EU Member State or a Third Country associated to Horizon Europe.

OBJECTIVES:
- Complement the Horizon Europe requirement that higher education and research organisations applying for funding have a GEP as an eligibility criterion
- Be an incentive to advance inclusive gender equality plans and policies within the European Research Area (ERA) policy agenda
- Act as an enabler for the transformation agenda for universities in line with the new European Strategy for Universities

PRIZE CATEGORIES (3 categories):
1. Sustainable Gender Equality Champions: organisations demonstrating a significant and sustained record of activity and a high level of achievement through their GEP implementation.
2. Newcomer Gender Equality Champions: organisations that recently finalised the implementation of their first GEP and can demonstrate the most progress achieved through its implementation.
3. Inclusive Gender Equality Champions: organisations demonstrating inclusive approaches to gender equality in research and innovation.

PRIZE:
Each winner in each category receives a trophy and a EUR 100,000 prize.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Open to universities, higher education institutions, and other research performing organisations (public or private)
- Must be established in an EU Member State or a Third Country associated to Horizon Europe
- Applicants can only apply to one of the three categories
- Winners from past editions cannot re-apply to another category

TIMELINE 2025:
- Call for applications open: 7 May 2025
- Application deadline: 18 June 2025, 17:00
- Evaluation and winner selection: second half of 2025
