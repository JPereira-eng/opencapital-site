# HORIZON-JU-CBE-2026-RIA-02 Funding Call Summary

## Basic Information
- Title: SSbD bio-based polymers from alternative sources
- Call Identifier: HORIZON-JU-CBE-2026
- Topic Identifier: HORIZON-JU-CBE-2026-RIA-02
- Type of Action: HORIZON JU Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
- Model Grant Agreement: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based

## Budget Overview
- Total Budget (2026): EUR 170,760,699
- Expected Grants: 2
- Contribution per Grant: EUR 3,250,000 (minimum and maximum)

## Timeline
- Opening Date: April 23, 2026
- Submission Deadline: May 19, 2026

## Keywords
Applied and industrial chemistry; Materials engineering; Polymer chemistry; Polymers and plastics; Alternative feedstock sources; Bio-based polymers; Circular-by-design; Polymer synthesis; Recycling; Safe and sustainable by design.

## Expected Outcomes
Projects should deliver:
- Sustainable feedstock diversification for bio-based polymer production.
- Scalable processes achieving SSbD bio-based polymers from non-primary biomass alternatives.
- Expanded portfolio of high-performance bio-based polymers for new market applications.

## Scope & Required Activities

Development Requirements:
- Develop (at TRL 5) efficient processes for synthesis and/or extraction of bio-based polymer(s) with high yield and selectivity.
- Pretreatment/fractionation of feedstock where applicable.
- Both novel and established polymers (from alternative sources) are eligible; primary agricultural/forestry biomass excluded.

Testing & Validation:
- Test suitability in circular-by-design applications across minimum two market sectors (TRL 4+).
- Validate technical performance and market requirement compliance.
- Test end-of-life options including recycling, biodegradation, reuse, and remanufacturing per existing standards.

Multi-Actor Requirements:
- Involve feedstock providers, waste management operators, end-users, and consumers.
- Apply Safe and Sustainable by Design (SSbD) framework for assessment.
- Ensure complementarity with past/ongoing Horizon 2020, Horizon Europe, and CBE JU projects.

## Eligibility

General Requirements:
- Participants must meet legal entity validation and financial capacity standards.
- Eligible countries defined in Annex B of Work Programme General Annexes.
- Specific eligibility conditions apply per Annex B.

Exclusion: Organizations subject to EU exclusion criteria are ineligible.

## Evaluation Criteria

Standard evaluation applies using Standard evaluation form (HE RIA, IA) with scoring and thresholds detailed in Annex D of Work Programme General Annexes. Evaluation covers excellence, implementation quality, and impact potential.

## Key Conditions
- Proposal page limits and layout per Annex A and E.
- Single-stage submission procedure.
- Admissibility and evaluation processes per Annex F.
- Grant agreement terms per Annex G of Work Programme General Annexes.
