# HORIZON-INFRA-2027-DEV-01-02 Funding Topic

**Title:** Consolidation of the research infrastructure landscape – development of complementarities, synergies and/or integration between a set of pan-European research infrastructures

**Identifier:** HORIZON-INFRA-2027-DEV-01-02

**Call Identifier:** HORIZON-INFRA-2027-01

**Call Title:** Research Infrastructures 2027

---

## Description

The topic seeks to strengthen Europe's research infrastructure landscape through developing complementarities, synergies and potential integration among multiple ESFRI Landmarks, European Research Infrastructure Consortia (ERICs), or other European research infrastructures of European interest.

Projects may pursue operational integration to optimize functioning and prevent duplication through service-sharing mechanisms, or examine feasibility of merging infrastructures. When addressing operational integration, proposals develop complementarities at thematic or cross-disciplinary levels, including shared horizontal services, joint scientific services, and staff skill development. When addressing merging, proposals must involve funders, develop comprehensive merger concepts covering governance and legal structure, and provide financial forecasts.

---

## Expected Outcomes

- Structured, integrated and strengthened European research infrastructure landscape
- Solid concepts and comprehensive plans supporting decision-making at national and European levels
- Increased operational and financial efficiency
- Enhanced synergies between research infrastructures
- Greater capacity to address EU policy priorities and support EU industry
- Reinforced global competitiveness
