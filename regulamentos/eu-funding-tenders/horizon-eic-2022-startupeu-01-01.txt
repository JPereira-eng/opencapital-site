Scope and Objectives
The initiative aims to support scaling up of companies in Europe by building connections between the EIC and broader European startup networks. Key objectives include:

- Targeting digital and deep tech startups receiving EIC support for international expansion
- Supporting deep tech startups not yet backed by EIC but with existing private or EU funding
- Connecting local digital and deep tech startup ecosystems and enabling cross-border acceleration
- Ensuring integration with Digital Europe Programme and national startup programs
- Supporting access to innovation procurement opportunities
- Facilitating matchmaking between startups and corporate partners, investors, and public procurers

Expected Outcomes
The action should contribute to building a more integrated and supportive European startup ecosystem. Activities include organising networking events and matchmaking opportunities, providing information and coaching services to startups, facilitating access to markets and investors, and supporting the internationalisation of European deep tech companies.

Three funded projects should together create a comprehensive support network covering all regions of Europe, with particular attention to emerging startup ecosystems in less developed regions.
