INSTRUMENTO: SNS JU Call for Proposals 2025 - Stream D (Advanced 5G/6G Trials in Vertical Sectors)
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-JU-SNS-2025-02
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - Smart Networks and Services Joint Undertaking (SNS JU)
AGENCIA: SNS JU
STATUS: Closed (deadline 18 September 2025)

BUDGET: EUR 24,000,000 (Stream D) | EUR 128,000,000 total 2025 investment (Streams B, C, D)
DEADLINE: 18 September 2025, 17:00 CET
ABERTURA: 18 June 2025
INFO DAY: 23 May 2025

OBJETIVO:
Support advanced 5G and 6G trials in key vertical sectors. Co-develop and demonstrate solutions that address concrete adoption barriers for 5G Advanced and 6G technologies, validating these in real operational environments and exploring viable business models.

SETORES VERTICAIS ALVO (Stream D):
1. Industry and Manufacturing - advanced 5G/6G industrial applications
2. Media - immersive and real-time media production and distribution
3. Transport and Logistics - connected and autonomous mobility
4. Emergency and Safety Services - critical communications and public safety
5. Healthcare - telemedicine, remote monitoring, connected care

CONTEXTO:
This is the 4th SNS JU call, following successful implementation of previous calls that have allocated more than EUR 500 million in EU public investment to boost Advanced 5G and 6G research and innovation across Europe.

Outcomes from earlier calls include EUR 130 million boost for 6G research and EUR 250 million for 35 new projects.

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Entities established in EU Member States and SNS JU member states. Consortia required. Details at: https://smart-networks.europa.eu/current-call-for-proposals/

COMO CANDIDATAR:
Applications through the EU Funding and Tenders Portal. Details on SNS JU website.
