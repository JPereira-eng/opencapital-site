CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-MARKETNET
TITLE: Markets and Networking
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Project Grants (CREA-PJG) - CREA Action Grant Budget-Based
BUDGET: 16,500,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 20 January 2026, 17:00 CET (closed - 105 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Evaluation results expected July 2026; grant agreement signature October 2026.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative aims to enhance promotion of European content globally and foster cooperation across the audiovisual value chain. Specific goals include:
- Improving innovative aspects of European industry markets and systemic impact
- Encouraging creation of professional networks and market cooperation
- Supporting co-creation of competitive European series
- Promoting sustainability and diversity in markets and networks
- Enhancing competitiveness and international circulation of European audiovisual works

SCOPE:
Markets: Physical, digital, or hybrid industry events for B2B exchanges among professionals, facilitating co-productions and sales. Activities should address current trends including videogames, VR, XR, AI, cross-media and thematic genres.

Networking: Cross-value-chain activities promoting European works through business-to-business support, best practice exchanges, or series co-creation initiatives.

ELIGIBILITY:
Applicants must meet admissibility conditions, operate from eligible countries, demonstrate financial and operational capacity, and avoid exclusion grounds. Detailed eligibility criteria are specified in the official call document.
