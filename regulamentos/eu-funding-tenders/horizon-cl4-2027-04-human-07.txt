## Complete Description

Expected Outcome:

Proposals should contribute to: increasing European expert participation and influence in digital standardisation to strengthen EU competitiveness; improving skills of European experts from SMEs, research institutions, open-source communities, academia and societal stakeholders in standards development; developing standardisation landscapes and gap analyses for key digital technologies per the Rolling Plan for ICT Standardisation; providing foresight analyses on emerging technology standardisation to identify short and medium-term needs; and raising awareness of standardisation benefits, particularly for SMEs and researchers, with greater visibility of Europe's digital standardisation ecosystem.

Scope:

Implementation should support the EU Standardisation Strategy and related policy frameworks including Europe's Digital Decade, Apply AI Strategy, and the Competitiveness Compass, emphasizing Europe's global standards-setting leadership in key digital technologies.

Goals encompass strengthening EU digital competitiveness, contributing to EU technological sovereignty, and promoting EU values internationally by empowering and financially supporting European stakeholder participation in international digital standards development.

The initiative targets reinforcing EU and associated country expert presence in global digital standards, particularly from SMEs, research institutions, open-source communities, academia, and societal organizations.

Required Activities:

Proposals must include: establishing a management facility supporting European specialist contributions and leadership in global digital standardisation activities; financially supporting international standardisation meetings hosting in Europe when relevant; conducting landscape and gap analyses of standardisation activities in priority digital areas; and promoting digital standardisation relevance and benefits through tools, materials, and educational initiatives on standardisation.

Beneficiaries intending to transfer ownership or grant exclusive licenses must notify the granting authority before transfer, which may object within four years post-action completion.

Proposals should build upon previous activities from ICT-40-2017, ICT-45-2020, HORIZON-CL4-2022-RESILIENCE-01-21, and HORIZON-CL4-2024-HUMAN-03-04 initiatives, referencing www.standict.eu.

## Conditions Summary

Admissibility: Proposal page limits and layout per Annex A and E of Horizon Europe Work Programme General Annexes; specific requirements in Application Form Part B.

Eligible Countries: Per Annex B; select non-EU/non-Associated Countries with specific funding provisions detailed in the Horizon Europe Programme Guide.

Evaluation Criteria: Award criteria and scoring thresholds detailed in Annex D of Work Programme General Annexes.
