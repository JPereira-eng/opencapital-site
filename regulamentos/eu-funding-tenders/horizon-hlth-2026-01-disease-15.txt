Description:
This Coordination and Support Action (CSA) aims to advance cardiovascular disease prevention and management across the EU. The initiative requires applicants to conduct comprehensive reviews of existing research and innovations, identify gaps particularly regarding sex and gender disparities, and develop a Strategic Research and Innovation Agenda (SRIA) on cardiovascular diseases and associated comorbidities like obesity and diabetes.

Key requirements include:
- Conduct a comprehensive review at national, EU, and international levels of existing cardiovascular research to identify implementation gaps
- Develop barrier analysis with recommendations for personalised prediction and screening
- Create detailed SRIA with stakeholder validation pathways
- Integrate sex/gender variables, age, racial/ethnic origin, and socioeconomic factors into research design
- Organise multi-sectorial events and develop standardised screening protocols
- Establish dissemination strategies using digital platforms

The scope emphasises that CVDs are the main cause of death in the EU, with over 1.7 million deaths annually, and addresses the need to translate innovations into implementable protocols. The CSA is the upstream reflection layer that should feed into future RIA and IA topics on cardiovascular health under the next Horizon Europe Work Programme cycles.
