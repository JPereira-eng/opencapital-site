INSTRUMENTO: EU Space Research Call 2025 (Cluster 4 - Space/HADEA)
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2025-02
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - Cluster 4: Digital, Industry and Space (Destination 5: Space)
AGENCIA: HaDEA (European Health and Digital Executive Agency)
STATUS: Closed - 27 proposals selected

BUDGET: EUR 138,600,000 (27 proposals selected)
DEADLINE: 25 September 2025, 17:00 CEST
PUBLICACAO: 15 May 2025
ABERTURA: 22 May 2025
MODELO: Single-stage submission
TOTAL PROPOSTAS SUBMETIDAS: 93

OBJETIVO:
Advance European leadership in the space sector through research and innovation in satellite telecommunications, Earth Observation, space transportation and Copernicus services. Part of Cluster 4 (Digital, Industry and Space), Destination 5: A Space-active Europe.

TOPICOS (20 topics):
- Digital solutions for autonomy for space transportation systems
- Digital enablers and building blocks for Earth Observation and Satellite telecommunication for Space solutions
- Copernicus service evolutions and next-generation Earth Observation capabilities
- Space debris mitigation and remediation technologies
- Satellite communication technologies for next generation connectivity
- In-orbit servicing, assembly and manufacturing
- Space weather monitoring and prediction
- Additional topics covering space data infrastructure, navigation, and space applications

RESULTADOS:
- 93 proposals submitted (deadline: September 2025)
- 27 proposals selected for funding (EUR 138.6 million)
- Grant agreements to be signed by end of May 2026

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Entities established in EU Member States and Horizon Europe Associated Countries. Research organisations, companies (including SMEs), higher education institutions, space agencies. Full details in the Horizon Europe 2025 Work Programme.

COMO CANDIDATAR:
All application details available on the EU Funding and Tenders Portal and HaDEA website.
