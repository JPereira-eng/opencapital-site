CALL IDENTIFIER: DIGITAL-2026-AI-DATA-10
TITLE: AI Continent - Digital Europe Programme 2026
FRAMEWORK: Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)
SOURCE: eu-funding-tenders

BUDGET

- Total call budget: EUR 8,500,000
- Grant per project: EUR 2,000,000 - 5,000,000
- Funding rate: 50% of eligible costs (lump sum grant)

DEADLINE

October 1, 2026 | Status: Open

OBJECTIVES

- Support projects testing digital solutions for EU legislative compliance information transmission
- Deploy advanced technologies ensuring data security, confidentiality, and regulatory compliance
- Identify and target sectors with substantial administrative burden: agriculture, environment, manufacturing, healthcare, energy
- Bridge the gap between digital technology research and market deployment
- Contribute to Europe's AI Continent Action Plan

DESCRIPTION

The call is part of the AI Continent initiative under the Digital Europe Programme. It aims to support projects testing digital solutions for transmitting information relevant for compliance with EU legislation, leveraging advanced technologies to ensure data security, confidentiality, and regulatory compliance.

Target sectors with significant administrative burden include agriculture, environment, manufacturing, healthcare and energy. Projects must demonstrate market-readiness and scalability of AI and data solutions.

ELIGIBILITY

- Legal entities (public or private) established in EU Member States, EEA countries, or Digital Europe Programme-associated nations
- Eligible beneficiaries: large companies (>250 employees), research centres and universities, SMEs and start-ups (<249 employees), state administration and affiliated entities
- Minimum consortium of 3 independent applicants from 3 different eligible countries
- Prior registration in the Participant Register required

KEY CONDITIONS

- Project duration: 24 months (extensible with justification)
- Applicants must be established in eligible countries
- Affiliated entities cannot count toward the minimum 3-applicant requirement
- Participants from non-EU countries must be from listed EEA countries or countries associated with the Digital Europe Programme
- Beneficiaries and affiliated entities must register in the Participant Register before submitting the proposal

PROGRAMME CONTEXT

Part of Europe's AI Continent Action Plan, managed by the European Health and Digital Executive Agency (HaDEA). Supports the Digital Europe Programme's goal of accelerating AI deployment across key industrial sectors.
