INSTRUMENTO: Supporting the implementation of the Soil Deal for Europe Mission
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-MISS-2025-05
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - EU Missions (Soil Deal for Europe)
AGENCIA: CINEA / REA
STATUS: Closed (deadline passed September-October 2025)

BUDGET: EUR 10,000,000 (componente soil resilience) - dotacao total da call superior
DEADLINE: 30 September 2025 (single-stage topics)
NUMERO DE TOPICOS: 11 topics (single-stage e two-stage)

MISSAO:
The EU Mission A Soil Deal for Europe aims to ensure that 75% of EU soils are healthy by 2030. Funding supports research, innovation and demonstration to protect and restore soils across Europe.

TOPICOS (HORIZON-MISS-2025-05 - 11 topics):
1. Living Labs for soil remediation and green redevelopment of brownfields
2. Social, economic and cultural drivers of land degradation
3. Soil-water nexus management
4. Transfer functions for the Soil Monitoring Law
5. EU global footprint on soils
6. Impact of farming practices on soil health in arable lands
7. Land suitability for soil health and sustainable biomass production
8. Science-policy interfaces and national soil-health hubs
9. Citizen engagement for sustainable land management
10. Carbon farming and emissions reductions network
11. Additional topics on soil salinity and quantitative methods

ESTRUTURA DAS CANDIDATURAS:
- Single-stage: applicants submit final proposals by the deadline
- Two-stage: applicants submit outline applications (maximum 10 pages) evaluated on Excellence and Impact; successful applicants invited to submit full proposals

NOTA: A call HORIZON-MISS-2025-05-two-stage (Living Labs e saude do solo) e uma componente separada desta call mais alargada.
