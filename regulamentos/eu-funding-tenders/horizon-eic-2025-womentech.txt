INSTRUMENTO: Women Tech.EU initiative
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-EIC-2025-WOMENTECH
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
DIVISAO: HORIZON.3.1.3 - Additional EIC activities
STATUS: Closed (deadline: January 5, 2026)

BUDGET: 15,000,000 EUR (2025)

DESCRICAO:
The initiative supports early-stage women-led deep-tech startups to address gender gaps in technology and foster diverse European ecosystems. Eligible companies must be registered in EU Member States or Horizon Europe Associated Countries, founded/co-founded by women holding executive positions (CEO, CTO, CSO, or equivalent), and operating in all deep tech domains.

ACTIVIDADES PRINCIPAIS:
- Design, organize, and manage the Women TechEU program with continuity
- Execute calls for proposals using EIC Accelerator evaluation criteria
- Establish linkages with Business Acceleration Services and EIC Community Platform
- Implement project reviews enabling Fast Track access to EIC Accelerator
- Promote the scheme through communication and landmark events
- Build networks among women entrepreneurs in deep tech
- Report implementation metrics to European Commission

FINANCIAMENTO A TERCEIROS:
Consortiums must allocate minimum 75% of budget as grants to third parties, with maximum 75,000 EUR per recipient. Grants support activities including product evaluation, design refinement, business model enhancement, strategy development, and market validation.

KEYWORDS:
Angel investment, business coaching, entrepreneurship, gender in innovation, SME support, start-up companies, seed capital, spin-offs, private equity

CONDICOES:
- Admissibility and eligibility conditions outlined in EIC Work Programme 2025 Annex 2
- Financial/operational capacity assessment required
- Award criteria and scoring detailed in Work Programme Annex 2
- Publish calls widely adhering to transparency and equal treatment standards
- Post all calls on Funding & Tenders Portal and beneficiary websites
- Maintain minimum 2-month submission windows
- Announce deadline changes immediately to registered applicants
- Publish outcomes promptly with third-party descriptions and details
- Ensure clear European dimension

TIPO DE ACCAO: HORIZON-CSA (Coordination and Support Actions)
GRANTS ESPERADOS: 1 projeto maximo
GRANT TYPE: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based
