INSTRUMENTO: Supporting the implementation of the Restore our Ocean and Waters Mission
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-MISS-2025-03
CALL REFERENCE: HORIZON-MISS-2025-03-OCEAN (8 topics)
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - EU Missions
AGENCIA: CINEA (European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency)
STATUS: Closed (deadline passed)

BUDGET: EUR 120,000,000 (indicativo)
DEADLINE: 24 September 2025, 17:00 CEST
ABERTURA: 7 May 2025
MODELO: Single-stage submission

MISSAO:
Protect and restore the health of our ocean and waters through research and innovation, citizen engagement and blue investments. Up to 18 projects are expected to be funded.

TOPICOS (8 calls under HORIZON-MISS-2025-03-OCEAN):

1. OCEAN-01: Blue Parks - research and innovation aimed at restoring marine biodiversity and ecosystem health.

2. OCEAN-02: Marine Litter Toolbox for Public Authorities - comprehensive toolbox to support public authorities in tackling plastic pollution and marine litter from rivers to the ocean.

3. OCEAN-03: Digital technologies and energy transition in fisheries and aquaculture - advancing use of digital technologies and energy-efficient solutions in fisheries and/or aquaculture.

4. OCEAN-04: Restoring Oceans and Waters in Regions - large-scale, place-based restoration of coastal and freshwater ecosystems in European regions.

5. OCEAN-05: Restoring Oceans and Waters in Waterfront Cities and their Ports - restoring ocean and water ecosystems in waterfront cities and their ports, emphasising urban resilience, pollution reduction and circular blue economy solutions.

6. OCEAN-06: Restoring Oceans and Waters in Islands - addressing ocean and water ecosystems in island communities, emphasising biodiversity protection, pollution prevention and a sustainable circular blue economy.

7. OCEAN-07: Mission Lighthouses Coordination and Support - coordinates and supports Mission Lighthouse areas to accelerate restoration of ocean and water ecosystems.

8. OCEAN-08: EU Digital Twin Ocean - developing and implementing the EU Digital Twin Ocean.
