Description:

The initiative targets next-generation self-protection capabilities to enhance aircraft survivability in hostile settings. It focuses on modular integration compatible with fixed and rotary wing platforms, offering protection against direct and indirect threats using open architecture and international standards while securing sensitive information and ensuring multi-platform compatibility.

Key elements include automatic countermeasure coordination, threat identification and analysis, and operation in complex scenarios. For indirect threats, systems should gather intelligence, disrupt adversary tracking, and enable cooperative defense across platforms.

This topic advances STEP objectives within defence technology investment areas.

Activities: Design, system prototyping, testing and qualification, including upstream and downstream development activities.
