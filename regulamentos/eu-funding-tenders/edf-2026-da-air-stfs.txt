Description:

The objective follows up and complements previous activities in smart technologies for next generation fighter systems. It explores future architectures and develops key technologies, including proofs of concept, demonstrations and prototypes, for next generation military integrated modular avionics for various military aerial platforms, both manned and unmanned aircraft, within a defence air cloud context. This topic contributes to STEP objectives in defence technologies. Targeted activities include integrating knowledge, studies, design and system prototyping, with upstream and downstream activities eligible for development actions.
