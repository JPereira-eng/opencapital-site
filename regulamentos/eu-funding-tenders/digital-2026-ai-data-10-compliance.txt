DIGITAL-2026-AI-DATA-10-COMPLIANCE
Digital solutions for regulatory compliance through data

Call Identifier: DIGITAL-2026-AI-DATA-10
Programme: Digital Europe Programme (2021-2027)
Type of Action: DIGITAL-LS (Lump Sum Grants)
Deadline: 2026-10-02

Budget Overview:
- 2027: 8,500,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 2

Keywords: Artificial intelligence, Cloud/Edge/Virtualisation, Digital Services and Platforms, cloud storage, data capturing technologies, data security, digital solutions, regulatory compliance through data, regulatory reporting requirements, reporting process

Description:
The objective is to deliver three to four projects demonstrating the streamlining of regulatory reporting through automated and trusted sharing of compliance data. The focus covers governance, technical, legal, and processing aspects of regulatory compliance.

Key objectives:
- Reduce the administrative burden on enterprises by applying advanced digital technologies to automate reporting processes
- Develop privacy-preserving technologies for trusted data sharing
- Enable automatic transmission and analysis of compliance data
- Implement cloud storage and encryption for data security
- Ensure systems are open source, interoperable with government data systems, and adhere to European data standards
- Create scalable and user-friendly solutions with comprehensive data governance frameworks

Sectors with significant administrative burden targeted include: agriculture, environment, manufacturing, healthcare, and energy.

Technical requirements:
- Privacy-preserving technologies
- Data capturing technologies
- Automatic data transmission and analysis
- Cloud storage with encryption for data security
- Interoperability with government systems
- Adherence to European data standards
- Scalability and user-friendliness
- Comprehensive data governance framework

Expected outcomes:
- Automated regulatory reporting systems demonstrated in real-world settings
- Reduced administrative burden for enterprises in regulated sectors
- Trusted data sharing mechanisms for compliance purposes
- Open source, interoperable platforms deployable across EU member states
