HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-44
Attracting management talent for capacity building for Technology Infrastructures staff members (CSA)

Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2026-04-21

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 2.500.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 1 (lump sum)

Description:
The initiative seeks proposals that develop schemes to recruit management professionals to technology infrastructures across EU member states. These programs attract talent capable of analyzing infrastructure needs regarding competencies, organizational structures, and business models to enhance service delivery to industry, particularly SMEs and startups.

Key Objectives:
- Recruit management professionals with skills in business development, strategy, and organizational transformation
- Provide advisory services to senior management of Technology Infrastructures
- Deliver staff training in sector-agnostic, remotely accessible formats
- Address emerging competency gaps: research security, data management, quality assurance
- Foster entrepreneurial capabilities within technology infrastructure teams
- Support transnational collaboration and knowledge exchange among Technology Infrastructures
- Develop ecosystem partnerships linking Technology Infrastructures with industry

Scope:
- Transnational programs spanning multiple EU Member States and Associated Countries
- Remote-accessible training modules targeting staff at all levels
- Management talent recruitment schemes tailored to Technology Infrastructure needs
- Knowledge exchange networks for best practice sharing

Conditions:
- Admissibility per Annexes A-G of Work Programme General Annexes
- Lump sum grant model per Decision of July 7, 2021
- Cost categories C/D require itemized justification in Excel budget sheets
- Evaluation uses standard CSA evaluation forms
