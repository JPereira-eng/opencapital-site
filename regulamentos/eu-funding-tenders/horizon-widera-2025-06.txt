CALL: HORIZON-WIDERA-2025-06
NAME: Enhancing the European R&I system (ERA call 2025)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - Widening Participation and Strengthening ERA (European Research Area)
MANAGED BY: REA (Research Executive Agency)
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
HORIZON-WIDERA-2025-06 is the ERA (European Research Area) strengthening call within the Widening Participation work programme of Horizon Europe 2025. It covers multiple topics designed to reform and enhance the European research and innovation system, supporting implementation of the New ERA policy agenda.

TOPICS (7 topics, EUR 44 million total budget):

1. Enable sustained coordination and guidance at the European level on institutional non-profit open access publishing
   Focus: Open science infrastructure and publishing reform

2. Research and innovation to provide evidence that supports reforms of research assessment
   Focus: Evidence base for research assessment reform (aligning with CoARA - Coalition for Advancing Research Assessment)

3. Investigating and addressing career barriers faced by underrepresented and marginalised researchers
   Focus: Diversity and inclusion in research careers

4. Programme-level collaboration
   Focus: Coordination and alignment between national/regional R&I programmes

5. Strengthening of the European Science for Policy Ecosystem
   Focus: Evidence-informed policy making, science-policy interface

6. Science comes to town 2027
   Focus: Public engagement with science and innovation

7. Additional ERA reform support topic(s)

DEADLINE:
Application deadline: 18 September 2025
(Note: This deadline has passed as of April 2026. Call is closed.)
Total submissions received: 113 proposals across all topics.

BUDGET:
Overall indicative budget: EUR 44 million across 7 topics.
Typical per-project funding: EUR 3-8 million depending on topic.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Research performing organisations (universities, research centres)
- Research funding organisations and national research councils
- Public authorities involved in R&I governance
- Established in EU Member States or Horizon Europe Associated Countries
- Consortia from multiple countries; some topics may allow single beneficiaries for coordination actions

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Policy-oriented research and analysis
- Capacity building for open science infrastructure
- Studies and evidence gathering for research assessment reform
- Coordination and support activities
- Stakeholder engagement and science communication
- Networking among national R&I funding bodies

ELIGIBLE EXPENSES:
- Personnel costs
- Subcontracting for specific expertise
- Dissemination and communication costs
- Coordination and governance costs
- Indirect costs (overhead)

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Excellence and quality of the proposed approach
- Relevance to ERA policy priorities and objectives
- Potential for systemic impact on the European R&I system
- Quality of consortium and implementation plan

ERA CONTEXT:
The New European Research Area (ERA 2.0) focuses on: open science, research assessment reform, gender equality in research, careers and mobility, knowledge valorisation, and international cooperation. HORIZON-WIDERA-2025-06 directly implements these ERA priorities through funded projects.
