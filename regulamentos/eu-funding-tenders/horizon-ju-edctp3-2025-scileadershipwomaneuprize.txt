Scientific Leadership Prize for Women (EU/Associated Countries)

Title: Scientific Leadership Prize for a woman resident of an EU Member State or a country associated with the Horizon Europe programme

Call Identifier: HORIZON-JU-EDCTP3-2025-SciLeadershipWomanEUPrize

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON) | Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness | Health Division

Budget and Funding:
- Single prize of EUR 15,000
- One of four total Scientific Leadership prizes available across different recipient categories
- Total prizes: EUR 60,000 across all four categories

Deadline:
- Submission Deadline: April 8, 2025 (CLOSED)
- One proposal was submitted; evaluation results expected May 2025

Programme: Global Health EDCTP3 (European and Developing Countries Clinical Trials Partnership)
Type: HORIZON Prize (Recognition Prize - HORIZON-RPr)

Eligibility Requirements:
Applicants must satisfy three core conditions:
- Be a natural person below 50 years old on the day of the launch of the contest
- Be established (residing) in the EU or a country associated to Horizon Europe
- Identify as female

All applicants require Participant Register validation and Central Validation Service approval.

Exclusions: Applicants cannot participate if they are current employees of Global Health EDCTP3, EU institutions, or EDCTP Association bodies, or have previously received an EDCTP prize for identical activities.

Evaluation Criteria (100 points total):
1. Scientific Contribution and Excellence (50 points): Contestants should have made significant achievements in their field and will continue to be leaders
2. Impact (50 points): Contributions toward Global Health EDCTP3 programme to strengthen research capacity in sub-Saharan Africa

Required Documentation:
- Application Form Parts A and B (Part B limited to 45 pages maximum)
- CV of applicant
- Letter(s) of support from reputable scientist(s) with their CV

Keywords: Africa, EDCTP3, Scientific Leadership, edctp

Submission Process: Applications submitted exclusively through Funding and Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System (no paper submissions accepted).
