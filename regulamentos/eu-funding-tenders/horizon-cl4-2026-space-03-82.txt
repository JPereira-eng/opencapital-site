HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-82
Space critical EEE components for EU non-dependence – GaN MMICs mm-Wave Foundations (Phase A): Development and Industrialization of Semi-insulating SiC Substrate Capabilities

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03 (SPACE)
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 6,860,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 1
- Min Contribution: 6,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 7,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: April 9, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Projects must strengthen EU strategic autonomy by reducing critical EEE space component dependencies; provide unrestricted access to technologies for Galileo, EGNOS, Copernicus, IRIS2 and EU pilot missions; develop independent operational capability in space; enhance competitiveness with equivalent or superior products globally.

SCOPE:
Development of semi-insulating SiC substrates for GaN components in mm-wave applications. Aims to create a sustainable supply chain preferably based in the EU. Applicants must demonstrate that the supply chain and final product are free of any legal export restrictions (ITAR, EAR). Includes vulnerability analysis, industrial roadmap, commercial plan and compliance with standards (EN, ECSS, ESCC, MIL, JEDEC). Target: TRL 5 at project end per ISO 16290:2013.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Entities established in EU Member States, Norway and Iceland
- Entities controlled by non-eligible countries require approved security guarantees
- Entities assessed as high-risk suppliers of mobile network communication equipment cannot submit guarantees
- Post-project obligation: hardware/processes must be available for 4 years at fair and reasonable market prices for entities in EU, Norway and Iceland, without legal restrictions

CONDITIONS:
- Page limit: 80 pages
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Confidential deliverables within 6 months analyzing each entity in the supply chain and its criticality level
