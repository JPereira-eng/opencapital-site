CEF-DIG-2026-SMART-CABLES-WORKS
Equipment for smart European cable systems - Works

Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-SMART-CABLES-WORKS
Call Identifier: CEF-DIG-2026-SMART-CABLES
Status: Open
Framework Programme: Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) / CEF2027
Programme Division: CEF-2.3.1 (Digital)
Budget Overview (EUR): 20,000,000 for 2026
Deadline Date: September 8, 2026
Action Type: CEF-INFRA CEF Infrastructure Projects, single-stage submission, CEF Action Grant Budget-Based (CEF-AG)

Description:
The call supports upgrading existing submarine telecommunications infrastructure with smart monitoring capabilities. It aims to enhance security and resilience of EU submarine cable systems through deployment of cutting-edge technologies. Expected impacts include improved EU digital sovereignty, enhanced threat detection, and advanced environmental monitoring. Key objectives involve deploying smart cable technologies, securing data sharing with regional hubs, and promoting data use among authorized stakeholders for environmental research.

Smart cables refer to submarine telecommunications cables instrumented with environmental and structural sensors (DAS, DTS, DOFS, hydrophones) able to detect physical disturbances along the cable route. The dual-use nature of the data, both for security/resilience and for ocean and climate science, makes this strand strategically important for the EU.

Scope: Funded actions can include the design, manufacturing, qualification and deployment of sensor packages on existing or planned cable systems; the establishment of secure data pipelines from cables to regional hubs; the integration with existing EU and national monitoring frameworks; and the development of governance models for sharing the data with authorised research, environmental and security stakeholders.

Eligibility:
Public bodies, telecommunications operators, cable consortia, research institutions and technology providers established in EU Member States, EEA countries and CEF-associated third countries. Cross-border consortia and projects building on existing cable assets are encouraged.

Despesas elegiveis: equipamento de sensorizacao (DAS, DTS, hydrophones), instalacao subaquatica e em landing stations, integracao com cables existentes, plataformas de dados, equipamento de seguranca, custos de pessoal de engenharia, marine operations, testes de aceitacao, gestao de projeto.

Criterios de selecao: relevancia para soberania digital e resiliencia da infraestrutura submarina UE, maturidade tecnica das solucoes smart cable, ambicao em deteccao de ameacas e monitoring ambiental, qualidade do plano de partilha de dados, sustentabilidade pos-projeto, dimensao europeia.

Conditions: Conditions are detailed across sections 5-11 of the call document, covering admissibility, proposal formatting, eligible countries, financial capacity, submission/evaluation procedures, award criteria, and grant setup requirements.

Keywords: Submarine cables, smart cables, sensors, DAS, DTS, hydrophones, ocean observation, environmental monitoring, threat detection, digital sovereignty, CEF Digital, CEF2027.
