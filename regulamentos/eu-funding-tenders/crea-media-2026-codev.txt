CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-CODEV
TITLE: European co-development
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Lump Sum Grants (CREA-AG-LS)
BUDGET: 9,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 25 February 2026, 17:00 Brussels time (closed)
STATUS: Closed. Grant agreement signature November 2026.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative aims to support the cooperation among European production companies that are developing works with a strong international audience potential. Expected outcomes include:
- Expanded cross-border collaboration between European producers
- Enhanced project quality and market feasibility
- Strengthened competitive positioning in European and international markets

DESCRIPTION:
The action funds development of single projects for cinema, television, or digital platforms across animation, creative documentary, or fiction categories. Projects require co-development by at least two European independent production companies with formal co-development agreements. The scheme emphasizes innovative collaboration, sustainable practices, environmental responsibility, and diversity considerations.

ELIGIBILITY:
- European independent production company status required
- Co-development partnership structure required (minimum 2 companies)
- Compliance with financial and operational capacity assessments
- Full eligibility conditions detailed in the call document

KEY DEADLINES:
- Publication: 30 September 2025
- Application deadline: 25 February 2026
- Evaluation: March-July 2026
- Grant agreement signature: November 2026
