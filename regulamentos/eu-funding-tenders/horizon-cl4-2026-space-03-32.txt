HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-32
Preparing demonstration missions for Earth Observation and Satellite telecommunication for Space solutions (Space Partnership)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-IA (Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
KEYWORDS: STEP-Digital/deep tech

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 26,000,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 4
- Min Contribution: 5,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 10,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: May 5, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Contribute to outcomes including: competitive European space sector development, maintaining global connectivity market share, advanced payloads and processing technologies, AI integration across space systems, enhanced security, and end-to-end demonstration capabilities.

SCOPE - R&I AREAS:
1. SatCom Mission Capabilities: End-to-end systems, secure services, satellite network nodes, RF payloads, flexible testbeds for architecture assessment, onboard RF signal processing, and compatibility/operations concepts.

2. Earth Observation Equipment: LEO/VLEO systems enabling real-time reaction, onboard processing optimization, ground segment standardization, and multi-source intelligence fusion.

3. Synergetic Technologies: Cross-applicable building blocks for both EO and SatCom, operational/design optimization, system-of-systems management, and "tip and cue" demonstrations using inter-satellite links.

Developments should target technologies in relevant ground environments with in-orbit software demonstrations where feasible, emphasizing digital tools and algorithms. Proposals must promote multi-actor cooperation (industry, SMEs, research institutions, academia) and demonstrate rapid commercialization pathways.

Projects should leverage existing EU technologies and building blocks while remaining complementary to H2020, Horizon Europe, and ESA-funded activities.

TRL TARGET: Achieve TRL 7-8 by project completion (ISO 16290:2013 standard).

ELIGIBILITY:
- EU Member States, Norway and Iceland
- Non-eligible country involvement requires demonstrated guarantees protecting Union strategic interests
- Projects using satellite-based Earth observation, positioning, or navigation must utilize Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS services

CONDITIONS:
- Lump sum contributions
- Balanced portfolio approach: grants awarded by ranking with minimum one highest-ranked proposal per development area, provided all evaluation thresholds are met
- STEP Seal awarded to eligible exceeding proposals
- Some activities may involve classified background or security-sensitive results (EUCI/SEN)
