Horizon Europe - Building innovative value chains from raw materials to sustainable products (IA)

Title: Building innovative value chains from raw materials to sustainable products (IA)
Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01-07
Parent Call: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Action Type: Innovation Actions (IA) under HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based framework

Budget:
- Total Budget: EUR 36,000,000
- Action type: Innovation Actions (IA)

Deadline:
- Submission Deadline: September 23, 2021
- Status: CLOSED

Description:
Projects must develop innovative, sustainable technology and business solutions reaching Technology Readiness Levels 6-7 for high-value products utilizing EU-produced raw materials.

Consortia should encompass industry players across value chains from extraction to manufacturing, focusing on:
- Battery materials for e-mobility
- Renewable energy sector raw materials
- Aerospace sector materials
- Critical raw materials including rare earth elements

Proposals require business cases and exploitation strategies with credible pathways to full-scale EU manufacturing post-project.

Keywords: Geology, Mechanical engineering, Mining and mineral processing, Sustainable design (for recycling, for environment, eco-design)

Eligibility:
- Legal entities established in EU Member States
- Associated countries
- OECD countries
- African Union countries
- MERCOSUR, CARIFORUM, and Andean Community nations

How to Apply:
Submission via Funding and Tenders Portal (F&T Portal). Proposals must address the full value chain from raw material extraction through to final product manufacturing within EU/associated territories.
