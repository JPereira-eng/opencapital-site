Scientific Leadership Prize for Men (EU/Associated Countries)

Title: Scientific Leadership Prize for a man resident of an EU Member State or a country associated with the Horizon Europe programme

Call Identifier: HORIZON-JU-EDCTP3-2025-SciLeadershipManEUPrize

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON) | Global Challenges and Industrial Competitiveness | Health Division

Budget and Funding:
- Amount: EUR 15,000 per prize
- Number of Awards: 1 prize under this specific category
- Total Available: EUR 15,000

Deadline:
- Opening Date: March 11, 2025 (11:00 CET)
- Submission Deadline: April 8, 2025
- Status: CLOSED (5 proposals submitted; evaluation results expected May 2025)

Eligibility Requirements (Applicant Criteria):
- Natural person under 50 years old at contest launch
- Established/residing in EU or Horizon Europe-associated country
- Identifies as male
- Registered in Participant Register before deadline

Ineligible Parties: Current employees of Global Health EDCTP3, EU institutions, or EDCTP Association governing bodies; prior EDCTP prize recipients for same activities.

Evaluation Criteria (100 points total):
1. Scientific contribution and excellence (50 points): Contestants should have made significant achievements in their field and continue to be leaders
2. Impact (50 points): Major contributions to Global Health EDCTP3 programme objectives, strengthening research capacity and networking in sub-Saharan Africa

Required Documents:
- Application Form Part A (online submission)
- Application Form Part B (45-page maximum, downloadable template)
- CV of applicant
- Letter(s) of support from reputable scientist(s) with their CV

Keywords: Africa, EDCTP3, Scientific Leadership, EDCTP

Action Type: HORIZON-RPr - HORIZON Recognition Prize (single-stage submission)

Associated Programme: Global Health EDCTP3 (European and Developing Countries Clinical Trials Partnership)

Additional Notes:
- Ethics review, security scrutiny, and legal entity validation required
- Prize money disbursed post-award ceremony
- Winners must acknowledge EU/EDCTP3 funding in all communications with required disclaimer
- Applications submitted exclusively through Funding and Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System (no paper submissions)
