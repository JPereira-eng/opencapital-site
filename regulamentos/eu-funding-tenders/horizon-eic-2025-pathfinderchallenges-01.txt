CALL: HORIZON-EIC-2025-PATHFINDERCHALLENGES-01
NAME: EIC Pathfinder Challenges 2025
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - European Innovation Council (EIC)
MANAGED BY: European Innovation Council
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
EIC Pathfinder Challenges supports coherent portfolios of projects within predefined thematic areas to achieve specific objectives for each Challenge. The overall objective is to develop the scientific basis for breakthrough technologies, providing support for the earliest stages of scientific, technological or deep-tech research and development.

This is distinct from EIC Pathfinder Open (which accepts any breakthrough technology theme). The Pathfinder Challenges define specific thematic areas where European scientific leadership is sought.

FOUR THEMATIC CHALLENGES (2025 edition):
1. Biotech for Climate Resilient Crops and Plant-Based Biomanufacturing
2. Generative-AI based Agents to Revolutionise Medical Diagnosis and Treatment of Cancer
3. Towards autonomous robot collectives delivering collaborative tasks in dynamic unstructured construction environments
4. Waste-to-value devices: circular production of renewable fuels, chemicals and materials

BUDGET:
Total budget for all four challenges: EUR 120 million
Allocation per challenge: approximately EUR 30 million each
Per project maximum: up to EUR 4 million

FUNDING MODEL:
- Lump sum model: beneficiaries do not need to justify actual costs
- Must deliver agreed milestones and outputs
- 100% funding rate (fully funded by EU)
- No co-financing requirement

ELIGIBILITY:
- Consortia or single legal entities
- Established in EU Member States or Horizon Europe Associated Countries
- Eligible entities: universities, research organisations, SMEs, startups, industrial partners
- No minimum number of partners required (single applicant possible)

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Fundamental and applied research at the earliest stages (TRL 1-4)
- Scientific and technological groundbreaking approaches
- Proof-of-concept and feasibility studies
- Multidisciplinary research combining science and engineering

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Breakthrough potential: transformative nature of the proposed concept
- Scientific excellence: soundness and ambition of the research approach
- Team quality: skills, experience, and complementarity
- Impact potential within the specific Challenge theme

CALL TIMELINE:
- Call launch: 28 July 2025
- Application deadline: 29 October 2025, 17:00 CET
