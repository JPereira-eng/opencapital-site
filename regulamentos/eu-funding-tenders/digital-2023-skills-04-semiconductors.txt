DIGITAL-2023-SKILLS-04-SEMICONDUCTORS
Reinforcing skills in semiconductors

Identifier: DIGITAL-2023-SKILLS-04-SEMICONDUCTORS
Call Identifier: DIGITAL-2023-SKILLS-04
Framework Programme: Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)
Status: Closed
Deadline Date: September 25, 2023

Budget Overview in EUR:
- DIGITAL-2023-SKILLS-04-SEMICONDUCTORS: 10,000,000 (expected 2 grants)
- DIGITAL-2023-SKILLS-04-BOOSTINGDIGIT: 6,000,000 (expected 1 grant)

Description:
The objective addresses the shortage of ICT and semiconductor professionals by improving public awareness of semiconductors' societal impact, bridging gaps between education and industry labor demands, and updating academic curricula. The scope includes two complementary actions: (I) developing a European Semiconductors Skills Academy through higher education networks with industry partnerships to increase student enrollment and provide cutting-edge training; and (II) establishing a vocational training platform among VET centers, SMEs, and startups to support continuing professional development and attract talent through bootcamps and apprenticeships.

The European Chips Act and the broader EU semiconductor strategy underline the urgent need to expand the talent pool of microelectronics designers, manufacturing engineers, packaging specialists, equipment technicians, and chip architecture researchers. This call funds structured, multi-country networks that can deliver coordinated curricula, joint master programmes, summer schools, internships in fabs and design houses, and public-private upskilling pathways.

Scope: Action I supports a European Semiconductors Skills Academy that combines higher-education degrees with industry-led specialisations across analog, digital, RF, power, and advanced packaging. Action II supports continuing professional development and attraction of new talent via bootcamps, apprenticeships, dual training, and re-skilling of mid-career engineers.

Despesas elegiveis: pessoal docente, equipamento de laboratorio (clean rooms, EDA tools, characterization equipment), bolsas, mobilidade entre HEIs e empresas, desenvolvimento de plataformas digitais de formacao, eventos, dissemination, gestao.

Criterios de selecao: alinhamento com Chips Act, dimensao europeia da rede, envolvimento da industria, qualidade tecnica do programa formativo, impacto no recrutamento e retencao de talento, sustentabilidade.

Actions:
1. DIGITAL-SIMPLE Digital Simple Grants (Budget-Based)
2. DIGITAL-CSA Coordination and Support Actions

Keywords: Semiconductors, higher education, vocational training, IT skills, microelectronics, emerging industries, innovation, education-enterprise partnerships, professional development, Chips Act, European Semiconductors Skills Academy.
