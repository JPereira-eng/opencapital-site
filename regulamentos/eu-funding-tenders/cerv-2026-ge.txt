ESTADO: Open (1 of 4 priorities of the call for proposals to promote gender equality)

TIPO DE ACAO: CERV Lump Sum Grants (single-stage submission)

DESCRICAO:
This topic focuses on tackling gender stereotypes in media and advertising as one of the four priorities of the gender equality call. The initiative promotes measures and campaigns that challenge and change stereotypical portrayals of women and men in media, advertising and other communication channels.
