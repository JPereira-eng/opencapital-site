HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-02
Advancing European Climate Risk Assessments

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07
TOPIC: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-02
STATUS: Open
PROGRAM: Horizon Europe - Climate Science and Solutions (HORIZON.2.5.1)
ACTION TYPE: RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
FUNDING MODEL: Lump sum grants

DEADLINE: 21 April 2026, 17:00 CET (postponed due to technical issues)
OPENING DATE: 18 September 2025

BUDGET:
- This topic (D1-02): EUR 15,000,000
- Expected grants: 3
- Grant range: EUR 4,000,000 - EUR 5,000,000 per project
- Total call (all D1 topics): EUR 82,000,000

OBJECTIVES:
Projects must advance understanding of climate risks by narrowing knowledge gaps identified in the EU Climate Risk Assessments and latest IPCC assessments. Work should integrate evidence from recent climate events and adaptation experiences to improve understanding of key factors that determine effective environmental and social resilience, preparedness and adaptation capacity.

SCOPE:
Proposals must address at least two interconnected systems (ecosystems, agriculture, health, infrastructure, energy, water, finance) with transboundary effects and include case studies spanning the four European regions defined in EUCRA.

DESPESAS ELEGIVEIS / ELEGIBILIDADE:
- EU member states and associated countries (Annex B)
- Must use Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS for satellite-based earth observation data
- Lump sum grants - budget justification required for categories C/D exceeding 15% of personnel costs
- Financial capacity and exclusion criteria per Annex C

KEYWORDS: Climate risk assessment, climate adaptation, resilience, transboundary effects, EUCRA, IPCC, EU Climate Risk Assessment
