HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-85
Critical Facilities Serving Space EEE components for EU non-dependence – High and Very High Energy Irradiation Test Facility Market Deployment

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03 (SPACE)
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-IA (Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Action Grant Budget-Based (HORIZON-AG)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 3,920,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 1
- Min Contribution: 3,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 4,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: June 10, 2026
OPENING DATE: March 10, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
Strengthen EU strategic autonomy by reducing critical EEE space component dependencies; provide unrestricted access to components and test facilities for EU space missions (Galileo, EGNOS, Copernicus, IRIS2); develop independent operational capability in space; increase competitiveness with equivalent or superior performance products.

SCOPE:
Fund development of a dedicated irradiation testing facility, accessible to European space stakeholders. Move from demonstrative prototypes to a fully operational facility with sufficient beam time distributed throughout the year, supporting EU strategic autonomy in the space sector. The supply chain must be preferably entirely EU-based.

Activities must reach TRL 8 by project end. Proposals must include detailed supply chain analysis demonstrating absence of legal export restrictions (ITAR, EAR), and describe a technical roadmap with commercial plan for open access to the facility for external space stakeholders.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Entities established in EU Member States, Norway and Iceland
- Entities outside the specified scope are ineligible
- Demonstrate supply chain free of ITAR/EAR restrictions

CONDITIONS:
- Page limit: 80 pages
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Confidential deliverables within 6 months analyzing each entity and its role in the supply chain and criticality level
- Guarantee hardware/process availability for 4 years post-project at fair and reasonable prices without legal restrictions
- Notify the granting authority before any ownership transfer or exclusive license
