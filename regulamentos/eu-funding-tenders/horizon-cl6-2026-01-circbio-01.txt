Improving circularity of multilayer flexible plastic food contact packaging

Description:
This topic focuses on improving the circularity of multilayer flexible plastic food contact packaging. The objective is to develop innovation actions that address the recyclability and circular economy challenges of multilayer flexible plastic packaging used in food contact applications.

Multilayer flexible plastics are widely used in food packaging due to their protective properties, but their complex multi-material composition makes recycling technically challenging. This topic seeks solutions to improve the design for recyclability of these materials, develop advanced sorting and recycling technologies, and create new business models that support circularity.

Expected outcomes include:
- Development of recyclable or mono-material alternatives to multilayer flexible food packaging
- Advanced sorting technologies capable of identifying and separating multilayer packaging streams
- Improved recycling processes that can handle multilayer materials
- Life Cycle Analysis demonstrating environmental benefits
- New industrial value chains and business models for circular packaging
- Reduced marine litter and plastic pollution from food contact packaging
