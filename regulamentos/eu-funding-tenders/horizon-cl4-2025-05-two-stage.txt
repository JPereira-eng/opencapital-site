INSTRUMENTO: INDUSTRY Two-Stage Call 2025 (Cluster 4)
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2025-05-two-stage
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - Cluster 4: Digital, Industry and Space
AGENCIA: HaDEA (European Health and Digital Executive Agency)
STATUS: Stage 1 closed; Stage 2 open

BUDGET: EUR 161,000,000 (indicativo)
DEADLINE STAGE 1: 23 September 2025, 17:00 CEST
DEADLINE STAGE 2: 14 April 2026, 17:00 CEST
PUBLICACAO: 15 May 2025
ABERTURA: 22 May 2025
MODELO: Two-stage submission

TOPICOS: 6 topics under this call

DESCRICAO:
Industry call under Horizon Europe Cluster 4 (Digital, Industry and Space), focusing on industrial transformation, advanced manufacturing, and twin transition (digital and green). The two-stage model requires first a short concept note, with full proposals invited only from selected applicants.

AREAS TEMATICAS (Digital, Industry and Space - Cluster 4):
- Advanced manufacturing technologies
- Key enabling technologies for industrial competitiveness
- Twin transition (digital + green) for European industry
- Processes4Planet partnership activities (including upcycling technologies)
- Materials for industrial applications
- Space-related industrial topics

RESULTADO:
882 proposals submitted in the 2025 call across Industry tracks.

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Entities established in EU Member States and Horizon Europe Associated Countries. Research organisations, companies (including SMEs), higher education institutions. Full details in the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2025 (wp-7-digital-industry-and-space).

COMO CANDIDATAR:
Full application details available on the EU Funding and Tenders Portal and HaDEA website.
