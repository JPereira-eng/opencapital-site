HORIZON-CL4-2026-SPACE-03-31
Digital enablers and building-blocks for Earth Observation and Satellite telecommunication for Space solutions (Space Partnership)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-03 (SPACE)
STATUS: Open
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
MODEL GRANT: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)

BUDGET:
- 2026 Budget: 12,000,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 3
- Min Contribution: 3,000,000 EUR
- Max Contribution: 6,000,000 EUR

DEADLINE: July 7, 2026
OPENING DATE: April 2, 2026

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
This topic covers actions on satellite communications (SatCom), Earth Observation (EO), and commercial space transport solutions, focusing on digitalisation for commercial space solutions.

SCOPE:
Projects must address:
1. End-to-end SatCom mission capabilities
2. Earth Observation equipment, subsystems and services
3. Building blocks common to EO and SatCom systems, including resource optimization, high-performance payload processing and hybrid RF/optical ground stations

Proposals must promote cooperation between industry, SMEs, research institutions and academia, converting technological innovation into commercial use. Must use existing EU technologies and be complementary to H2020, Horizon Europe and ESA-funded projects. Activities must achieve TRL 4-5 at project end.

ELIGIBILITY:
- EU Member States, Iceland, Norway, Canada, New Zealand, United Kingdom and Switzerland
- Restrictions on control by non-eligible countries
- Mandatory use of Copernicus and/or Galileo/EGNOS data if applicable

CONDITIONS:
- Lump sum costs
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Balanced selection between development areas
- Award criteria per Annex D and F
