TIPO DE ACAO: CEF Action Grant Budget-Based (CEF-AG) for CEF Infrastructure Projects

DESCRICAO:
The call supports deployment of alternative fuel supply infrastructure to decarbonize transport along TEN-T networks. Eligible actions include electricity recharging stations for heavy-duty vehicles (150kW, 350kW, 1MW), hydrogen refueling for HDVs and public transport, hydrogen and electricity infrastructure for maritime/inland waterway vessels, and bunkering facilities for ammonia/methanol supply to ships and port operations. Vehicle costs are ineligible except vessel cost differentials for zero-emission maritime vessels.
