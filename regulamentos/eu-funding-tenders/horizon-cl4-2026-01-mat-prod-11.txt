HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-11
Innovative technologies and tools for exploration and data modelling of raw materials (RIA)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
STATUS: Open
FUNDING SCHEME: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
SUPPORTED ACTIONS: Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
PROGRAMME PERIOD: Horizon Europe 2021-2027
PUBLICATION DATE: July 31, 2025

BUDGET OVERVIEW (2026):
Total Budget: EUR 18,500,000
Expected Grants: 3
Min Contribution per Grant: EUR 5,000,000
Max Contribution per Grant: EUR 7,000,000

DEADLINE: January 29, 2027

DESCRIPTION:
The action targets advancing geological modelling and mineral system analysis using multi-source data to identify critical raw materials deposits across EU regions. Projects must:
- Integrate AI, machine learning, and conventional geological methods
- Generate better geological understanding of critical raw material deposits
- Develop ore genetic models for predicting deposit locations
- Improve efficiency and accuracy of exploration activities

The goal is to reduce EU import dependencies for critical raw materials by improving domestic exploration capabilities and geological data quality.

Activities should progress from TRL 3-4 at start to TRL 5 by project completion.

ELIGIBILITY:
Participation restricted to entities established in:
- EU Member States and associated countries
- OECD member nations
- African Union member states
- MERCOSUR, CARIFORUM, Andean Community countries
- Countries with EU raw materials strategic partnerships

Entities controlled by ineligible countries are excluded.

CONDITIONS:
- Research and Innovation Actions (RIA) type
- Single-stage submission procedure
- Business case and exploitation strategy required
- Clustering with complementary raw materials projects encouraged

KEYWORDS: Critical raw materials, mineral exploration, geological modelling, geophysical data, machine learning, AI, mineral deposits, ore genetic models, EU supply security, data modelling
