HORIZON-CL6-2025-02-two-stage - Cluster 6 Food Bioeconomy Two-Stage Call 2025
==============================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL6-2025-02-two-stage
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe
CLUSTER: Cluster 6 - Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment
MANAGING AGENCY: REA (Research Executive Agency)
STATUS: Closed (Stage 1 deadline passed; Stage 2 deadline: 18 February 2026)

THEMATIC FOCUS
--------------
Two-stage call under Cluster 6 covering food systems, agroecology, biodiversity, and bioeconomy under the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2025.

KNOWN TOPICS
------------
HORIZON-CL6-2025-02-FARM2FORK-05-two-stage: Developing agroecology living labs and lighthouses for climate action under the Food and Nutrition Security and Sustainable Agriculture (FNSSA) partnership.
- Objective: Establish and scale agroecology living labs integrating multiple stakeholders (farmers, researchers, extension services, policymakers)
- Supports the Farm to Fork Strategy and EU food system transformation

COVERED AREAS
-------------
- Fair, healthy and environmentally friendly food systems (primary production to consumption)
- Agroecology and sustainable agriculture practices
- Biodiversity and ecosystem services
- Circular economy and bioeconomy sectors
- Land, oceans and water for climate action
- Clean environment and zero pollution
- Innovative governance and digital solutions for the Green Deal

BLIND EVALUATION PILOT
-----------------------
First-stage proposals under this call were subject to the blind evaluation pilot: proposals were evaluated without knowledge of the applicant's identity, institution, or prior track record. Only the scientific content was assessed at Stage 1.

TWO-STAGE PROCESS
-----------------
Stage 1: Outline application (max 10 pages), evaluated on Excellence and Impact only.
Stage 1 deadline: (passed in 2025)
Successful applicants invited to full proposal.

Stage 2: Full proposal evaluated on Excellence, Impact, and Implementation.
Stage 2 deadline: 18 February 2026

ELIGIBLE PROJECT TYPES
-----------------------
Research and Innovation Actions (RIA), Innovation Actions (IA), Coordination and Support Actions (CSA).

ELIGIBLE COUNTRIES
------------------
EU Member States and Horizon Europe associated countries, including Portugal.
