## Expected Outcomes
The proposal should deliver results contributing to:

1. Member States identifying priority areas where NAMs and infrastructures are most needed with highest short to medium-term impact.
2. Joint validation and qualification of limited NAMs sets for regulatory testing acceptance.
3. Common education and training programmes based on best practices for researchers and regulators on NAMs and the 3Rs principles.
4. Harmonised openness and awareness programme improving open access to NAM protocols and animal experiment results.

## Scope & Activities

This topic coordinates the European Research Area policy action accelerating NAMs development, validation, acceptance, and uptake in biomedical research and regulatory testing. The selected proposal should establish an EU-wide forum bringing together relevant ministries, regulatory agencies, research funding organisations, academia, industry and other stakeholders.

Activities span four thematic Working Groups:

WG1 - Development: Identifies NAM development opportunities, establishes supporting infrastructures, focuses on disease areas and safety/quality/efficacy assessment endpoints.

WG2 - Validation/Acceptance: Defines optimal NAM criteria, proposes validation priorities, facilitates regulatory uptake decisions.

WG3 - Education/Training: Maps existing programmes, assesses quality and outreach, develops high-quality training modules on NAMs and 3Rs principles.

WG4 - Openness/Awareness: Develops policies improving openness, facilitates negative/neutral result publication preventing duplication, enhances regulator confidence in validated NAMs.
