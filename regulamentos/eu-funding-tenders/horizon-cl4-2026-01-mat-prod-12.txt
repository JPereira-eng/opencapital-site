HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-12
Technologies for innovative extraction of critical raw materials (RIA)

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
STATUS: Open
FUNDING SCHEME: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
SUPPORTED ACTIONS: Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)
PROGRAMME PERIOD: Horizon Europe 2026-2027
PUBLICATION DATE: April 8, 2025

BUDGET OVERVIEW (2026):
Total Budget: EUR 19,000,000
Expected Grants: 3
Min Contribution per Grant: EUR 5,000,000
Max Contribution per Grant: EUR 7,000,000

DEADLINE: January 29, 2027

DESCRIPTION:
The topic focuses on developing sustainable extraction technologies for complex mineral deposits critical to EU supply security. Expected outcomes include:
- Increasing availability of efficient extraction technologies for critical raw materials
- Improving EU primary sourcing capacity for raw materials
- Substantially reducing greenhouse gas emissions from extraction activities
- Reducing environmental impacts including water pollution, biodiversity loss, and ecosystem health degradation

Actions should address challenges of accessibility, efficiency, viability, safety, and environmental concerns related to mineral extraction. Proposals must demonstrate significant improvement over current state-of-the-art extraction methods.

Activities should progress from TRL 3-4 at start to TRL 5 by project completion.

ELIGIBILITY:
Participation restricted to entities established in:
- EU Member States and associated countries
- OECD member countries
- African Union member states
- MERCOSUR, CARIFORUM, Andean Community countries
- Countries with EU strategic raw materials partnerships or relevant trade agreements

CONDITIONS:
- Extended 3-page limit for business case and exploitation strategy
- Lump sum budget items in categories C/D require justification
- Clustering with other projects required
- Single-stage submission procedure

KEYWORDS: Critical raw materials, sustainable extraction, mineral deposits, TRL 3-5, greenhouse gas reduction, environmental impact, EU raw materials supply, mineral processing
