PROGRAMA: Global Health EDCTP3 Joint Undertaking - Call 2025-01
CODIGO: HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-01-two-stage
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-01-two-stage
ESTADO: Closed (prazo encerrado)
FONTE: Global Health EDCTP3 JU / Horizon Europe

DOTACAO E FINANCIAMENTO
- Dotacao total: EUR 214.000.000
- Contribuicao JU por projeto: entre EUR 1.340.000 e EUR 15.300.000
- Tipo de call: Two-stage

DEADLINE
- Prazo de submissao: 20 de marco de 2025

TOPICOS DA CALL
1. HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-01-TB-01-two-stage
   - Global collaboration for vaccine development for Tuberculosis in sub-Saharan Africa
   - Tipo: Research and Innovation Action (RIA)

2. HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-01-MALARIA-02-two-stage
   - Research on existing Malaria therapeutics and clinical development of new antimalarial candidates
   - Tipo: Research and Innovation Action (RIA)

3. HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-01-NTD-03-two-stage
   - Accelerating development of prophylactic vaccines against Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs) in sub-Saharan Africa
   - Tipo: Research and Innovation Action (RIA)

OBJETIVOS
Desenvolver vacinas e terapeuticas inovadoras para tuberculose, malaria e doencas tropicais negligenciadas, bem como acoes de coordenacao para fortalecer o ambiente de investigacao clinica em Africa.

ELEGIBILIDADE
- Consorcio multinacional com parceiros de paises europeus e africanos
- Instituicoes de investigacao, universidades, hospitais, empresas farmaceuticas
- Foco geografico: Sub-Saharan Africa para as intervencoes

KEYWORDS
Global Health, EDCTP3, Tuberculosis, Malaria, Neglected Tropical Diseases, Vaccine Development, Clinical Research, Africa, Sub-Saharan, Horizon Europe
