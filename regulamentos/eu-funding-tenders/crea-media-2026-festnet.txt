CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-FESTNET
TITLE: Networks of European Festivals
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Project Grants (CREA-PJG) - CREA Action Grant Budget-Based (CREA-AG)
BUDGET: 6,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 16 April 2026, 17:00 CEST (closed - 43 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Results notification October 2026; grant agreement January 2027.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative aims to bolster coordinated activities among European audiovisual festivals. Key goals include:
- Strengthen cooperation among festival networks screening substantial quantities of non-national European films
- Expand audience reach for European audiovisual works across the continent
- Promote knowledge exchange and best-practice sharing for festival cooperation
- Leverage digital transformation and develop online tools for enhanced distribution

DESCRIPTION:
Deadline model: single-stage submission. Projects should foster collaboration between European film festivals to increase the reach and impact of European cinema. Activities must address sustainability and diversity priorities.

KEY DATES:
- Call published: 4 December 2025
- Submission deadline: 16 April 2026
- Evaluation period: April-September 2026
- Results notification: October 2026
- Grant agreement signature: January 2027

ELIGIBILITY:
Applicants must review detailed eligibility requirements covering admissibility, eligible countries, participant types, financial and operational capacity, and exclusion criteria.
