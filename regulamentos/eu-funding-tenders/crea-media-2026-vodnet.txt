CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-VODNET
TITLE: European VOD Networks and Operators
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Project Grants - Budget-Based (CREA-PJG)
BUDGET: 8,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 8 April 2026, 17:00 CET (closed - 21 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Evaluation results September 2026; grant agreements January 2027.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative aims to support European Video on Demand platforms that feature substantial non-national European content, enhancing their competitive position and market appeal. Expected outcomes include:
- Strengthening cross-border collaboration between VOD platforms
- Boosting platform attractiveness for international audiences
- Improving visibility and discoverability of European audiovisual content
- Developing sustainable business models with economies of scale

DESCRIPTION:
Applicants should propose joint/collaborative actions across borders to improve VOD platform competitiveness while increasing accessibility of European content globally. Projects must incorporate strategies for environmental sustainability, gender balance, and inclusion/diversity.

ELIGIBILITY:
Full eligibility criteria including admissibility requirements, eligible countries, financial/operational capacity assessments, and exclusion conditions are detailed in the official call document.

KEY DATES:
- Submission deadline: 8 April 2026
- Evaluation results: September 2026
- Grant agreements: January 2027
