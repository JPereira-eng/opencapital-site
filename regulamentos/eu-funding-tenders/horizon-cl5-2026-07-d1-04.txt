HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-04
Fighting Disinformation and Communicating Effectively on Climate Change

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07
TOPIC: HORIZON-CL5-2026-07-D1-04
STATUS: Open
PROGRAM: Horizon Europe - Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness - Climate, Energy and Mobility - Climate Science and Solutions
ACTION TYPE: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions)
FUNDING MODEL: Lump sum grants (HORIZON-AG-LS)

DEADLINE: 21 April 2026, 17:00 CET (postponed twice due to technical issues)
PLANNED OPENING DATE: 27 September 2025

BUDGET:
- This topic (D1-04): EUR 15,000,000
- Expected grants: 3
- Grant range: EUR 4,000,000 - EUR 5,000,000 per project
- Total call (all D1 topics): EUR 82,000,000

OBJECTIVES - EXPECTED OUTCOMES:
1. Advanced knowledge and understanding on the dynamics of disinformation affecting climate policy from communication perspectives
2. Tools and products developed for public authorities, media and civil society to detect and combat climate-related disinformation at scale
3. Tailored, innovative and effective communication techniques, tools and materials tested with real-world contexts
4. Strengthened acceptance of climate action through improved trust in science and climate literacy

SCOPE:
- Understand sources, channels, and influence of climate misinformation/disinformation
- Investigate AI role in information spread
- Develop detection and monitoring strategies for public/private sectors
- Explore pathways from climate knowledge to behavioral change
- Design targeted campaigns addressing climate anxiety while countering false information
- Interdisciplinary approach required: natural sciences, social sciences, humanities, behavioral sciences, gender studies, and media studies
- SME participation strongly encouraged
- Geographic focus: primarily Europe with international cooperation encouraged, especially with US and low/middle-income countries

DESPESAS ELEGIVEIS / ELEGIBILIDADE:
- Countries: EU member states and associated countries (Annex B compliance)
- Admissibility criteria per Annex A and Annex E
- Financial and operational capacity per Annex C
- Lump sum justifications required for cost categories C/D exceeding 15% of personnel costs
- Must collaborate and envisage clustering activities with other funded projects and EU Missions on Climate Change Adaptation and Climate-Neutral Smart Cities

RECENT UPDATES:
- Deadline postponed twice due to technical issues (latest: 16 April to 21 April 2026)
- Lump sum budget justification guidance issued 10 February 2026
