## Description

The call supports operational establishment of approximately 14 Cross-Border Coordination Points under EU Regulation 2025/925. Neighboring Member States may create joint CBCPs, with only one co-creator state required to submit acknowledgment.

Eligible activities include:
- Real estate rental or purchase
- Digital equipment and systems
- Furniture
- Office supplies
- Studies and consulting services
- Web design and development
- Legal consulting
- Legal assessment of files
- Communication activities related to CBCPs
