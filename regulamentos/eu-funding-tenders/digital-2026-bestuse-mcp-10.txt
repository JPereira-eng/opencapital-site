DESCRICAO:
The initiative establishes a hub providing comprehensive support to European Digital Infrastructure Consortia (EDICs). Key functions include legal and operational guidance, data repository management, coordination between EDICs, community-building activities, annual gatherings, and impact reporting. The hub aims to ensure EDICs long-term sustainability while strengthening governance compliance with EU digital policy objectives.

FUNCOES PRINCIPAIS DO HUB:
- Legal and operational guidance to EDICs
- Data repository management
- Coordination between EDICs
- Community-building activities and annual gatherings
- Impact reporting
- Ensuring long-term sustainability and governance compliance
