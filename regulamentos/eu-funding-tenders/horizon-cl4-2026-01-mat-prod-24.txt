Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 800.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 1
- Contribution: 800.000 EUR (fixed)

Description:
The initiative strengthens EU-Japan cooperation in advanced materials through the EU-Japan Enhanced Dialogue launched in April 2024. It enables European researchers from Member States and Associated Countries to conduct research visits to Japanese institutions, fostering bilateral collaboration in strategic materials research.

Key Objectives:
- Strengthen EU-Japan collaboration under the EU-Japan Enhanced Dialogue (launched April 2024)
- Fund travel grants for European researchers to conduct research visits to Japanese institutions
- Focus on advanced materials with applications in mobility, energy, construction, electronics, or medical devices
- Ensure broad geographical coverage across eligible EU Member States and Associated Countries
- Support visits typically up to one month duration

Target Research Areas:
- Mobility (automotive, aerospace)
- Energy (storage, conversion, generation)
- Construction materials
- Electronics and semiconductors
- Medical devices and biomedical materials

Eligibility:
- Participants from EU Member States and Associated Countries
- Researchers visiting Japanese research groups and institutions
- Lump sum contributions per Decision of July 7, 2021
- Budget justification required for cost categories C/D exceeding 15% of personnel costs
