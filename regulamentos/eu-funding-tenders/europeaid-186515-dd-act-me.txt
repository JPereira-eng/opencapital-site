EuropeAid/186515/DD/ACT/ME - Civil Society Engagement in Public Policy, Local Development and EU Integration (Montenegro)

Title: Civil Society Engagement in Public Policy, Local Development and the EU Integration Process
Reference: EuropeAid/186515/DD/ACT/ME
Programme: Civil Society Facility and Media Programmes for Montenegro (CSF) 2024-2025, IPA III instrument
Source: EU Delegation Montenegro announcement
Original call URL: https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/screen/opportunities/topic-details/186515PROSPECTSEN

OVERVIEW
The EU Delegation to Montenegro has launched a call for proposals to strengthen participatory democracy and the EU integration process in Montenegro through enhanced contribution by civil society organisations (CSOs). The call promotes inclusive and gender-sensitive participation of CSOs in public policy processes, including those related to EU accession.

KEY DEADLINES AND CALENDAR
- Full application submission deadline: 28 July 2026 at 14:00 (Brussels date and time).
- Information sessions: published on the EU Delegation Montenegro website and EEAS pages, typically May-June 2026.
- Two-stage process: concept notes evaluated first, full applications requested from shortlisted applicants.

INDICATIVE BUDGET
Total envelope: EUR 2,250,000.
Divided into 3 lots of EUR 750,000 each.

LOTS / FUNDING STREAMS
- Lot 1: Strengthening democratic participation and CSO contribution to public policy processes (national and local).
- Lot 2: Local development, anti-corruption and accountability initiatives, with focus on EU integration chapters (Chapters 23-24 - rule of law).
- Lot 3: Inclusive and gender-sensitive participation of marginalised groups (women, youth, minorities, persons with disabilities) in democratic decision-making.

GLOBAL OBJECTIVE
Strengthen participatory democracy and the EU integration process in Montenegro through an enhanced contribution by civil society. Specific focus: strengthening CSO contribution to public policy processes (including EU accession) by promoting inclusive, gender-sensitive participation.

ELIGIBILITY (LEAD APPLICANTS)
- Non-profit civil society organisations (NGOs, foundations, networks, professional associations).
- Established in: Montenegro, EU 27 Member States, EEA, IPA III beneficiaries (Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kosovo, North Macedonia, Serbia, Turkey), beneficiaries listed in Regulation (EU) 2021/947 Annex I.
- Must have at least 2 years of relevant experience in democratic governance, civic engagement or EU integration topics.
- Cannot apply as a natural person.

CO-APPLICANTS
At least one Montenegrin co-applicant required. International applicants strongly encouraged to partner with local Montenegrin CSOs to ensure local impact and sustainability.

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES
- Policy research, analysis and monitoring on EU integration chapters.
- CSO advocacy at national and local level for democratic reforms.
- Capacity building of grassroots and rural CSOs.
- Civic education on EU values and accession process.
- Watchdog activities on rule of law, anti-corruption, public administration reform.
- Promotion of inclusive participation: women in decision-making, youth engagement, minorities representation.
- Cross-sector dialogue platforms (CSO-government-business-academia).
- Local development pilots demonstrating EU integration benefits.

ELIGIBLE COSTS
Standard IPA III rules: staff, travel, equipment and supplies, local office, services, sub-granting (up to 30% of EU contribution), audit, visibility/communication, indirect costs (flat rate up to 7%).

CO-FINANCING
EU contribution typically up to 90% of eligible costs. Applicants must contribute at least 10% from own or other non-EU sources.

PROJECT DURATION
Typically 24-36 months.

APPLICATION PROCESS
- Online submission via PROSPECT platform of the EU Funding & Tenders Portal mandatory.
- Languages: English (Montenegrin annexes accepted for local context).
- Two-stage: concept note (mandatory), then full application for invited.
- Evaluation conducted by EU Delegation Montenegro evaluation committee.

EVALUATION CRITERIA
Concept notes: relevance to call objectives and EU integration priorities (35-40 points), design and methodology (20-25), feasibility and sustainability (20), cost-effectiveness (15-20).

CONTACT
EU Delegation to Montenegro, Podgorica. Reference EuropeAid/186515/DD/ACT/ME in all correspondence. Specific call mailbox published on Delegation website.

DOCUMENTS
Full Guidelines for Applicants, annexes (concept note template, full application template, budget, logical framework, declarations) available via EU Funding & Tenders Portal under reference 186515.

NOTES
This call is the 2026 cycle of the long-running Civil Society Facility for Montenegro under IPA III. It complements parallel programmes such as CERV 2026 (citizens engagement) and ReLOaD3 (Regional Local Democracy in the Western Balkans), all aligned with the EU enlargement agenda and Montenegro's accession negotiations.
