CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-CULT-2026-LIT
TITLE: Circulation of European literary works
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA2027) - Creative Europe Division
ACTION TYPE: CREA Lump Sum Grants (CREA-LS)
BUDGET: 5,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINE: 29 January 2026, 17:00:00 CET (closed - 201 proposals submitted)
STATUS: Closed. Evaluation results July 2026; grant agreement signature October 2026.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative seeks to advance transnational circulation and the diversity of European literary works through coordinated activities. Priorities include promoting reading and linguistic diversity, supporting literary translators with fair compensation (recommended 20-30% of budget allocation), and enhancing book sector competitiveness through value-chain collaboration.

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
Applications must involve translation, publication, distribution, and promotion of European fiction works. Projects may be implemented by single entities or multi-beneficiary consortiums. The call targets approximately 40 supported projects.

DESCRIPTION:
Projects must address EU priorities including digital transition, greening, inclusion, and gender equality. Special emphasis on works in less-widely translated languages and Ukrainian literature support following exceptional provisions for Ukraine assistance.

CONDITIONS:
- Part B application limit: maximum 70 pages
- Evaluation period: February-May 2026
- Grant agreement signature: October 2026
- Evaluation results: July 2026
