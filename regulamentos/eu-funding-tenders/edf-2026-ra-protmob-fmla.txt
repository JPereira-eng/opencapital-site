EDF-2026-RA-PROTMOB-FMLA - Future multirole light aircraft

Title: Future multirole light aircraft
Call Identifier: EDF-2026-RA
Topic Identifier: EDF-2026-RA-PROTMOB-FMLA
Programme: European Defence Fund (EDF)
Status: Open
Type of Action: EDF-RA EDF Research Actions
Model Grant Agreement: EDF Action Grant Budget-Based (EDF-AG)

Indicative Budget: EUR 15,000,000
Number of Proposals: Several proposals may be funded
Deadline Date: 19 December 2026

Description:

There is a need for a new multirole light aircraft bridging the gap between today's battlefield and modern technology, as most EU fleets in this segment are aging. The FMLA concept must not duplicate or fragment existing turboprop and tactical military transport aircraft systems. By leveraging existing European platforms, technologies and innovations, this research reduces risks in developing FMLA capabilities for air-to-ground operations. FMLA should be effective in light attack, anti-drone interception and close air support roles.

The objective is developing a low-cost, single-engine turboprop aircraft operating in various environments, providing direct air support, ground targeting, intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance with combat elements, and air strike coordination with air traffic control support in forward positions during counter-terrorism missions.

This topic contributes to STEP objectives in defence technologies. Targeted activities include studies and design with upstream and downstream research-eligible activities.
