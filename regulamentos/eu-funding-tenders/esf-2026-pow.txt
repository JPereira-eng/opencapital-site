ESF-2026-POW - Posting of workers: enhancing administrative cooperation and access to information

Title: Posting of workers: enhancing administrative cooperation and access to information
Call Identifier: ESF-2026-POW
Programme: European Social Fund Plus (ESF+)
Status: Open
Type of Action: ESF Project Grants (ESF-PJG)
Model Grant Agreement: ESF Action Grant Budget-Based (ESF-AG)

Budget: EUR 4,000,000 for 2026
Submission Deadline: 6 October 2026, 17:00 CET

Description:

This call supports implementation of European Pillar of Social Rights principles including equal opportunities, secure employment, wages, employment condition information, social dialogue, and safe work environments.

General objective: contribute to the implementation of the European Pillar of Social Rights regarding posted workers.

Specific objectives:
- Promote transnational cooperation among public authorities and stakeholders, including IMI use and best practice sharing
- Increase accessibility, transparency and quality of information concerning employment terms and conditions and Member State monitoring/enforcement practices under the Posting of Workers Directive
- Promote evidence collection through original data and analysis of posting processes and working conditions, including university collaboration across disciplines, research centers, and stakeholders, particularly social partners

Expected results include developing or improving exchanges, peer reviews, training programs between public officials and social partners for monitoring compliance and enforcement, plus developing or improving online platforms distributing quality, user-friendly information for posted workers.

Award Criteria & Scoring:
- Relevance: 28/40 minimum
- Quality (Design/Implementation): 14/20 minimum
- Quality (Team/Cooperation): 14/20 minimum
- Impact: 14/20 minimum
- Overall threshold: 70/100 points

Application Requirements: Maximum 70 pages; electronic submission required; must include Part A and Part B application forms plus supporting documents as PDFs.
