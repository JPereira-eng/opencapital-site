DESCRIPTION:
The topic targets enhanced material recycling and upcycling, reduced EU import dependencies, minimized resource usage, and cost-efficient operations across the circular economy value chain. The scope addresses value chain optimization for secondary resource recovery, technology development from waste collection through material production, and integration across industrial sectors.

Proposals must address at least 3 of the following 7 points:
1. Sustainable feed streams for secondary raw materials recovery
2. Product design improvements for end-of-life recyclability
3. Enhanced collection and sorting technologies
4. Secondary raw material recovery from complex waste streams
5. Critical material reduction or substitution
6. Manufacturing efficiency improvements
7. Contaminant understanding and removal

Laboratory-scale to pilot demonstrations required. At minimum, two highest-ranked proposals focusing on iron-containing residuals via low-CO2 processes shall be awarded if evaluation thresholds are met.

ELIGIBILITY:
Described in Work Programme General Annexes Annex B. Non-EU/Associated Country provisions available. Entities from EU Member States, associated countries, and relevant third countries may participate.
