PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - EU-RAIL Joint Undertaking
STATUS: Open (2025 call)

ABOUT EU-RAIL JOINT UNDERTAKING
---------------------------------
The EU-RAIL Joint Undertaking was established under Horizon Europe to accelerate the development and deployment of an innovative, competitive and sustainable European rail system. It brings together the European Commission, the rail industry, and research organisations.

EU-RAIL aims to develop the technologies and operational concepts needed to:
- Transform the European rail system into a truly integrated, interoperable network
- Increase rail capacity and reliability
- Reduce costs and environmental impact
- Improve passenger experience and safety

CALL FOCUS: SYSTEM PILLAR DEMONSTRATIONS
-----------------------------------------
The EU-RAIL JU Call 2025-02 focuses on large-scale research and innovation actions requiring real-world demonstration in actual operational rail environments.

Projects are expected to involve technology providers led by experienced rail stakeholders with proven capacity to manage large, multi-partner European projects.

CONSORTIUM REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------
- Rail operators must play a central role as consortium leaders or core partners
- Rail operators' involvement is essential to define real operational needs
- Operators must provide access to rolling stock in service
- Projects must enable large-scale demonstrations under real passenger conditions
- Multi-country consortia expected for European interoperability goals

RESEARCH AREAS
--------------
- Environmental monitoring and air quality in rail environments (air quality and health monitoring in trains, stations and tunnels)
- AI-driven predictive modelling for rail operations
- Indoor comfort conditions assessment for passenger well-being
- Rail safety and resilience technologies
- Smart mobility services and integrated ticketing
- Sustainable rail traction and energy efficiency
- Digital rail systems and automation
- Noise and vibration reduction

ELIGIBLE PROJECT TYPES
-----------------------
Research and Innovation Actions (RIA) and Innovation Actions (IA), with emphasis on large-scale real-world demonstrations.

ELIGIBLE APPLICANTS
-------------------
Organisations established in EU Member States and Horizon Europe associated countries. Rail operators, technology providers, research institutions, and SMEs with relevant expertise. Minimum consortium sizes apply per topic.
