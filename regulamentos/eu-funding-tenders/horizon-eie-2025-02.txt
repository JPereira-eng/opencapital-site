CALL: HORIZON-EIE-2025-02
NAME: Interconnected Innovation Ecosystems (2025.2)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - European Innovation Ecosystems (EIE)
MANAGED BY: EISMEA (European Innovation Council and SMEs Executive Agency)
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
The HORIZON-EIE-2025-02 call encompasses topics under the "Interconnected Innovation Ecosystems" (CONNECT) destination. The aim is to foster efficient, open, inclusive and interconnected European innovation ecosystems in line with the New European Innovation Agenda, creating connected regional innovation valleys across the EU.

TOPICS:

TOPIC 1 - HORIZON-EIE-2025-02-CONNECT-01:
Preparatory action for setting up joint programmes among innovation ecosystems actors.
National, regional and/or local authorities in charge of innovation policies and programmes from Member States, Associated Countries and/or their regions, and other research and innovation actors, prepare joint multi-annual programmes of activities and action plans to strengthen the performance and capacity of their innovation ecosystems and their alignment towards EU-level priorities.
Budget: EUR 5 million
Deadline: 15 October 2025, 17:00 CEST

TOPIC 2 - HORIZON-EIE-2025-02-CONNECT-02:
Implementing co-funded action plans for connected regional innovation valleys.
Fosters the creation of efficient, open, inclusive and interconnected European innovation ecosystems, intended to create connected regional innovation valleys across the EU, involving regions with lower innovation performances, building on strategic areas of regional strength and specialisation.
Deadline: 15 October 2025, 17:00 CEST

ELIGIBILITY:
- National, regional and/or local authorities in charge of innovation policies and programmes from EU Member States and Associated Countries
- Other research and innovation actors and stakeholders
- Consortia involving multiple countries/regions
- For CONNECT-02: Must include at least one region with lower innovation performance

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Preparation of joint multi-annual programmes and action plans
- Capacity building for innovation ecosystem actors
- Cross-regional collaboration and co-investment mechanisms
- Alignment with New European Innovation Agenda priorities
- Programme-level coordination across national and regional boundaries

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Excellence and innovative approach
- Quality and efficiency of implementation
- Potential for impact and scale-up
- Involvement of lower-performing innovation regions
- Co-funding and leverage from participating authorities
