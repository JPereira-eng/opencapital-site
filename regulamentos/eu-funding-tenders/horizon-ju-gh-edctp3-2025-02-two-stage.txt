INSTRUMENTO: Global Health EDCTP3 - Fellowship Call 2025-02 (Two-Stage)
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-JU-GH-EDCTP3-2025-02-two-stage
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - Global Health EDCTP3 Joint Undertaking
AGENCIA: Global Health EDCTP3 JU (coordinated by EDCTP Association)
STATUS: Closed (Stage 2 deadline September 2025)

DEADLINE STAGE 1: 20 March 2025, 17:00 Brussels time
DEADLINE STAGE 2: 2 September 2025, 17:00 Brussels time
MODELO: Two-stage submission

OBJETIVO:
Establish an African cohort of epidemiologists, biostatisticians and mathematical modellers to address infectious disease challenges and support Africa CDC's public health workforce development framework.

DESCRICAO:
The call supports institutions in sub-Saharan Africa and Europe that provide Master's training in epidemiology and biostatistics, or those that process public health data with advanced quantitative methods to inform policy. It is part of the Africa CDC's framework for public health workforce development.

ATIVIDADES SUPORTADAS:
- Master's training programmes in epidemiology and biostatistics in sub-Saharan Africa
- Training in quantitative methods for public health data analysis
- Fellowship exchanges between African and European institutions
- Capacity building for mathematical modelling of disease outbreaks
- Science-policy interface activities for evidence-based health decision making

CONTEXTO:
Global Health EDCTP3 invests in global health through funding collaborative research and innovation projects tackling infectious diseases and supporting activities for research capacity building in Africa. The program brings together EU Member States and African countries to address health challenges through joint research funding.

PAPEL DA EDCTP ASSOCIATION:
As coordinator, the EDCTP Association facilitates communication between consortia and Global Health EDCTP3, distributes payments to beneficiaries, submits required reports and ensures coordination with the Scientific Project Leader.

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Institutions in sub-Saharan African countries and EU Member States or Associated Countries. Consortia required with African lead or strong African participation.
