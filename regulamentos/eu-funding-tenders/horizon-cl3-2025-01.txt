INSTRUMENTO: Civil Security for Society (Cluster 3 - 2025)
CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL3-2025-01
PROGRAMA: Horizon Europe - Cluster 3: Civil Security for Society
AGENCIA: REA (Research Executive Agency)
STATUS: Call for proposals published (opening June 2025)

ABERTURA DAS CANDIDATURAS: 12 June 2025
INFO DAY: 11-12 June 2025 (Brussels and online)

OBJETIVO:
Cluster 3 Civil Security for Society responds to the challenges arising from persistent security threats, including cybercrime, as well as natural and human-made disasters. The 2025 calls for proposals cover key areas for making Europe more resilient, safe and secure.

AREAS TEMATICAS COBERTAS:
1. Better Protect the EU and its Citizens against Crime and Terrorism
   - Tools and technologies for cross-border prevention and responses to crime and terrorism

2. Effective Management of External Borders
   - Innovation for border security and management

3. Resilient Infrastructures
   - Protection of critical infrastructure against threats

4. A Disaster Resilient Society for Europe
   - Testing/validating tools and technologies for cross-border prevention and responses to climate extreme and geological events
   - Advancing autonomous systems and robotics for disaster response

5. Strengthened Security Research and Innovation (SSRI)
   - Coordination and support activities for security research

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Entities established in EU Member States and Horizon Europe Associated Countries. Consortia typically required. Full eligibility in the Horizon Europe 2025 Work Programme document (wp-6-civil-security-for-society_horizon-2025_en.pdf).
