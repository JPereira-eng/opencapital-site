DESCRICAO:
The initiative aims to develop competitions serving as catalysts for engaging young European people in cutting edge digital technologies. Student teams will compete across six digital areas: Quantum, GenAI, Chips, Virtual Worlds, Destination Earth Initiative, and one consortium-selected area.

ENTREGAVEIS ESPERADOS:
- Well-defined, relevant challenges for competitions
- Competition rulebook for participants and jury
- Implementation roadmap
- Six operational competitions
- Post-project sustainability plans

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Detailed eligibility requirements, country restrictions, financial capacity assessments, and operational conditions are specified in Sections 6-7 of the official call document.
