HORIZON-JU-SNS-2025-01 - Smart Networks and Services Joint Undertaking Call 2025-01
====================================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-JU-SNS-2025-01
FULL NAME: SNS JU Call for Proposals 2025 - Stream D: Trials and Pilots with Verticals
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - Smart Networks and Services Joint Undertaking (SNS JU)
STATUS: Closed (Deadline: 18 September 2025 at 17:00 CET)

ABOUT SNS JOINT UNDERTAKING
-----------------------------
The Smart Networks and Services Joint Undertaking (SNS JU) is a public-private partnership under Horizon Europe focusing on 5G and 6G research and innovation in Europe. SNS JU is the EU's main vehicle for accelerating 6G development and deployment to ensure European technological leadership in next-generation communications.

SNS JU 2025 FUNDING OVERVIEW
-------------------------------
Total EU public investment for 2025: EUR 128 million
- Streams B and C (already allocated): EUR 104 million
- Stream D (this call): EUR 24 million EU funding

STREAM D: TRIALS AND PILOTS WITH VERTICALS
-------------------------------------------
Stream D specifically targets field trials and validation pilots demonstrating 5G Advanced and 6G technologies in real operational environments across key vertical sectors.

Budget: EUR 24 million EU funding

Opening date: 18 June 2025
Deadline: 18 September 2025 at 17:00 CET

VERTICAL SECTORS TARGETED
--------------------------
1. Industry and Manufacturing - Factory automation, connected manufacturing, smart production
2. Media - Immersive content delivery, remote production, live events
3. Transport and Logistics - Connected vehicles, smart logistics, autonomous transport
4. Emergency and Safety Services - Mission-critical communications, public safety
5. Healthcare - Remote patient monitoring, connected health services, telemedicine

CALL OBJECTIVES
----------------
- Validate 5G Advanced and 6G technologies and solutions in real operational environments
- Explore viable business models to monetise 6G services and applications
- Address sector-specific "pain points" and adoption barriers
- Co-develop and demonstrate solutions paving the way for commercial deployment
- Build industrial ecosystem partnerships between technology developers and vertical sector stakeholders

EXPECTED OUTCOMES
-----------------
- Field-validated demonstrations of advanced connectivity technologies
- Quantified business case assessments for 6G services in target verticals
- Documented lessons learned for future 6G standardisation and commercialisation
- Strengthened European technology partnerships across sectors

CONSORTIUM REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------
Consortia must include both connectivity technology providers and vertical sector stakeholders (end-users). Multi-country European consortia preferred.

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
Organisations established in EU Member States and SNS JU participating countries, including Portugal.

CONTEXT: SNS JU BROADER 2025 INITIATIVE
-----------------------------------------
The 2025 SNS JU call package (EUR 128M total) complements prior investments:
- 35 projects selected worth EUR 250 million in prior rounds
- Europe has committed EUR 130 million in 6G research in recent funding rounds
