CALL: HORIZON-HLTH-2025-03-two-stage
NAME: Cluster 1 - Health (Two stage - 2025)
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - Cluster 1: Health
MANAGED BY: HaDEA (European Health and Digital Executive Agency)
SHARD: eu-horizon
DETECTED: 2026-04-26

DESCRIPTION:
HORIZON-HLTH-2025-03-two-stage is the two-stage submission call within Cluster 1 (Health) of the Horizon Europe 2025 Work Programme. It covers research and innovation topics focused on health outcomes, disease prevention, and innovative medical interventions.

TOPICS INCLUDED:

1. Improving the quality of life of persons with intellectual disabilities and their families
   Budget: EUR 40 million (indicative ~EUR 6-8 million per project, 5 projects expected)
   First deadline: 16 September 2025; Second stage: 16 April 2026

2. The impact of pollution on the development and progression of brain diseases and disorders
   Budget: EUR 40 million (indicative ~EUR 6-7 million per project)
   First deadline: 16 September 2025; Second stage: 16 April 2026

3. Advancing knowledge on the impacts of micro- and nanoplastics on human health
   Budget: EUR 40 million (indicative ~EUR 7-8 million per project)
   First deadline: 16 September 2025; Second stage: 16 April 2026

4. Advancing innovative interventions for mental, behavioural and neurodevelopmental disorders
   First deadline: 16 September 2025; Second stage: 16 April 2026

5. Facilitating the conduct of multinational clinical studies of orphan devices and/or of highly innovative breakthrough devices
   Budget: EUR 40 million (indicative ~EUR 6-8 million per project)
   First deadline: 16 September 2025; Second stage: 16 April 2026

TWO-STAGE PROCESS:
Stage 1: Short outline proposals evaluated on Excellence and Impact
Stage 2: Full proposals invited for selected outlines, evaluated on all criteria
Stage 1 deadline: 16 September 2025, 17:00 CEST
Stage 2 deadline: 16 April 2026, 17:00 CEST

TOTAL BUDGET:
Approximately EUR 200 million across all topics in HORIZON-HLTH-2025-03.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Universities, research institutes, hospitals, clinical centres, SMEs, industry
- Established in EU Member States or countries associated to Horizon Europe
- Consortium required: minimum 3 independent legal entities from 3 different countries
- For clinical studies: patient associations and ethics board involvement recommended

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
- Research on disability and quality of life interventions
- Environmental health and exposure research
- Clinical trials and observational studies
- Development of novel therapeutic or diagnostic approaches
- Multinational clinical research infrastructure
- Development of medical devices (orphan/breakthrough)

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Excellence: scientific quality, innovative aspects, soundness of methodology
- Impact: significance and relevance, dissemination and exploitation measures
- Quality and efficiency of implementation: consortium quality, resources allocation
