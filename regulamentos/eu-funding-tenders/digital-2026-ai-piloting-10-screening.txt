DIGITAL-2026-AI-PILOTING-10-SCREENING
Apply AI: Piloting AI-based image screening in medical centres

Call Identifier: DIGITAL-2026-AI-PILOTING-10
Programme: Digital Europe Programme (2021-2027)
Type of Action: DIGITAL-SIMPLE (Simple Grants)
Deadline: 2026-10-02

Budget Overview:
- 2027: 9,000,000 EUR
- Expected Grants: 2

Keywords: Artificial Intelligence and Decision support, Artificial intelligence, intelligent systems, Biomedical software, Cloud Services, Cloud/Edge/Virtualisation, Health and Ecosystem Services, Healthcare, Health data, Health information, Health sciences, Healthy lifestyle, Technological innovation, eHealth, GenAI, Medical, Medical imaging devices

Description:
Expected outcomes include proofs-of-concept of replicable, scalable cloud-based AI/GenAI systems for analysis of medical imaging data in healthcare settings.

Key objectives:
- Implement scalable cloud-based AI/GenAI systems with trustworthy and safe AI algorithms
- Develop, train, and validate algorithms using large patient data sets
- Focus on secure, privacy-preserving cloud environments with appropriate access and identification tools
- Ensure seamless integration in clinical workflows
- Perform technical and clinical validation generating evidence on AI system performance and usability in different clinical settings
- Deploy AI/GenAI solutions tested and validated within TEF for Health where relevant

Disease areas of focus:
- Cancer
- Cardiovascular diseases

Expected deliverables:
- One or more proofs-of-concept of replicable, scalable AI/GenAI systems for medical image screening
- Evidence-based cost-efficiency analysis
- Two or more annual events/workshops per project
- Sustainability plan for continued deployment
- Detailed data protection and cybersecurity plan
- Regulatory approval plan (including MDR compliance where applicable)

Technical requirements:
- Scalable cloud-based architecture
- AI/GenAI algorithms trained on large patient datasets
- Privacy-preserving data handling
- Secure access and identification mechanisms
- Clinical workflow integration
- Interoperability with existing healthcare IT systems
