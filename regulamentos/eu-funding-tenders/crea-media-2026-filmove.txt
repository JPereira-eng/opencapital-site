CALL IDENTIFIER: CREA-MEDIA-2026-FILMOVE
TITLE: Films on the Move
PROGRAMME: Creative Europe Programme (CREA) 2021-2027
ACTION TYPE: CREA Project Grants (CREA-PJG) - CREA Action Grant Budget-Based
BUDGET: 21,000,000 EUR (2026)
DEADLINES:
- 1st cut-off: 19 March 2026, 17:00 CET (22 proposals submitted)
- 2nd cut-off: 16 July 2026, 17:00 CET (open)
STATUS: 2nd cut-off open.

OBJECTIVES:
The initiative seeks to encourage and support the wider distribution of recent non-national European films by engaging sales agents and theatrical distributors in promoting and distributing non-national European content across borders.

EXPECTED RESULTS:
- Development of pan-European theatrical and/or online distribution strategies
- Increased investment in promotion and distribution to expand audience reach
- Strengthened links between production and distribution sectors

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES:
Campaigns for pan-European theatrical and/or online distribution of eligible European films, coordinated by sales agents. Proposals must address sustainability, environmental responsibility, and ensure gender balance, inclusion, diversity and representativeness.

ELIGIBILITY:
Detailed in the call document; partner search permitted. Full eligibility criteria cover admissible countries, financial capacity, and exclusion grounds.
