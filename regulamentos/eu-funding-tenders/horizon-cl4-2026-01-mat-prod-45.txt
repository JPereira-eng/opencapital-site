HORIZON-CL4-2026-01-MAT-PROD-45
Pilot access schemes to Technology Infrastructures for European startups, scaleups and innovative SMEs (CSA)

Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2026-01
Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Programme Division: Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness / Digital, Industry and Space (HORIZON.2.4)
Action Type: HORIZON Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)
MGA Type: HORIZON Lump Sum Grant (HORIZON-AG-LS)
Status: Open
Deadline: 2026-04-21

Budget Overview (2026):
- Total: 5.000.000 EUR
- Expected grants: 2
- Contribution per project: 2.500.000 EUR (approx.)

Description:
A proposta visa desenvolver e testar esquemas piloto de acesso multi-site para startups, scaleups e PMEs inovadoras, envolvendo um conjunto critico de operadores de Infraestruturas Tecnologicas em areas prioritarias: materiais avancados, energia limpa, saude e biotecnologia.

Key Objectives:
- Understand specific needs of industrial users for technology infrastructure services
- Create expanded opportunities for development, testing, and deployment of new technologies
- Strengthen innovation capacity of European SMEs, startups, and scaleups
- Develop attractive and effective access schemes for technology infrastructures

Scope:
- Establishment of a centrally managed and funded pilot access program
- Simplified and standardized access conditions applicable across the EU
- Activities to increase visibility and promote use of technology infrastructures
- Transnational collaboration and best practice sharing among infrastructure operators
- Focus areas: advanced materials, clean energy, health and biotechnology

Target Beneficiaries:
- Startups and scaleups at early and growth stages
- Innovative SMEs seeking access to specialized research infrastructure
- Companies in advanced materials, clean energy, health, and biotechnology sectors

Conditions:
- Admissibility per Annexes A-G of Work Programme General Annexes
- Lump sum grant model per Decision of July 7, 2021
- Financial and operational capacity validation required
- Evaluation per award criteria in Work Programme
