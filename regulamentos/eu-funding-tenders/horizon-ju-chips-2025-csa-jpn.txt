Eligibility Requirements
Minimum three independent institutions required from three different EU Member States or Associated Countries, with at least one from an EU Member State.

Japanese entities may participate but cannot receive EU funding; they must secure alternative or own financing.

Eligibility details are outlined in Appendix 5 of the Chips JU Multi Annual Work Programme.

Key Objectives and Expected Outcomes
The action supports joint R&I activities between the EU and Japan advancing semiconductor cooperation, including:

- Talent exchanges between R&D or production sites in both regions
- Networking and conferences connecting semiconductor companies across supply chains
- Life cycle assessment coordination for semiconductor manufacturing processes
- Researcher exchange programs addressing comparable research priorities
- Joint workshops and dissemination events for technology transfer

Scope
Activities include organising networks, conferences, and workshops facilitating EU-Japan cooperation in semiconductor research and manufacturing. The action coordinates parallel research initiatives and engages stakeholders through workshops and dissemination efforts.

The call reflects the strategic importance of semiconductor supply chain resilience following global chip shortages. It supports implementation of the European Chips Act by strengthening international technology partnerships.
