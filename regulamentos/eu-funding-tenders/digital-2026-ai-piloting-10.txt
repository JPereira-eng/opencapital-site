INSTRUMENTO: DIGITAL-2026-AI-PILOTING-10-SCREENING
NOME: Apply AI: Piloting AI-based image screening in medical centres
PROGRAMA: Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL)
CALL IDENTIFIER: DIGITAL-2026-AI-PILOTING-10
FONTE: EU Funding & Tenders Portal

DOTACAO: EUR 9,000,000 (2027)
GRANTS ESPERADOS: aproximadamente 2

PRAZO: October 31, 2026
DATA DE ABERTURA: April 6, 2026

TIPO DE ACAO: DIGITAL Simple Grants (Budget-Based Action Grant)
PROCEDIMENTO: Single-stage submission

DESCRICAO:
The initiative targets scalable cloud-based AI/GenAI systems running European trustworthy and safe AI/GenAI algorithms. Projects must demonstrate integration of cloud-based systems in real healthcare settings, focusing on cancer and cardiovascular disease screening with clinical validation.

RESULTADOS ESPERADOS:
- Proof-of-concept AI systems for medical imaging analysis
- Cost-efficiency evidence for regulatory/HTA purposes
- Minimum two annual workshops per project for European screening network
- Regulatory approval plan and data protection/cybersecurity framework
- Sustainability and stakeholder engagement plans

AREAS DE APLICACAO:
- Cancer screening (medical imaging)
- Cardiovascular disease screening
- Clinical validation in real healthcare settings

ELEGIBILIDADE:
Legal entity validation, financial capacity assessment, and compliance with EU frameworks required. Details in section 6 of the call document.
