HORIZON-CL6-2026-03-two-stage - Cluster 6 Food Bioeconomy Natural Resources Call 2026
======================================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-CL6-2026-03-two-stage
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe
CLUSTER: Cluster 6 - Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment
MANAGING AGENCY: REA (Research Executive Agency)
STATUS: Two-stage call

DEADLINES
---------
Stage 1 (Outline Application): 16 April 2026
Stage 2 (Full Proposal): 23 September 2026

THEMATIC FOCUS
--------------
This two-stage call is part of Cluster 6 (Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment) under the Horizon Europe Work Programme 2026-2027. It covers a broad range of food systems, circular economy, biodiversity, and environmental topics aligned with the EU Green Deal and Farm to Fork Strategy.

COVERED AREAS
-------------
Cluster 6 two-stage calls for 2026 include topics across:
- Circular economy and bioeconomy sectors
- Bio-based innovation and standardisation
- Critical raw material recovery from biological sources
- Food systems sustainability (Farm to Fork strategy)
- Biodiversity and ecosystem services
- Agroecology and sustainable agriculture
- Climate action in food and land use sectors
- Clean environment and zero pollution

KNOWN THEMATIC PRIORITIES (Circular Economy and Bioeconomy Call, 2026)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Improving sustainable plastic food packaging
- Textile recycling technologies
- Critical raw material recovery
- Bio-based innovation and standardisation (11 topics in scope)

TWO-STAGE PROCESS
-----------------
Stage 1: Outline application (max 10 pages), evaluated on Excellence and Impact criteria only.
Stage 1 deadline: 16 April 2026
Successful applicants invited to submit full proposal.

Stage 2: Full proposal evaluated on Excellence, Impact, and Implementation.
Stage 2 deadline: 23 September 2026

NOTE: First-stage proposals may be subject to blind evaluation pilot (proposals evaluated without knowing applicant identity).

ELIGIBLE PROJECT TYPES
-----------------------
Research and Innovation Actions (RIA), Innovation Actions (IA), Coordination and Support Actions (CSA), Co-funded Partnerships - per specific topic requirements.

ELIGIBLE COUNTRIES
------------------
EU Member States and Horizon Europe associated countries. Projects eligible to apply from organisations in Portugal.
