HORIZON-EIC-2025-TRANSITIONOPEN - EIC Transition Open 2025
================================================================

CALL IDENTIFIER: HORIZON-EIC-2025-TRANSITIONOPEN
PROGRAMME: Horizon Europe - European Innovation Council (EIC)
TYPE: EIC Transition Grant
STATUS: Closed (Deadline: 17 September 2025)

FINANCIAL DETAILS
-----------------
Total Budget: EUR 98,000,000 (2025)
Expected Grants: ~40
Minimum Contribution: EUR 500,000 per project
Maximum Contribution: EUR 2,500,000 per project
Grant Rate: typically up to 100% of eligible costs

OBJECTIVE
---------
The EIC Transition Open supports innovation activities beyond experimental proof of concept, focusing on technology maturation and validation from lab to real application environments. It bridges the gap between early-stage research (TRL 3) and market-ready solutions (TRL 6+).

Projects must address two complementary dimensions:
1. Technology maturation and validation (TRL 4 to 6)
2. Sustainable business case exploration toward commercialization

ELIGIBILITY - APPLICANTS
------------------------
- Mono-beneficiary applications accepted
- Consortia of 2 entities accepted
- Consortia of 3 to 5 entities accepted
- Maximum 22 A4 pages for evaluation sections
- Must build on prior eligible project results (minimum TRL 3 at proposal stage)

ELIGIBLE ACTIVITIES
-------------------
- Technology maturation and validation in relevant environments (TRL 4-6)
- Development of sustainable business models and commercialization pathways
- User-centric methodology for market and customer discovery
- IP protection and freedom-to-operate analysis
- Regulatory pathway assessment
- Prototype development and testing in real-world conditions

EVALUATION CRITERIA
-------------------
- Excellence: Quality of the technology and innovation potential
- Impact: Market opportunity and pathway to commercialization
- Implementation: Quality of the team and project plan

Evaluation process (2025 edition):
- Step 1: Remote evaluation (completed)
- Step 2: Jury panel interviews (December 1-5, 2025)
- Final results: January 2026
- 611 proposals submitted in this edition

SUBMISSION PROCEDURE
--------------------
Single-stage submission via the EU Funding and Tenders Portal.
Maximum proposal length: 22 A4 pages for evaluation sections.

TECHNOLOGY READINESS LEVELS
----------------------------
- Entry level: TRL 3 (proof of concept from prior project)
- Target at end of grant: TRL 6 (technology demonstrated in relevant environment)

PRIOR PROJECT REQUIREMENT
--------------------------
Applicants must demonstrate that their proposal builds on results from a prior eligible project. Eligible prior projects include EIC Pathfinder grants, ERC grants, or other Horizon Europe and Horizon 2020 grants. The prior project must have demonstrated at least experimental proof of concept (TRL 3).
