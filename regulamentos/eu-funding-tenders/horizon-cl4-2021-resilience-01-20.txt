Horizon Europe - Antimicrobial, Antiviral, and Antifungal Nanocoatings (RIA)

Title: Antimicrobial, Antiviral, and Antifungal Nanocoatings (RIA)
Call Identifier: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01-20
Parent Call: HORIZON-CL4-2021-RESILIENCE-01

Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
Action Type: HORIZON-RIA (Research and Innovation Actions) - Budget-Based Action Grant

Budget:
- Total Budget: EUR 23,000,000
- Expected Grants: 4 grants
- Contribution Range: EUR 4,000,000 to EUR 6,000,000 per project

Deadline:
- Submission Deadline: September 23, 2021
- Status: CLOSED

Description:
This call seeks research on inorganic nanomaterials with enhanced antimicrobial and antiviral activity for high-traffic surfaces and textiles.

Focus areas:
- Sustainable nanocoating synthesis methods
- Application methods for surfaces and textiles
- Surface durability against wear and degradation
- Toxicity assessment of nano-materials
- COVID-19 pandemic response applications

Projects must address the pandemic response context and include business case strategies for commercial exploitation.

TRL: Expected to start at TRL 3 and achieve TRL 6 by project completion.

Keywords: Coating and films, Materials engineering, Surface modification, Surface physics, Surface science and nanostructures, Thin films

Eligibility:
General conditions outlined in Annex A and E of the Horizon Europe Work Programme General Annexes. Eligible countries described in Annex B.

How to Apply:
Submission via Funding and Tenders Portal (F&T Portal).
