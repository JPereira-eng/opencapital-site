Accelerating the discovery and development of chemicals and innovative advanced materials through digitalisation and artificial intelligence (RIA/IA)

Description:
This initiative targets accelerated pathways to market for novel substances with superior functionalities using digital tools and AI methods. Projects develop interoperable workflows with shared standards, incorporate demonstrators validating materials under realistic conditions, and establish digital feedback loops from production through product integration.

Key Scope Elements:
- Development of interoperable digital workflows and shared data standards for materials discovery
- AI-driven methodologies for accelerating chemical and materials R&D
- Operationalizing Safe and Sustainable by Design (SSbD) frameworks
- Advanced risk assessment methodologies for novel substances
- Collaboration with Materials Commons for Europe initiative
- Demonstrators validating materials under realistic industrial conditions
- Digital feedback loops from production through product integration
- FAIR data principles adoption
