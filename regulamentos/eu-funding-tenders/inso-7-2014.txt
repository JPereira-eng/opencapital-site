Objectives
This action addresses strengthening public-public partnerships (P2P) by supporting three key areas:

1. Information Platform Development: Create a user-led platform supporting P2P stakeholders with monitoring, evaluation, learning materials, and web-based portal maintenance. The platform should serve as a central hub for information exchange among European joint programming initiatives.

2. P2P Optimisation: Develop framework conditions for Joint Programming Initiatives (JPIs) and support knowledge hub concepts for less research-intensive countries. This includes developing guidelines and tools to enhance the efficiency of joint calls and co-funded actions between member states.

3. National Activity Alignment: Assess current approaches enabling Member States to increase competition and excellence implementing jointly-agreed research agendas. Support alignment of national research priorities with European-level joint programming objectives.

Description
The action supports the development and operation of public-public partnerships under Horizon 2020. Joint programming is a process by which member states voluntarily agree to address common challenges through coordinated or common national research programmes.

The Commission notes that proposals requesting EUR 1-3 million would address this challenge appropriately, though other amounts are acceptable. Expected outcomes include increased public administration efficiency and deeper understanding of P2P functioning across European, national, and regional levels.

Open access must be granted to all scientific publications resulting from Horizon 2020 actions.
