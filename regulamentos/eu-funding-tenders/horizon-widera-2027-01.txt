# EIC Pre-accelerator - Widening Topic Details

## Core Information
- Title: EIC Pre-accelerator - Widening
- Call Identifier: HORIZON-WIDERA-2027-01
- Topic ID: HORIZON-WIDERA-2027-01-WIDENING-01
- Framework Programme: Horizon Europe (HORIZON)
- Programme Division: HORIZON.4.1 (Widening participation and spreading excellence)

## Budget Overview
- Total Budget (2027): EUR 40,000,000
- Expected Grants: 60
- Grant Range: EUR 500,000 to EUR 1,000,000 per beneficiary
- Funding Rate: 70% of eligible costs

## Key Dates
- Planned Opening Date: April 2, 2027
- Submission Deadline: June 1, 2027
- Deadline Model: Single-stage

## Eligibility
- Applicant Type: Single legal entity (mono-beneficiary)
- Required Status: Small and Medium Enterprise (SME)
- Location: Must be established in a Widening country (Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czechia, Estonia, Greece, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, and equivalent Associated Countries)

## Project Duration & Support
- Project Duration: Up to 2 years
- Grant Type: Lump sum (EUR 300,000-EUR 500,000 maximum)

## Evaluation Criteria & Thresholds
Three independent evaluators assess proposals using:
- Excellence (Threshold: 4/5): Technological novelty, commercial potential, credible objectives, methodology appropriateness
- Impact (Threshold: 4/5): Commercial credibility, economic/societal benefits, investment readiness, market strategy
- Quality & Efficiency (Threshold: 3/5): Team capabilities, KPIs/milestones, resource allocation

## Primary Focus
This action targets early-stage deep-tech startups with high-impact innovation technology capable of creating new markets. Applicants must demonstrate technology validated at minimum TRL 4, with clear IP protection and commercialization pathways. Projects should advance innovations from TRL 4 to TRL 5/6 while improving investment readiness.

## Support Services
Successful applicants receive complimentary EIC Business Acceleration Services including coaching and tailored events, plus potential access to EIC Accelerator Fast Track following mid-project review.

## Despesas elegíveis
- Custos de pessoal afetos ao projeto
- Subcontratacao limitada para desenvolvimento tecnologico
- Equipamento e amortizacoes
- Custos de viagem e participacao em eventos EIC
- Custos indiretos a taxa flat de 25%

## Criterios de Seleção
Avaliacao por tres avaliadores independentes em tres dimensoes (Excellence, Impact, Quality and Efficiency) com thresholds de 4/5, 4/5 e 3/5 respetivamente. Aplicacao mono-beneficiaria submetida atraves do EU Funding & Tenders Portal.
