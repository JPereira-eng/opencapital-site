Name: Startup Lisboa
Type: Business Incubator (Municipal)
Founded: 2012 by Câmara Municipal de Lisboa (CML)
Website: https://www.startuplisboa.com
Model: Equity-free (0% equity taken)
Sectors: Technology, Commerce, Services, Tourism

Description:
Startup Lisboa is a business incubator established to foster entrepreneurship and job creation through infrastructure and specialized support services. Founded by the City of Lisbon, it supports early-stage and established companies across technology, commerce and tourism sectors.

Programs:
1. Incubation Program - Mentoring, specialized consultancy, equipped office space (10-40m2), internet, facility support
2. Pre-incubation Program - Business plan development, tutorials, training, networking events
3. Rocket Program - 10-week acceleration covering critical business building topics for early-stage startups

Locations:
- Startup Lisboa Tech (technology companies)
- Startup Lisboa Commerce (commerce, services, tourism)
- Casa da Startup Lisboa (temporary housing for visiting entrepreneurs)
- Main address: Rua do Comércio, Lisboa city center

Funding Available:
- Startup Lisboa Loans Fund: up to EUR 45,000
  - New/early-stage companies: 85% of investment covered
  - Established companies (3+ years): 100% of investment covered

Eligibility: Startups and entrepreneurs in technology, commerce, services and tourism sectors. Both national and international entrepreneurs accepted.

How to Apply: Via candidaturas on startuplisboa.com (periodic open calls)
Next deadline: Not publicly confirmed (applications open periodically)

Partners: CML (Câmara Municipal de Lisboa), Invest Lisboa, Montepio, IAPMEI

Support Services:
- Mentoring and business development guidance
- Expert meetings for early-stage companies
- Thematic events addressing startup challenges
- Access to investor network through demo days
