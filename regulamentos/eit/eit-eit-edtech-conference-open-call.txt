# EIT EdTech Conference Open Call - Financing Information

## Basic Details
- **Name:** EIT EdTech Conference Open Call
- **Call Type:** Open Call for Conference Organization
- **Program:** EIT Urban Mobility (part of EIT - European Institute of Innovation and Technology)
- **Funding Body:** EIT (EU body)

## Timeline
- **Opening Date:** 22 April 2026, 09:00 CET
- **Deadline:** 23 June 2026, 17:00 CET
- **Info Webinar:** 7 May 2026, 15:00-16:00 CEST

## Eligibility
Applicants must be:
- Legal entities established in EU Member States or Horizon Europe associated countries
- Single organizations or consortia with complementary expertise

## Required Qualifications
Applicants must demonstrate:
- "Proven experience in organising high-level European or international conferences"
- Strong positioning in EdTech, education or innovation sectors
- Capacity for end-to-end event delivery management
- Ability to mobilize diverse, multi-stakeholder European audiences

## Project Scope
- End-to-end delivery of two consecutive conference editions (2027 and 2028)
- High-level, policy-relevant, outcome-oriented events in Brussels
- Ecosystem-building initiative connecting EdTech industry with EIT Community

## Budget & Co-financing
- **Specific amount:** Not disclosed in the document provided
- **Co-financing rate:** Not specified

## Eligible Expenses
Document does not detail specific eligible expense categories. Expected to cover:
- Conference venue and logistics
- Speaker fees and travel
- Marketing and communication
- Event production and audiovisual
- Project management and administration

## Selection Criteria
Evaluation focuses on:
- Track record of organising high-impact European conferences
- Strategic vision aligned with EIT EdTech ecosystem objectives
- Capacity to deliver two consecutive editions with consistent quality
- Network within EU policymaker and educator communities
- Financial and operational solvency

Submit applications via: https://www.eiturbanmobility.eu/call-for-proposals/eit-edtech-conference-open-call/
