TITLE: Alliances for Sectoral Cooperation on Skills - Blueprint (Erasmus+ KA2 2025)
FRAMEWORK: Erasmus+ (ERASMUS2027)
SOURCE: erasmus-ka2-alliances

DESCRIPTION

This initiative strengthens Europe's innovation capacity through cooperation among higher education, vocational training, and socio-economic stakeholders. The program aims to develop new curricula and boost entrepreneurial mindsets while addressing skills mismatches in the labour market.

The Blueprint track specifically targets the 14 industrial ecosystems identified in Europe's New Industrial Strategy. It creates strategic partnerships to develop sector skills strategies, occupational profiles, and training programs under the Pact for Skills framework, focusing on both immediate and long-term workforce development needs.
