TITLE: Alliances for Education and Enterprises (Erasmus+ KA2 2025)
FRAMEWORK: Erasmus+ (ERASMUS2027)
SOURCE: erasmus-ka2-alliances

DESCRIPTION

This initiative aims to strengthen Europe's innovation capacity through cooperation among higher education, vocational training, and socio-economic sectors. The program develops new curricula and fosters entrepreneurial mindsets while addressing skills gaps related to digital transformation and green economy transitions.

The action targets talent and skills development with emphasis on digital competencies across all job profiles, green skills for sustainable development, and adoption of innovative deep tech solutions. Partnerships must include both VET and higher education organisations, bringing together enterprises and educational providers to address societal challenges including climate change, demographic shifts, digitisation, and rapid employment changes.
