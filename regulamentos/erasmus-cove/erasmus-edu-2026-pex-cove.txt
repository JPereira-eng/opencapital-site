DESCRIPTION AND OBJECTIVES

The initiative establishes transnational collaborative networks bringing together VET stakeholders - institutions, companies, social partners, authorities, higher education bodies, and research centers - to create comprehensive skills ecosystems. The program aims to ensure high quality skills through flexible and learner-centred VET provisions that lead to quality employment while supporting regional development, innovation, and social inclusion.

CoVEs operate at national and transnational levels, focusing on specific economic sectors or innovative approaches addressing challenges like climate change, digitalisation, and artificial intelligence.

SCOPE

Projects should develop innovative VET practices including digital technologies and AI applications, enhance VET attractiveness, facilitate mobility and apprenticeships, support teacher professional development, and create joint provisions including micro-credentials.
