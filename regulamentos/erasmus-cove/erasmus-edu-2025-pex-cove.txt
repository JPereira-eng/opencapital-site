DESCRIPTION OVERVIEW

This initiative supports international collaborative networks of Centres of Vocational Excellence (CoVEs) to advance vocational education and training (VET) across Europe. CoVEs operate as regional skills ecosystems for innovation, regional development and social inclusion while connecting internationally.

CORE OBJECTIVES

- Ensure high-quality, flexible VET provisions meeting labour market needs
- Support local/regional development, innovation, and social inclusion during green and digital transitions
- Contribute to upward convergence of VET excellence across countries
- Ensure sustained impact beyond project completion

TWO-LEVEL OPERATION

1. National: Involving diverse local stakeholders to create skills ecosystems
2. International: Networks sharing interests in specific sectors, innovative challenge-addressing approaches, or outreach improvements

THREE ACTIVITY CLUSTERS

1. Teaching and learning
2. Cooperation and partnerships
3. Governance and funding
